Grande Cosmetics Has Released the GrandeHAIR Full Boost Collection

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, May 22, 2024

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

If there’s one lash growth serum that’s effectively taken the masses by storm, it’s easily the award-winning formula from Grande Cosmetics. The TikTok-viral serum instantly made waves for its ability to produce thicker, fuller lashes in as quick as four weeks. And now, Grande Cosmetics has added more growth products to its line-up with the release of the GrandeHAIR Full Boost Collection.

The new range features three hero products—a shampoo, a conditioner and a 2-in-1 hair foam and scalp treatment—designed with advanced scalp and hair science that help rectify fine, thinning and damaged hair. Per the brand, the collection combines clinical efficacy with “luxurious cleansing, conditioning and styling formulations” to deliver visible results both instantly and in the long term.

The GrandeHAIR Full Boost Shampoo immediately gets to work on energizing the scalp and giving flat hair a noticeable lift. It contains niacinamide, rice protein and microgreens to minimize fall out and produce thicker, fuller locks with continued use. 

Through a consumer perception study, 91 percent of subjects reported fuller looking hair while 94 percent said their scalp felt healthier. 

Pair the Full Boost Shampoo with the matching Full Boost Conditioner for even better results. The hydrating formula reduces breakage and detangling time without weighing down the hair. Just like the shampoo, it’s suitable for color-treated and keratin-treated hair and leaves behind subtle scents of violet, iris and sandalwood.

If you’ve already found a shampoo and conditioner you love, the GrandeHAIR Full Boost Volumizing Foam is capable of bringing transformations all on its own, especially if you apply it before blow drying. It adds body, bounce and a fullness that lasts all day without leaving your strands sticky or crunchy. It also doubles as a scalp treatment by providing nutrients that build stronger, healthier looking hair with each use.

The formula contains a mix of Grande’s Hair Complex featuring vitamin B1, rice protein, microgreens, lotus seed and nori seaweed, alongside a keratin alternative complex with biotin, peptides, amino acids and botanical extracts.

In its own consumer study, it was reported that 97 percent of testers witnessed instant body and fuller-looking hair from use while 94 percent agreed it provided a weightless bounce.

All three of the GrandeHAIR Full Boost Collection products are available at Ulta starting at $26 each.

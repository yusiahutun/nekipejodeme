1. Joaquin "El Chapo" Guzman Loera

El Chapo is one of the most dangerous inmates and most famous Supermax prisoners. He is renowned for his status as the most powerful and dangerous drug kingpin in the world. El Chapo is the former leader of the Mexican Sinaloa Cartel. In 2019, he was sentenced to life in prison for being a notorious Mexican crime lord.

El Chapo was found guilty of several crimes, including drug trafficking, conspiracy to commit murder, and money laundering. He committed the crimes in Mexico, and you must be wondering why he is being held at ADX Florence.

Well, he proved to be a master at escaping from prison. He broke out of two Mexican prisons and went back to lead the Sinaloa Cartel. After a few hours, he was whisked away to the ADX Florence facility.

2. Zacarias Moussaoui

Zacarias is one of the famous prisoners in Colorado. The former Qaeda operative was born in Saint-Jean-de-Luz, France. Zacarias is renowned for his role as a conspirator in the 9/11 terrorist attacks. He is the only person convicted for the attacks.

3. Dzhokhar A. Tsarnaev

7. Michael Swango

Michael is one of the most famous Supermax inmates. He was born on October 21, 1954, in Tacoma, Washington, United States. The former physician came to the spotlight when he was accused of fatally poisoning his patients and colleagues. It is alleged that he fatally poisoned more than 60 people to death.

The exhumed remains of some of his patients contained poisonous substances. Michael confessed that he had only caused four deaths. He was found guilty of committing the crime and was awarded a life sentence by the jurists. Michael is serving his sentence at ADX Florence federal prison in Colorado.

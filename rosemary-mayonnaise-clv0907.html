Rosemary Mayonnaise

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 9, 2024
Getty Images/StockDisc

Fresh rosemary and grated garlic elevate a common condiment to new culinary heights. Use this mayonnaise when making our Smokey "BLT" or any variety of sandwich.

Read More

Yields: 1 c.

Total Time: 10 mins

Cal/Serv: 100

Ingredients

Save Recipe
  • 3 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tsp. finely chopped rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp. grated garlic
  • 3/4 c. mayonnaise
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice

Directions

  • Step 1Combine the oil, rosemary, and garlic in a small pan over low heat, stirring occasionally and cook until garlic begins to sizzle--about 5 minutes. Remove from heat to cool completely.
  • Step 2Stir the mayonnaise, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Whisk the lemon juice and the olive oil mixture into the mayonnaise until smooth. Stores refrigerated up to 5 days.

    Serving size = 1 tablespoon

Tips & Techniques

TIMESAVER: Simply stir in a tablespoon of rosemary-infused oil per 3/4 cup mayonnaise and skip the remaining ingredients. Try Salute Sante! Rosemary Grapeseed oil. To order, visit salutesante.com.

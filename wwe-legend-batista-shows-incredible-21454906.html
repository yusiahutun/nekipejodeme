WWE legend Batista shows off incredible body transformation over the years

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, February 2, 2024

WWE legend Dave Bautista may no longer be strutting his stuff in the ring, but his body suggests he could do exactly that!

The now 51-year-old has retained his incredible physique despite leaving the wrestling world behind to try his hand at other things.

Since quitting the WWE for a second time he has appeared in several films, including Spectre where he starred alongside Daniel Craig.

The pair engaging in several intense scenes with the former wrestler out to get 007.

It is a good thing Craig never engaged into a brawl with Batista in real life because you wouldn't put your life savings on him getting out in one piece.

Batista shared several images on his Instagram which showed how he has kept in shape over the years - starting in 1987!

That physique earned him the nickname 'The Animal' during his WWE career with power one of his main weapons.

Batista was a six-time world champion having held the WWE Championship belt as well as being the World Heavyweight champion.

He still holds the record for being the longest World Heavyweight champion with a rein of almost 300 days.

The powerhouse also won the Royal Rumble in 2005 which allowed him to go on and defeat former mentor Triple H at Wrestlemania 21.

Batista left the WWE in 2010 before re-signing in 2013 and made his final appearance in the ring last year at Wrestlemania 35.

His success will see him achieve Hall of Fame status when he is inducted this April with a ceremony due to take plat at Wrestlemania 36 in Florida.

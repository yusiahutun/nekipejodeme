Taylor Swift fans predict Ed Sheeran is her next collab after adorable cat pic

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 24, 2024

The wait for Reputation Taylor’s Version may soon come to an end, and fans think Taylor Swift’s longtime friend Ed Sheeran is back in the studio for a collab.

Taylor Swift and Ed Sheeran are friendship goals for life. The music stars became friends a decade back and despite rumors of falling out, they are still going strong. The Thinking Out Loud singer explained being friends with Tay Tay was like ‘therapy.’ The duo, who previously collaborated on songs like Everything Has Changed and End Game, may pair up again as she gets ready for Reputation – Taylor’s version.

Fans think Ed Sheeran posing with Taylor Swift’s cat is a ‘sign’ of new collab

After working with Kendrick Lamar in the Taylor Version of Bad Blood, fans are speculating Ed Sheeran is next for Reputation. It wouldn’t be the first time, as they previously collaborated on the album’s track, End Game.

The British singer’s recent Instagram photo dump can be a clue to that as the Shape Of You crooner posed with (what looks like) Taylor’s cat Meredith.

Swifties flooded X (formerly Twitter) with possible theories. One posted: “We are so ready for that music!” Another fan quipped: “Everyone is tripping about the End Game but I’m tripping about this being proof, Meredith is alive!”

Clues regarding Reputation Taylor’s version

In Swiftie land, nothing is ever a coincidence, as the Blank Space crooner herself has admitted she enjoys leaving Easter eggs and clues for fans.

The 34-year-old celebrity was recently named Time’s Person Of The Year, where she talked about next re-recording and Reputation Vault tracks. She teased it will be “a goth-punk moment of female rage.”

But now fans noticed her caption includes some letters capitalized randomly in a sentence, a trick she has used before to hint at songs.

Looking at the placement of the capitalized letters, a fan theorized: “6 capitalized Ss… the letter that looks like a snake. The Sound a Snake Makes… her 6th album. Reputation is coming.”

Another predicted a vault track name of Reputation from the letters, which could be ‘Trust Issues.’

Fans also have ideas about the release date timeline of Reputation’s Taylor’s version.

One explained: “My official reputation TV theory- 1. Taylor unpinned the Speak Now TV announcement exactly 13 days before she announced 1989 TV 2. She just unpinned the 1989 TV announcement. 13 days from today is 12/19. 1+2+1+9 = you guessed it. 13. 3. 13 days from 12/19? New. Year’s. Day.”

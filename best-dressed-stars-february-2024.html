Best dressed stars in February 2024: Jennifer Lawrence, Emma Watson, Tracee Ellis Ross, more

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 24, 2024

From Demi Moore to India Amartefio, see which celebrities struck a sartorial chord this month

    February has arrived, meaning Cupid has already started sprinkling his love potion over the style set.

    With Valentine's Day over, the romantic holiday isn't the only thing that has us lovestruck this month. The revival of the 'Mob Wife' aesthetic has finally given leopard print the renaissance it deserves. The upcoming Grammy Awards poses the perfect opportunity for another glittering harlequin moment from Harry Styles. While New York, Milan, Paris and London Fashion Week will no doubt bring a fleet of perfectly dressed stars through February's doors. 

    From faux fur to Fendi, menswear to monochrome mavens, here you'll find February's best-dressed celebrities.

    Best-dressed stars in February 2024

    Jennifer Lawrence

    Jennifer Lawrence was a monochrome maven in slick tailoring as she stepped out for the Christian Dior Womenswear Fall/Winter 2024-2025 show as part of Paris Fashion Week.

    Emma Watson

    Emma Watson schooled us all in effortless dressing when she opted for preppy layers at Milan Fashion Week. 

    Tracee Ellis Ross

    Tracee Ellis Ross is the latest celebrity to adopt the sheer style trend as she stepped out during the Milan Fashion Week rocking a transluscent skirt and edgy layers. 

    Nicola Coughlan

    sweetheart Nicola Coughlan graced the front row at the Erdem show at London Fashion Week wearing an avante garde cranberry-hued dress and dramatic puffed opera gloves. 

    Lila Moss

    Kate Moss' daughter, Lila, embodied relaxed glamour at the Burberry LFW show, dressing in a forest green top and a grungy tartan maxi skirt. 

    Leomie Anderson

    Victoria's Secret model Leomie Anderson looked breathtaking at the Burberry Winter 2024 show during London Fashion Week wearing a lilac fringed dress. 

    Iris Law

    Iris Law looked sublime in suede as she led the star arrivals at the Burberry Winter 2024 show during London Fashion Week.

    Maya Jama

    Miss Maya Jama was a monochromatic maven on the Front Row of the Chet Lo AW24 show during London Fashion Week, sporting one of Lo's signature popcorn looks for a healthy dose of 90s nostalgia. 

    The Love Island presenter rocked CHARLES & KEITH's x Chet Lo 'Spike' textured long sleeve top and textured midi skirt in black. 

    Kaya Scodelario

    actress Kaya Scodelario was tulle perfection in a frothy lime green Molly Goddard dress and platform loafers to attend the Molly Goddard show during London Fashion Week.

    Rosamund Pike

    Rosamund Pike took style notes from the Princess of Wales at the EE BAFTA Film Awards 2024 Nominees' Party, adding dramatic leather opera gloves to a pastel pink tulle dress.

    The star also graced the Front Row of Molly Goddard's show at London Fashion Week, exuding preppy elegance in a puff ball dress and 'Très Vivier Rhinestone Buckle Babies Pumps' by Roger Vivier.

    Katie Holmes

    New York Fashion Week was the perfect excuse for Katie Holmes to dress to the nines in her vampiest FROW outfit. Channelling her inner goth in an all-black ensemble, the actress rocked a see-through lace dress with a floor-length skirt and a ruffled hem that showed off her toned physique. She layered it over a matching lace bra and high-waisted underwear and finished off the look with an oversized jacket, a white clutch and open-toe heels.

    Julianne Hough

    Black was the colour of the moment at New York Fashion Week, as Julianne Hough proved with her daring thigh-split gown. The 35-year-old displayed her dancer's physique in a floor-sweeping, sequinned dress with a plunging neckline and structured shoulders, creating an edgy look with her tinted sunglasses and slick wet-look blonde bob.  

    Anya Taylor-Joy

    Anya Taylor-Joy gave TikTok's coquette trend a luxe makoever in a sublime sheer jumpsuit adorned with satin bows on the sleeves, to join Dior and Peter Philips celebrate Rouge Dior at La Dolce Vita in Beverly Hills, California.

    Rachel Zegler

    s Rachel Zegler embodied flapper girl glamour in a fringed co-ord at the Dior celebration of Rouge Dior at La Dolce Vita.

    Alexandra Shipp

    actress Alexandra Shipp stayed true to the Barbiecore aesthetic in a luxurious rippling gown to party with Dior in Beverly Hills, California.

    Sabrina Elba

    Sabrina Elba embodied preppy chic at REFY's 'You Are The Art' exhibition in London. Idris Elba's wife rocked a collared mini dress ankle socks and platform heels for the event, before later slipping into Harris Reed's AW24 Show as part of LFW.

    Jess Hunt

    REFY founder Jess Hunt joined co-founder Jenna Meek at the brand's London pop-up 'You Are The Art' exhibition, donning a sheer dress layered over black underwear, finished with an oversized blazer. She sported slick ballerina hair, defined brows and glossy lips to introduce the brand's latest Curated and Lash Sculpt collections.

    Dakota Johnson

    Forget winter hues, Dakota Johnson made a beelin​e for a vermillion jumpsuit to step out in SoHo, New York, layering with a long leather coat. 

    Florence Pugh

    Florence Pugh opted for minimalism at the screening of Dune: Part Two in Mexico, exuding angelic sophistication in a ruched white maxi dress with a sleeveless bodice and elegant mock neckline. 

    Her platinum pixie cut was styled into 'wet look' wisps to add drama to her ensemble.

    Zendaya

    Zendaya was an ethereal goddess in a draped, navel-baring dress at the photocall for at Mexico City Four Seasons Hotel.

    Troye Sivan

    Australian music artist Troye Sivan served up a beret-good outfit for the W Magazine, Mark Ronson, and Gucci's Grammy After-Party at Bar Marmont on 4 February.

    Simone Ashley

    muse Simone Ashley's vampy deconstructed lingerie co-ord highlighted her elegant silhouette as she hosted a Nespresso x Liberty intimate dinner to celebrate the new collaboration.

    India Amarteifio

    's leading lady India Amarteifio was a darling in the 'Louisa' dress from Reiss, complete with all-over metallic detailing and a luxe silky finish. 

    The young star joined a glittering guestlist for an exclusive lunch with jewellery brand, Mejuri.

    Meg Bellamy

    Meg Bellamy, AKA 's Kate Middleton toyed with androgyny in an oversized, sandy suit and open-toe sandals. Departing from her Princess of Wales-esque aesthetic, Meg added a soft smokey eye and vampy curls to complete her soft glam aesthetic. 

    The actress, 21, was also in attendance at the exclusive Mejuri lunch hosted in London. 

    Demi Moore

    Actress Demi Moore was suited and booted when she stepped out in the earlier hours after her appearance on T

    Rocking a fitted, silhouette-skimming blazer and sheer tights, the raven-haired beauty epitomised sophistication in her monochrome ensemble.

    Demi Lovato

    Redefining the meaning of 'Queen of Hearts', Demi Lovato looked resplendent in red as she donned a billowing ball gown for The American Heart Association's Red Dress Collection Concert 2024 at Jazz at Lincoln Center in New York City.

    Poppy Delevigne

    Boho babe Poppy Delevigne was a vision of colour in a woven coat from Zazi Vintage Boutique for her Della Vite Valentine's lunch at The Ivy Chelsea Garden, attended by Princess Beatrice, Princess Maria-Olympia of Greece, and more noteworthy socialites. 

    Her colourful coat, named the 'ZEBAY' is described by the brand as" imbued with a 70s feel and handcrafted from selected vintage suzani embroidery interwoven with upcycled sheepskin."

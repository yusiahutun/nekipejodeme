5 Alternative Cheap Online Stores to Shein

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, February 13, 2024

Shein is a great site to get good clothes at a low price. Despite being a great site, Shein can only stock so much. If you’re looking for a wider range of clothing options, or just feel like you’ve exhausted everything that Shein already has to offer, then it might be time to look for something new.

Luckily, there are a ton of sites out there that are just like Shein, with cheap clothes, inclusive deals, and a wide range of different options. Here are some of the very best.

1. boohoo

First on this list comes boohoo. Created in 2006, Boohoo is an online clothing store that aims to bring you affordable clothing that still looks great.

boohoo has a huge range of different options for you to choose from. Tops, dresses, shorts, pants, jeans, skirts, and many, many more are all available.

Finding the right item of clothing is easy thanks to the various filters such as occasion, which lets you search for the perfect outfit based on what you need it for.

There are options for plus size, petite, tall, and maternity clothes here as well, so no matter who you are and how your body looks you’ll be able to find the perfect outfit for you. The site includes a men’s section as well, with similar options all available there for you to search through.

What’s great as well about boohoo is how often it runs various sales, promotions, and more. If you’re ever looking for a great deal that just its cheap prices can’t provide, then there will probably be some sale coming up such as 50% off, or cheap shipping to make sure you get a bargain.

2. ASOS

Next up on this list is ASOS, a UK-based online clothing store with a huge range of different options for you to choose from. Much like Shein, the store ships from all over the world, so don't worry if you're not UK-based, yourself.

The range at ASOS is a truly impressive one, and the prices here are tantalizing as well. What’s cool here is that ASOS is constantly changing and updating as new trends are entering the market, which means that there’s always a reason to come back and check out ASOS for something new.

No matter what you’re looking for, ASOS will have it. There are men and women sections, and a huge variety in what you can look for. There are tall, plus size, petite, and maternity options here as well, so you’ll always have options when you shop.

ASOS is full of deals, from a student discount you can apply store-wide, to the various sales that ASOS runs frequently such as its Black Friday sales.

If you’re looking for something a little different, there’s also ASOS Marketplace, which allows you to shop from a huge number of small businesses and boutiques. There are over 900 different stores included here, from independent brands to vintage boutiques. If you’re after something truly unique, this is a great place to find it.

3. Missguided

If you’re looking for a store that caters exclusively to women, while still having cheap prices and a great range of clothing, then Missguided is an excellent place to have a look.

The range here is huge, as you might expect, and you can find just about anything that you might be looking for. The range is constantly expanding and evolving as well, with up to 1,000 new styles dropping every week.

You can search through Missguided by these new outfits, or by season, trending looks, or a whole host of other options. If you want to search by body fit, then that’s an option, too. Missguided provides plus size, tall, petite, and maternity options to any who may be looking for them.

There are a ton of different sale options happening all the time with Missguided, but what’s cool about this site are all the year-round options. For example, you can purchase a VIP delivery pass that gives you free standard delivery year-round. There are also options for student discounts and more.

For any of you who are socially conscious, Missguided is a great option. Missguided makes attempts to be as transparent as possible and to source all its clothing as ethically as possible, while avoiding any acts that constitute modern slavery.

4. Rosegal

Next up on this list is Rosegal. First launching in 2013, Rosegal is an online clothing store with a huge range of cheap options that are entirely plus size.

If you’re looking for plus size clothing, there’s no better place than somewhere that caters specifically to your body fit.

The range here is impressive in what it provides, with all sorts of different options available. If you’re unsure on an outfit, for example, you can purchase plus size outfit bundles, which match various pieces such as tees and leggings to give you outfits that are ready to go and cheaper for it.

Tops, bottoms, dresses, swimwear, and more are all available here, and more often than not you’ll be able to find a great deal in one way or another. There are tons of bargains here such as sales, coupons, points, and more for you to shop through.

5. Romwe

Finally, Romwe makes it onto this list. If you’re looking for an online clothing store that’s about as similar to Shein as you can get, then Romwe is a great option for just that.

Romwe is owned by the same parent company as Shein is, meaning that the two clothing stores are about as similar as you could possibly hope for.

The main difference between the two is that Romwe has some options even cheaper than Shein does, with slightly more upmarket options going to Shein. As you might guess, this means that Romwe, like Shein, is legit and safe to shop at.

As a result, you’ll never struggle to find a great deal with Romwe. There are options for both girls and guys, and a huge range of different clothing options.

If you’re looking for bargains, there are plenty here, with offers on shipping, discounts, and even free returns all being readily available.

What’s cool about Romwe is that you can browse the store by aesthetic, which is a really cool way to look for new items that appeal to you.

You Don’t Need to Break the Bank to Buy a New Outfit

As you can see, there’s a huge range of different options out there that are similar to Shein. No matter what part of the store is appealing to you, you’ll be able to find exactly what you’re looking for if you just keep it up.

What’s great is that you’re not locked in to any one store, so you can visit all of these great options and still scour Shein for deals at the same time.

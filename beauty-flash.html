Beauty Flash | South China Morning Post

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Mask magic

Regular use of facial masks can make a difference to your skin, and the Tourmaline Charged Radiance Masque by Aveda is one of the latest products claiming to do just that. The Tourmaline Charged skincare series was initially produced for professional use only, but Aveda has recently launched a home-use edition. Tourmaline is one of nature's most energising minerals and is said to boost the skin's energy. The mask costs $280.

Film watch

To coincide with the exclusive screening of Japanese film 19 at the Hong Kong Arts Centre's Agnes B. Cinema, the celebrated French designer is launching a '19' wristwatch in July. The watch has black leather straps and a blue face with 'HAPPY?' printed on it. 'HAPPY?' was a logo on one of the T-shirts from the film. The price has yet to be announced.

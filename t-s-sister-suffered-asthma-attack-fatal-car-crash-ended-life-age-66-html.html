T.I.'s sister suffered an asthma attack before car crash which lead to her death at age 66

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 11, 2024

A cause of death has been determined for rapper T.I,'s late sister Precious Harris. 

Harris passed after having an asthma attack while driving, then subsequently crashing her car according to reports from Atlanta PD that were obtained by TMZ on Thursday. 

Precious passed away last Friday after spending nearly a week on life-support following a serious car accident.

Cause of death: T.I.'s sister Precious Harris' fatal car crash happened after she suffered an asthma attack, lost consciousness, and veered into a nearby telephone pole, according to reports from the Atlanta Police Department which were obtained by TMS on Thursday

The reports revealed that T.I.'s grandniece Kairi Chapman was in the car at the time of the accident.

In a statement after the crash, she told police Harris had passed out, then veered into a telephone pole.

Afterwards paramedics arrived on scene to a breathing but unconscious Harris. She was taken to a hospital but died after a week in intensive care and on life support.

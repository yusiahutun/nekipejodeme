Morgan Freeman's blunt 7-word response to Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce is brutal

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, September 7, 2024

Morgan Freeman’s ‘brutal’ response to Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift’s romance has sparked mixed reactions from fans.

From Katy Perry and Kelly Clarkson to even Travis Kelce‘s brother, everyone has an opinion on the new romance heating up. They have weighed on the blooming relationship of the singer and Kansas City Chiefs tight end in some shape or form. While most celebrities poured praises for the NFL heartthrob and music legend together, others have ‘complicated’ responses. These also include the likes of Olivia Wilde wishing the singer would have dated a scientist instead. Now one of Hollywood’s favorite voices has weighed in on the Taylor Swift mania.

Morgan Freeman on Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s romance

Morgan Freeman is a certified Kansas City Chiefs fan. But the movie star is not interested in talking about anything other than the game.

The 86-year-old Academy Award-winning celebrity‘s blunt response to the ‘Traylor’ has sparked many responses from followers. Some were shocked over his candid reply, others praised his straightforward answer.

Speaking of the celebrity couple who have been making regular headlines for a month, the actor shared his thoughts briefly. When asked what he thinks about the headlines, he said on CBS Mornings: “I don’t think about them at all”.

While Morgan does not have an opinion about the Kansas City Chiefs tight end’s romance, he did open up about the latest matches.

“Yeah, are you winning? Great, OK. Now, it’s great to watch (Patrick) run. He’s got a rifle for an arm, so that’s all good,” Freeman said adding, “So that’s what I’m interested in.”

Celebrities who ‘ship’ Traylor as much as we do

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s newfound ‘serious’ romance is a hot topic everywhere in Hollywood and celebrities are openly talking about the exciting relationship between the biggest pop music icon and NFL’s (former) playboy!

Gushing over Taylor and Travis, Katy Perry was honest and upfront, “I ship.” The Teenage Dream singer joins the long list of Alex Rodriguez and Erin Andrews who are loving the new couple.

American sports commentator and TV personality Erin Andrews is even thinking one step further who quipped in a recent interview, “We hope to officiate the wedding.”

Brother of the man of the hour, Jason Kelce has also joined the conversation revealing: “I don’t really know a lot about what is happening in Travis’ love life. I try to, like, keep his business [as] his business and straight out of that world,” adding, “Having said that. … I think it is 100 percent true.”

Alex Romer Bio, Age, Height, Parents, Girlfriend, Wiki

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, April 5, 2024

Alex Romer is a 17-year-old youngster who belongs to Florida. He is the finalist on the American Ninja Warrior Season 15, where he showed his skills, and has also traveled to Los Angeles. The lower age limit of the show is 15 years old, and the competitor will eventually earn the prize of $1 million if the finalist continues to advance to the National Finals in Las Vegas. Talking about Alex, he has learned the qualifying round and the semi-final round.

Early Life

Alex Romer is 17 years old and is studying at Ridgeview High School. He was announced to be the semi-finalist on 28th August 2023 in the famous TV show contest called American Ninja Warrior. Before this, Alex had nearly seven years of training. Alex spent his freshman and sophomore years at Middleburg High School, then went to Ridgeview.

He spent a junior year and would have a senior year in the upcoming days. He got his confidence at home gym, where he practiced the games and got the confidence to participate in Ninja Warrior. Pete Romer, the dad of Alex Romer, has also remained a swimmer and young athlete before joining the Navy.

Parents 

His father and other family members [including two siblings] are involved in the family business Mighty Orion Fitness, which has further afforded him a rigorous training regimen. Alex feels like having fun with the games of Ninja Warrior. Pete, the father of Alex, is a retired personality from the US Navy.

Alex’s mother, Dawn Romer, co-owns the backyard area for American Ninja games and obstacles with her husband, Pete. The name of the gym where Alex practices his games is Mighty Orion Fitness Gym, where Alex used to do gymming but had Ninja Games in his mind since the first day.

Alex Romer Career

Alex went to Los Angeles and gave auditions for a TV show, American Ninja Warrior. After that, he got selected and committed to playing games for the show. He has given his name to American Ninja gyms in the area and in Georgia, where he used to play Ninja League and competes at the local level against other gyms.

This further helps Alex to stay active and develop the skillset for the V show. Alex often plays soccer and is also a band member in his school. Around six years ago, Alex had a competition at the home gym that he won.

After that, he started playing regional games and attended championship events in New Mexico. Now, he is playing for the World Finals and the FINA League, the Federation of Ninja Athletes. His father says Alex can float, fly, and carry his body through obstacles.

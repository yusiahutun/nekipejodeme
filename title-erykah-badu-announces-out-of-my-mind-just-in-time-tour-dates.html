Erykah Badu Announces &quot;Out Of My Mind, Just In Time&quot; Tour Dates

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, April 29, 2024

In celebration of her latest Top 10 debut release, New Amerykah Part 2 (Return of the Ankh) , Erykah Badu has announced the Out Of My Mind, Just In Time Tour. The May-August will include Badu’s contemporaries such as Maxwell, Bilal and more recent starlet and DXnext alum, Janelle Monae.

The tour is broken into legs with different guests, including a closing segment with this year’s Lilith Fair women-in-music festival.

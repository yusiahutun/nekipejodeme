Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

Kim Kardashian's ex-BFF Larsa Pippen cryptically claimed the famous family dumped her because she "knew too much."

The 47-year-old opened up about her former friendship with Kim, 41, during a recent episode of The Real Housewives of Miami.

Thursday's installment of the show saw Larsa, Kiki Barth, and Adriana de Moura go out for dinner.

Kim's estranged husband Kanye West came up in conversation and her co-stars were intrigued to know what really happened between the mom of four and Larsa.

The entrepreneur didn't say much, but later opened up to the cameras when she was alone.

Larsa said: "I was best friends with Kim, and I love her and I love Kanye, and I just was the person that was stuck in the middle.

"I took a beating because I was the friend that was basically there and saw everything. And that basically was the demise of our relationship.

"I knew too much, I was a problem, and so, whatever. That's kind of what happened."

It comes just weeks after Larsa shaded her ex BFF and admitted how much better she's doing without her.

Back in July 2020, Larsa and the Kardashian crew all unfollowed each other on Instagram.

She opened up about the “falling out” they all had while a guest on Hollywood Unlocked, explaining that they all entered into a "a weird, ugly place” and couldn’t repair their friendships.

'OLD FRIENDS'

And when she found a picture of herself and Kim while on the RHOM, she reflected on her life.

Larsa, who had divorced her husband of 20 years Scott Pippen, said: ”People thought I wouldn't make it without Scottie. People thought I couldn't make it without some old friends.

“I'm great. I just basically want to live my best life, have fun, do whatever I want unapologetically."

It is unknown when Kim and Larsa became pals, but their friendship dates back as early as 2010.

The two were close for many years, but the Keeping Up With The Kardashian star unfollowed her when Kanye mentioned her in a July 2020 Twitter rant.

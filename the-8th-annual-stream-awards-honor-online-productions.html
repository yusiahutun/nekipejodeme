The 8th Annual Streamy Awards Honor Online Productions

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, February 23, 2024

LOS ANGELES, CA (October 22, 2018) –  dick clark productions and Tubefilter announced the winners of the final 18 Streamy Awards categories, presented live from The Beverly Hilton Oct. 22. The Streamy Awards, honoring the best in online video and the creators behind it, was streamed live globally on YouTube and hosted by comedy quartet The Try Guys.

Jessica Marie Garcia Is Expecting a Baby with Her Husband Adam Celorier

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 18, 2024

Jessica Marie Garcia and her husband, Adam Celorier, are expecting a baby after over three years of marriage. 

The couple dated for a long time before getting engaged in January 2016. Allegedly, the pair dated for over 13 years before settling down.

It is reported that Celorier popped the question to the Liv and Maddie star in front of their friends and family.

After over two years of engagement, the pair tied the knot in October 2018.

From Garcia's bridal party to the reception, the entire ceremony was lavish and extraordinary. The actress took to Instagram stories to share her wedding photos with her fans and followers.

Some of the famous faces who attended the wedding were Audrey Whitby, Shelby Wulfert, Kira Kosarin, and Joey Bragg.

Now, Garcia and her husband are ready to welcome a baby into their lives. 

Jessica Marie Garcia and her husband on their wedding day. (Source: Instagram)

Jessica Marie Garcia and Husband Expecting Baby 

The On My Block actress told People exclusively in November 2021 that she was pregnant. She told the tabloid that she felt "so good to finally tell the world our beautiful little secret."

She also revealed that she would give birth to the baby in February 2022. She also clicked some pictures with People where she flaunted her baby bump in a nude pleated taupe dress. 

On January 16, 2022, Garcia and her husband threw a baby shower for their child. The theme was a pink winter wonderland, and it was held at Lombardi House. 

The restored Victorian-style farmhouse was adorned with pink and white roses and peonies, along with lights, candles, and decors. The guests included Garcia and Celorier's friends and family. 

The actress posing for a picture and flaunting her baby bump. (Source: Instagram)

Garcia's On My Block co-stars and Chrissy Metz also joined the celebration. Because of the rapid growth of COVID-19, all the guests had to show a negative PCR test mandatorily to attend the baby shower.

They were served buffet meals, hors d'oeuvres, cocktail drinks, beverages, and hot chocolate, among others.

The soon-to-be parents also cut a three-tiered white cake decorated with snowflakes and their picture on top. 

As she did with her wedding photos, Garcia shared pictures and videos of the baby showers on her Instagram. She also took to Instagram stories to share the moments with her fans and followers. 

She also made TikToks with her friends and family, celebrating the event. 

Jessica Marie Garcia and Adam Celorier at their baby shower. (Source: Instagram)

Who Is Jessica Marie Garcia's Husband?

Celorier was born in Massachusetts on August 13, 1979. He studied and graduated from the New England Technical Institute for Television and Radio Program. 

Unlike his wife, Celorier is not active in front of the camera. However, he has contributed to several projects as a props master. 

While Celorier and Garcia have not revealed how they met, it is speculated that the pair met on set. He started in 2013 as a prop master in Barracuda's art department.

His other credits include Discarnate, Palm Swings, Little Women: LA, and Submerged. He has also worked with Apex, The Body Tree, and HTC Esports.

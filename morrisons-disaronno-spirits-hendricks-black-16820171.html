Morrisons selling HUGE bottles of Disaronno and Hendrick's Gin for Black Friday

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Black Friday 2018 falls on November 23, 2018, and Morrisons has gone all out for the super sale to help you save money.

The supermarket has launched a range of deals across their products, and announced they are selling huge bottles of spirits for the event.

Morrisons has announced it is selling 1.75 litre bottles of Disaronno, Boodles Gin and Hendrick’s Gin.

The 1.75L bottle of Hendrick’s costs £60. A 70cl bottle is usually £28 so customers could save £10 by buying bigger.

Meanwhile, the 1.75L bottle of Disaronno will cost £30. Customers can save a whopping £16.25 by buying bigger as a 70cl bottle usually costs £18.50.

And a 1.75L bottle of Boodles Gin will cost £40. 70cl is usually £22 so customers could save £15 by buying bigger.

However, customers will have to be quick as Morrisons has revealed stocks are limited - once they’re gone they’re gone.

Aldi has also announced the launch of their six litre bottle of Prosecco.

The giant bottle of fizz holds a massive 64 glasses - perfect for a Christmas gathering.

Julie Ashfield, managing director of buying at Aldi UK, said: “Christmas is a time when we know our shoppers come together with their friends and family to share a quality glass of fizz.

"As ever, sparkling wine is incredibly important in the run up to the festive season, so we wanted to give them something bigger and better than ever!"

The 6 Best Winter 2023 Nail Trends to Inspire Your Holiday Manicure

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Denim Blue

Jim McIsaac//Getty Images

When Taylor Swift stepped out at the Jets-Chiefs game in October, all eyes were on her—and her beauty look. Her moody mani puts a Midnights-esque spin on her recent blue theme timed to 1989 (Taylor's Version).

CND Vinylux in Midnight Flight

CND Vinylux in Midnight Flight

This polish is a near perfect match—and just so happens to be called Midnight Flight. And if you're looking for a slightly sparklier version, there's Midnight Swim, too.

Pale Shimmer

Paintbox

If you like a little glimmer with your winter manicure, get inspired by the pearl nails at PatBo, metallic pink at Christian Siriano, and glittery tips at LaPointe (pictured).

Paintbox On The Verge

Paintbox On The Verge

Not great at nail art? These Paintbox press-ons were the exact ones used on models at LaPointe.

Pop of Red

Courtesy Sundays

Red is continuing its reign into winter. The red French manicure seen at Alice + Olivia spring 2024 is a fun twist for the season, though classic red nails were also spotted on the Proenza Schouler and Brandon Maxwell runways.

Sundays No.16

Sundays No.16

At Alice + Olivia, nail artists painted on this exact shade of cherry red.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Moody Aura

Courtesy Jinsoon

Dreamy aura nails are still going strong, with Christopher John Rogers showcasing cool, contrasting color combinations by Jin Soon Choi.

Jin Soon Keppel

Jin Soon Keppel

Mint green is poised to have a big 2024. Get ahead of the trend by using it as a base for your aura mani.

Sugar-Plum Sheers

When a naked nail is too boring, but you don't want anything over-the-top, try this slightly pink, super-shiny mani from Michael Kors spring 2024.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Essie Gel Couture Long Lasting Nail Polish Kit - Inside Scoop + Top Coat

Essie Gel Couture Long Lasting Nail Polish Kit - Inside Scoop + Top Coat

The exact shade of polish used at Michael Kors isn't available yet, but this Essie duo will get you a similar look with gel-like shine.

Crystal Tips

Maya Spangler

If you want to go a little extra for the holidays, look no further than Sandy Liang spring 2024, where nails and fingertips were adorned with tiny rhinestones.

Olive & June Moonstone Shine Nail Art Gems

Olive & June Moonstone Shine Nail Art Gems

If you're nervous about sticking crystals directly to your skin, try using these stick-on gems from Olive & June x e.l.f., which don't require nail glue.

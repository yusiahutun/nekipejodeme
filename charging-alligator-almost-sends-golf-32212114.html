Charging alligator almost sends golf buggy careering into pond in terrifying close call

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, February 19, 2024

A shocking video has captured the moment a group of golfers were chased by an alligator at their course, causing them to almost crash their golf cart.

The wild incident, which took place in Florida, US, saw the beast run from the surrounding rough onto a road used to get between greens. The alligator managed to get in the way of the golf cart, causing it to swerve off the road and almost go flying into a nearby pond.

A clip of the incident has since been uploaded to the social media site X, formerly known as Twitter, where it has racked up more than 2.9million views. "Florida golf just hits different," the caption said.

Reports say reptiles often frequent golf courses in Florida and that they are likely to frequent ponds around the course. However, the animals are usually docile and no threat to players.

However, the video has left viewers terrified as many took to the comments to share their fear. One user said: "Damn, dinosaurs are coming for us."

Another said: "Did they drive in the lake? I’m not sure they’re out of peril," while a third posted: "This is my type of golfing." But a fourth joked: "Apparently he thought a warm meal was being delivered to him..."

Others defended the gator, claiming the creature was innocently trying to make it to the pond. A user wrote: "He wasn’t trying to get the humans, he was heading the river and the car came towards him and scared him out."

Big Head Todd &amp; the Monsters Jump On The Tour Wagon

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, April 2, 2024

BOULDER (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Stir in a little folk, blues, country, rhythm and blues, soul, contemporary, indie, alternative and everything in between and you have Big Head Todd & the Monsters. The Boulder based band just released their latest album, 'From the Archives – Volume 1', on Big Records their private label and will be mailing 500,000 free copies before following the new release up with their tour.

BHTATM's Theory is that there is so much piracy out there why not give the people what they want yourself, much the way Radiohead and Prince did but not on quite such a grand scale next follow it up with succesful tour. Very forward thinking for such a "laid back" band.

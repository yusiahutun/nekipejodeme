Beyonce's daughter Blue Ivy is 'richer' than Kylie Jenner and she's not even in High School

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, May 25, 2024

It’s no doubt Beyonce and Jay Z were going to create superstars together, and their eldest, Blue Ivy already seems to be running the world with her epic performances at her mom’s recent World Tour. If that wasn’t enough, the star, who turns 12 today is ‘richer’ than Kylie Jenner.

Blue Ivy was born on January 7, 2012, to music icons, Beyonce and Jay-Z. At the time, she was dubbed “the most famous baby in the world,” by Time. Over a decade later, and it looks like Blue has certainly lived up to this if her popularity, and net worth, are anything to go by.

Blue Ivy is already ‘worth’ $500 million

It’s a lot more than we were worth at 12 years old, we’ll tell you that. It’s said that Bey and Jay’s eldest, Blue Ivy is worth $720 million, Yahoo Finance reports.

But how? You may ask. Well, she’s been a star since around four years old, when Blue appeared on Jay-Z’s 4:44 album, delivering an epic performance on Blue’s Freestyle.

It’s not the only time her voice has featured on a track though. In 2019, the young star partnered up with her mother on the 2019 soundtrack album to Disney’s live-action, The Lion King: The Gift, in the song Brown Skin Girl.

She has production, and song-writing credits to her name, which all equal royalties. As well as her singing voice, her talking voice has also earned her some dollars in the bank, as the 12-year-old narrated the audiobook version of Hair Love.

Speaking of young rich stars, Kylie Jenner was once branded “the world’s youngest self-made billionaire” by Forbes, at age 21.

However, this was proved inaccurate and as of 2023, is estimated to have a net worth of $680 million, as per Forbes. An estimated $40 million less than Blue Ivy.

Beyonce was ‘so proud’ of her youngest during the Renaissance World Tour

Blue stunned fans when she made her huge onstage debut at her mom’s Renaissance World Tour last year. She was first spotted on stage in May, as she danced the night away to My Power and Black Parade.

The Irreplaceable singer was beaming with pride each night, as she took to her Instagram to gush about her daughter’s performances, writing: ” I’m so proud and thankful to be your mama. You bring us so much joy, my sweet angel.”

The 12-year-old continued to wow fans and by the end of the tour, they were amazed with her progress and stage presence. She’s definitely Bey and Jay’s daughter, that’s for sure.

Despite doing so well, the 42-year-old revealed she was hesitant to let her daughter perform in front of thousands of fans at first.

“She told me she was ready to perform, and I told her no,” fans heard her say during the Renaissance movie.

Marc Jacobs' Tattoo Confessions, The Man Repeller Gets a New Look, Plus More!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Media Platforms Design Team

- Marc Jacobs reveals his iconic tattoos for New York Magazine's September issue—including a Simpsonized version of himself that appeared in Harper's BAZAAR. Cut

- The Man Repeller gets a new look with a fully relaunched site. The Man Repeller

- The battle against designer counterfeits continues: the UK's National Physical Laboratory has created a type of radiation that can identify the cheap materials used to create knock-offs. Salon

- Damien Hirst will design 30 limited-edition scarves for Alexander McQueen to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the brand's iconic skull scarf. WWD

- Louis Vuitton will honor six "Timeless Muses"—including Catherine Deneuve, Kate Moss and Sofia Coppola—at an exhibit in Tokyo at the end of August. WWD

- The Cut profiles the seven women inspiring Hedi Slimane's nouveau-grunge aesthetic at Saint Laurent.

- Now that Rag & Bone has collaborated with Hunter on a selection of rain boots, we actually have a reason to look forward to inclement weather. SHEfinds

- Grazia chats with Annabelle Dexter-Jones about her new campaign for a limited-edition collection of Hervé Léger dresses on the Outnet. Her campaign co-star, DJ Alex Merrell, recounts her adventures and lists her inspirations at her blog, Vagabond.

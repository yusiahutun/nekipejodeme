Dando fiance describes their last moments together

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, March 14, 2024

The last minutes which Jill Dando spent with her fiance on the morning before she was murdered were today described to the jury trying her alleged killer.

Alan Farthing, a consultant at St Mary's Hospital in London, was due to marry the Miss Dando, known as the BBC's Golden Girl, on September 25 1999, but she was murdered that April.

In a statement read to the court Mr Farthing said that on the day before she was shot dead on her doorstep the couple had met and lunched with friends and then spent a quiet evening at home writing letters.

The following morning, Mr Farthing said he got up at 6.45am to get ready for work.

"Jill woke up. Sometimes she had difficulty in getting back to sleep. She reset the alarm.

"I showered. She offered to get some breakfast for me. I told her not to bother."

Then he found that "she had made some tea and bowl of cereal for me and gone back to bed".

At 1.30 that day, April 26, he learned of her death.

As his statement was read to the jury by Jonathan Laidlaw, for the prosecution, Mr Farthing sat in the well of the court showing no emotion.

His statement said he had first met Miss Dando in October 1997 through a colleague of his called Jenny.

"Jenny's aim for Jill and I to meet was a match-making exercise."

They went on to meet a number of times. Their first dinner date was on 23 November, he said.

"We hit it off and very quickly fell in love."

Mr Farthing was due to go to Australia for the New Year and "suggested to Jill she came with me and was delighted when she agreed."

She could only go for a week, which they spent in Sydney. He travelled to Adelaide to visit friends after she left.

"On my return Jill met me at Heathrow."

Mr Farthing said he was separated from his wife Maria. It had been a good marriage, but due to his wife's personal problems they could not go on living together.

He had bought a home in Chiswick, but his relationship with his wife deteriorated "to the degree we separated and we were divorced in 1998.

"The period of separation and divorce was a very difficult time for me. Jill was very supportive of me."

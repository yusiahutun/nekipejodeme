Amazon has slashed the price of this Steelseries gaming headset for PS5 and PC

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 19, 2024

If you're a PS5 or PC gamer looking for a new headset, Amazon might have the perfect deal for you. Gaming headsets can be pricey, but this one could be just right in terms of cost and quality.

The Steelseries Arctis Nova Pro wired headset is now only £149.99, down from £249.99 on Amazon. It's become a favourite among gamers, and it's easy to see why.

This headset comes with high-quality audio hardware, so you can hear even the quietest sounds in your games. It also has a stealth mic for clear chats with your mates and teammates.

Plus, it has a built-in amplifier that lets you adjust the sound to suit whatever game you're playing.

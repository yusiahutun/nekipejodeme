What is the Erica Cobb’sCareer and Net Worth?

Erica Cobb is an American media personality who has been on a number of radio and television programmes, including Pyke at Night, Eddie & Jobo, Eddie, Jobo & Erica Show, and Bj, Howie & Erica Show.

She made her way to television after college, working as a co-host on CBS Radio B96 Chicago Morning Show. Erica has made numerous and unselfish efforts since beginning her career in media, demonstrating her importance. She was a member of the DePaul Demonette Dance Team and possessed talent in the media field.

Erica Cobb is currently a television host and announcer for Tegna’s syndicated daytime show Daily Blast Live, as well as the host of the podcast Comeback with Erica Cobb. Erica has undoubtedly earned a sizable net worth, which has yet to be published in statistics, after experiencing the flavor of tremendous success and prosperity. According to a source, her monthly earnings as a TV host are expected to be around $55,000 per year.

Cobb’s Life Partner:

Cobb, who has been married to Anthony since 2016, doesn’t miss an opportunity to demonstrate her affection for him through her sweet Instagram pictures. On their wedding anniversary, July 16, 2020, Cobb released a stunning dreamlike fantasy wedding video in a spectacular backdrop on a beach in Mexico via Instagram. She didn’t waste any time in celebrating her 2019 anniversary by posting a photo of the couple on their wedding day and captioning it with heartfelt sentiments dedicated to her spouse.

Cobb is a family woman who values her family above all else. She spends a lot of time with them and posts about her happy moments on social media. She also used Instagram to wish Anthony a happy birthday on March 11 and express gratitude for his love, support, and encouragement in her life.

Cobb’s Failed First Marriage:The TV host was married to Jess Lehman before meeting the man of her dreams, Anthony. In 2010, the couple appeared on the television reality show The Marriage Ref. The pair came in with the hopes of resolving their disagreement over whether Cobb should keep her maiden name after her marriage, but Cobb lost the debate due to the majority of the audience’s support. The couple’s marriage dissolved after their participation on the show. But, after she met her genuine partner Anthony, love found its way back to her.

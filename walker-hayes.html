Who is Walker Hayes?

  • Hayes co-wrote and sang guest vocals on “Dirty Side” from Colt Ford’s 2014 album Thanks for Listening.   • In 2016, Hayes inked a publishing and production deal with Shane McAnally and SMACK/RareSpark, which resulted in the publication of two extended plays, 8Tracks Vol. 1 and 8Tracks Vol. 2.

Eliud Kipchoge training

Kipchoge’s trains at Kaptagat training camp with other athletes. Their training is planned around a weekly structured program and everyone has to stick to it despite the weather changes. The exercises also focus on discipline and teamwork.

The weekly program is divided into three main sections:

  • Track-Tuesday
  • Long run-Thursday
  • Interval training-Saturdays.

Part of the daily workout is a 10km afternoon recovery run. Intense training at a fast speed is also done on Thursday and Saturdays, while Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Friday are also for recovery sessions that incorporate mild muscle exercises.

Get To Know Chris Lardakis Parents, Siblings, Net Worth, Shaina Hurley

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, January 28, 2024

Love Is Blind star Shaina Hurley made her new relationship with her fiance Chris Lardakis Instagram official in March 2022. This, as expected, brought attention to Chris and his personal and professional life. So, here we are going to discuss details about his parents, siblings, and his net worth.

Keep learning about Chris Lardakis’ family with this article.

When Are Shaina Hurley And Chris Lardakis Getting Married?

Shaina Hurley and Chris Lardakis are getting married over the summer (of 2022) in Greece, as per the reports of PEOPLE.

Love Is Blind‘s Shaina Hurley went Instagram official with her fiance-turned-to-be-husband Chris Lardakis. The reality star recently posted the picture of a getaway with her new boyfriend at Nammos Mykonos. She captioned the post, “My ride or die forever”. The series of photos showed off sunset views and a few snaps holding Chris close.

In addition to that, a source confirmed to E! News, two of them are engaged. They reportedly met each other a couple of months after filming wrapping for Love Is Blind.

As reported, Chris proposed Shaina on 10 March 2022. Shaina went public with the relationship after announcing that she was in a new relationship on the Viall Files podcast. She also confirmed on the podcast that the relationship was pretty serious.

While on the show, Shaina first accepted the engagement proposal from Kyle Abrams but later admitted that she had feelings for Shayne Jansen. Shaina also mentioned that she had reservations because of the religious differences.

Chris was born in April 1982. That makes him 39 years old in 2021. He and his family identify themselves as Christian/Greek Orthodox.

Who Are Chris Lardakis Parents?

Chris Lardakis was born to his parents Sia and George K Lardakis.

Born in November 1944, his mother is currently 78 years old. Sia is the owner of House of Beauty and Devon Hair Design in Chicago Illinois.

As for his father, George was born in December 1948 and turned 74 years old in 2021.

Who Are Chris Lardakis Siblings?

Chris Lardakis has an older brother named Peter Lardakis who is married to his wife Kayla Sanford.

Peter and Kayla’s mutual friend Phil set them up after a few months after Phil started working for Peter at Kanela Breakfast Club in Lakeview. They have at least one child named Sia.

Peter and his wife Kayla both work at Kanela Breakfast. Kayla is working as the director of operations in the restaurant. She moved to Chicago in 2011 after graduating with a BS in Math and Theater from Black Hills State University, in South Dakota. Moreover, she also finished her time as a Combat Medic with the North Dakota Army National Guard.

Kayla also worked for Vines on Clark, and eventually was hired on as a personal banker for Chase Bank where she worked for two years. At Kanela, she helped with schedules and payroll, bartending a private event, and serving on the weekend. She even interviewed and hired people at the second Kanela Breakfast Club location in Old Town.

Kayla is originally from Watford City, North Dakota.

Peter and Chris also have a sister named Dora Drake.

How Much Is Chris Lardakis Net Worth?

By March 2022, Chris Lardakis accumulated a total net worth above $500K.

According to LinkedIn, Chris made his career debut joining Apple Home Health as Marketing Executive. In November 2012, Chris also founded Municipal Bar and Dining Co.

Chris started working in the company starting from May 2010 while based in greater Chicago. He continued working till December 2013. After leaving his job at Apple Home Health, he joined Providence Home Health Care as Director of Marketing in Northbrook, Illinois in December 2013.

After working for one year and two months, Apple Home Health again hired him in Business Development in August 2016. But in August 2018, he quit his job. In 2019, he shut down his upscale sports bar that he had started in 2012.

Currently, Chris is the co-founder/CEO of Kanela Breakfast Club. It is a Greek-inspired eatery bringing an organic and delicious approach to traditional American breakfast and lunch. He also started a few other outlets with former Illinois Treasurer Alexi Giannoulias. He founded it in February 2011 and opened the restaurant in other five different locations.

Moreover, Chris is also working as the General Contractor at CL Development LLC which he started working in September 2011. The report of Indeed.com reveals that General Contractors make $21.26 per hour in Chicago, Illinois.

Diet, Workout, Net Worth

Alexia of age 29 encourages people to find their favorite healthy food. According to her, dieting in the longterm can be hard to maintain, thus finding healthy food that one likes can be effective. In such a way, one doesn’t need to feel like they cannot eat something. 

Along with eating healthy, being happy about one’s diet is also one of Alexia’s secrets to fitness. Specifically, in diet, the fitness guru likes to have protein and fiber-rich food like chicken breasts, egg whites, and green leafy vegetables.

Interestingly, she shares before and after pics of her clients to inspire people about fitness. 

Talking about her workout routine, Alexia trains seven days a week. Her secret to staying motivated to go to the gym is switching up one’s exercise routine every two days.

According to the fitness guru, the changing of exercise helps her stay excited about going to the gym. And, the changes need not be significant, just small changes here and there among the traditional CrossFit and workouts. Further, she suggests staying hydrated always.

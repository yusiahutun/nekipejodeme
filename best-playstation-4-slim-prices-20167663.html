Is it a 4K console?

Unfortunately not, although the PS4 Slim does offer HDR colours. For 4K media and gaming, you'd need to opt for the more expensive PlayStation 4 Pro.

How big is the PS4 Slim hard drive?

The PlayStation 4 Slim comes with two storage options, 500GB or 1TB. The 1TB option is more expensive, but both can be expanded - either by opening the console up (not recommended), or plugging in a USB 3.0 hard drive.

About

Michelle Pfeiffer is a 65 year old American Actress. Born Michelle Marie Pfeiffer on 29th April, 1958 in Santa Ana, California and educated at Fountain Valley High School, Fountain Valley, California (1976), she is famous for Dangerous Liaisons, The Fabulous Baker Boys, Batman Returns in a career that spans 1977–present. Her zodiac sign is Taurus.

Al Pacino is a 83 year old American Actor. Born Alfredo James Pacino on 25th April, 1940 in East Harlem, Manhattan, New York City, New York, USA and educated at Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts, Manhattan, New York City, New York, USA (dropped out), he is famous for his roles as Michael Corleone in Francis Ford Coppola's The Godfather film trilogy, Frank Serpico in the 1973 neo-noir biographical crime drama film Serpico, Sonny Wortzik in the 1975 biographical crime drama film Dog Day Afternoon, Tony Montana in the 1983 crime drama film Scarface, Carlito Brigante in the 1993 crime drama film Carlito's Way, Lieutenant Colonel Frank Slade in the 1992 drama film Scent of a Woman, and Roy Cohn in the HBO drama miniseries Angels in America in a career that spans 1965–present. His zodiac sign is Taurus.

Are Bronte and Harrison from Married at First Sight Australia still together?

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Find out if the stars from series ten are still together

    Married At First Sight Australia stars Bronte Schofield and Harrison Boon were one of the first couples to meet for the first time at the altar for the tenth series of the reality show.

    But although their faces lit up when they first laid eyes on each other, things took a dramatic turn when Bronte was informed by her friend that Harrison had been keeping a girl on the side for when he "finished" the experiment. Watch the video below to see how the shocking moment unfolded…

    The couple immediately went into a downward spiral in the episodes that followed – so did they make it work in the end? Keep reading to find out more about where Bronte and Harrison are now, but warning, potential spoilers for series ten are ahead.

    Are Bronte and Harrison still together?

    Despite trying to make it work, Harrison and Bronte are no longer together. Fans watching the show in the UK will have to wait and see how things panned out on screen between them since the series is airing in the UK behind the Aussie schedule, but from social media, the couple have parted ways.

    MORE: What happened to Shannon and Caitlin on Married at First Sight Australia? 

    MORE: Married at First Sight Australia fans furious after star admits to cheating in new episode 

    Bronte posted a lengthy caption on Instagram about the fallout of the relationship and how she's been dealing with the split. She wrote in part: "I wanted this to work for so many reasons, reasons that no one will ever know or understand. Walking away from false hope & promises is not easy in real life, yet alone [sic] in an experiment you have given up EVERYTHING to be a part of.

    She added: "For any woman who has been in a similar situation, it is not weak to walk away. I will forever feel an immense amount of guilt for trying to make it work with someone who never wanted me to begin with and showing women everywhere that this is okay to keep choosing someone because you are scared to be alone."

    MORE: Where are Claire and Jesse from Married at First Sight Australia now? 

    Where is Bronte from MAFS Australia now?

    Judging by her Instagram, Bronte has moved on from her life. She hasn't posted much about what she's up to these days, but she did share some images of her recent vacation with a friend. Before joining the show, Bronte worked as an online beauty educator in her home city of Perth. It's not known whether she'll return to her career after appearing on the reality show.

    Where is Harrison from MAFS Australia now?

    Harrison has also kept mostly to himself since filming the show. The 32-year-old builder from New South Wales has shared some thoughts about how he was portrayed on the programme. He wrote on Instagram: "Coward: a person who is contemptibly lacking in the courage to do or endure dangerous or unpleasant things.

    View post on Instagram 

    "I don't meet this definition of a coward because I have endured moments within the experiment that have been unpleasant while being courageous in my relationship. #MAFS."

    As for the other girl he was reportedly seeing on the outside, there's no word on whether they stayed together but the reality star did say it was "blown out of proportion."

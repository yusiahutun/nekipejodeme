Katie Holmes Keeps Wearing This $795 Bag, and I Found an $15 Lookalike

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

Katie Holmes keeps toting around massive bags, so naturally I need a massive bag. The style icon has been sporting the statement accessory on repeat this spring, and her most recent errand-running outfit included the Mansur Gavriel Everyday Soft Tote, which retails for $795. Considering I’m not exactly working with a celeb-sized budget, I ran to Amazon to track down an iteration of Holmes’ signature style, and you’ll never guess what I found. Meet: the Nodykka oversized tote that’s just $15. (No, that’s not a typo.) 

The shockingly budget-friendly tote boasts the same sizable silhouette as the bag Holmes carries. It’s roomy enough to stow far more than your essentials, whether it’s serving as your carry-on companion, work bag (yes, it fits a laptop), or everyday purse. Made from a soft faux leather material, the bag has a relaxed shape with a small pocket to keep your valuables protected and snap closure for added security. While black is always in, the tote is sold in a whopping 135 additional styles to pair perfectly with your wardrobe. Be warned, though: You’ll likely want to snag a few colorways. 

Nodykka Oversized Tote Bag 

According to their rave reviews, Amazon shoppers are big fans of the $15 find. One customer called the tote a “no brainer” thanks to its jaw-dropping price point; they added that they’ve “spent so much money” on “expensive leather bags with designer names,” but they “wish [they] just bought this bag instead.” The same person went on to say it “looks gorgeous, holds everything, has comfortable handles,” and its material is “sturdy enough to stand alone.” And in spite of its price point, its “quality is exceptional,” according to a second reviewer who said it has “not one bit of wear” after a year.  

Above all, shoppers can’t stop applauding the bag’s effortlessly chic appearance. One customer said it completely “exceeded [their] expectations,” adding that its faux leather material and “sturdy stitching” give it “a sophisticated and luxurious” look, making it “suitable for both formal and casual occasions.” Another person “can’t believe how incredible the purse is” for such a great price point; they said it “looks and feels way more expensive than it is.” Plus, someone else confirmed it “looks like a designer bag,” but “doesn’t cost you an arm and a leg” to get your hands on.

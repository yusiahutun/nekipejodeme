    How to Choose the Best Pheromone Colognes?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before splurging on the best pheromone cologne:

    Notes: Every cologne has a distinctive blend of top, middle, and base notes that are combined with plenty of pheromones like methoxyestratraenone, androstenol, androstadienone, and estratetraenol. If you are a fan of citrusy fragrances, pick colognes that are combined with more fresh and aquatic notes of orange blossom and lemons. On the other hand, perfume heads who are more inclined towards purchasing woody fragrances may pick colognes with base notes of sandalwood, musk, amber, etc just like Stetson Black Cologne Spray. We recommend you try out a variety of colognes and then stick to the one that compliments your personal style and natural pheromone the most. 

    Season: Make sure you pay heed to the application season. Certain colognes are meant to be applied during winters whereas there are a few that work for all seasons, like the Virochemistry Pheromone Cologne. Seasons like fall and winter call out for more bodacious fragrances whereas for warm weather you may stick to floral, aquatic, and fruit fragrances. 

    Fragrance Family: Now, let us discuss the fragrance family. There is a wide range of fragrance families like fruity, aquatic, floral, woody, etc. Pick a cologne that compliments your personal style, taste, and preference. Ensure that you consider the occasion too. It is wise to invest in one like True Pheromones’s True Love that is suitable for all occasions, be it casual or formal.

    Longevity: Last but not least, make sure you get your hands on colognes that are long-lasting. Usually, the base notes of the cologne play the game here as they tend to linger on your skin for a prolonged period of time. We recommend Alfamarker Pheromone Cologne for Men for its superior longevity.

    After taking note of the above-mentioned aspects, get to know how to apply one accurately.

    How to Apply Pheromone Cologne?

    Following are the steps for applying a pheromone cologne in the right way:

    Step 1: Make sure you moisturize your skin thoroughly post-shower. This boosts the longevity of the cologne. 

    Step 2: Focus on body parts that are warm, like the corners of your elbows, etc.

    Step 3: Check the application method of each cologne. If it is a roll-on, you can directly apply it to your skin. 

    Step 4: To apply the cologne as a spray, put on pulse points like behind the ears, neck, wrist, etc. from a distance between 10 to 20 cm. 

    Step 5: Cologne sprays can also be applied on clothes; 2 to 4 spritzes will be sufficient to stay fragrant all day long.

    FAQ's

    What are pheromone colognes?

    Pheromone colognes are a kind of scent in which pheromones are added to perfumes to trigger arousal or attraction.

    Do pheromone colognes suit men as well as women?

    Yes, pheromone colognes work for everyone, be it male or female.

    How long do the effects of pheromone colognes last?

    The effect of pheromone colognes lasts straight for up to 24 hours.

    Can I combine pheromone colognes with other fragrances?

    Yes, you can layer them but do not combine them with non-fragrances like isopropyl alcohol or water.

    Can pheromone colognes be used in professional or formal settings?

    Yes, pheromone cologne can be used in professional, formal, or causal settings.

    Are there any specific considerations for individuals with allergies or sensitive skin?

    It is advisable to patch test and pick colognes that are meant to be applied on clothes.

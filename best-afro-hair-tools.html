Best hair tools for Afro hair

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Dyson Airwrap™ Styler Complete in Blue Blush

Dyson Airwrap™ Styler Complete in Blue Blush

Now 31% Off

For those who are yet to succumb to the hype around Dyson’s slick suite of revolutionary haircare tools, rest assured there’s nothing out there quite like the Airwrap, which relies on airflow technology, rather than excess heat, and comes equipped with an array of attachments for styling. Most importantly, it’s suited to all hair types – head to celebrity stylist and super-influencer Melissa Holdbrook-Akposoe’s Instagram for proof – and is capable of drying, smoothing and straightening Afro strands in one fell swoop.

Charlotte Mensah Paddle Brush

Charlotte Mensah Paddle Brush

Veteran hairstylist Charlotte Mensah is not only the owner of London’s best-loved Afro-hair salon and the brains behind some of Little Simz's and Michaela Coel’s most boundary-pushing ‘dos – she’s also the creator of her namesake luxury product line, which has won her a flurry of well-deserved plaudits. The most underrated gem? This cushioned paddle brush. Where poorly suited alternatives may have led those with thick Afro hair to avoid brushes entirely, this must-have tool has been specifically engineered for detangling curly and coily textures with minimal damage.

Ghd Helios Hair Dryer and Comb Nozzle

Ghd Helios Hair Dryer and Comb Nozzle

As for Mensah’s hairdryer of choice? That would be the Ghd Helios, along with its corresponding comb nozzle. Thanks to the brand’s ionic technology, it keeps moisture locked in (a must for curly and coily textures) and frizz to a minimum. Best of all, it speeds up the process by delivering an airflow of 120km per hour, all with surprisingly low levels of noise.

Briogeo Wide Tooth Detangling Comb

Briogeo Wide Tooth Detangling Comb

No Afro-haircare toolkit is complete without an extra wide-tooth comb, which – when paired with a dedicated product with ample slip and an easy-does-it technique – makes detangling a breeze. Enter this lilac creation by US curly-hair experts Briogeo, which works a treat on wet and dry hair alike, and comes with a hook for latching onto your shower.

T3 Lucea 25mm Professional Straightening & Styling Iron Set

T3 Lucea 25mm Professional Straightening & Styling Iron Set

While these days there’s no shortage of innovative tools that promise a sleek at-home blow-dry without the acrobatics, if a glossy silk press is what you’re after, then nothing beats the final pass of a flat iron. T3’s Lucea can hold its own among the better-known big-name offerings, thanks to its nine (yes, really) heat settings and frizz-reducing plates that promise that elusive high-shine finish.

Ruka EdgeSlick hair tool

Ruka EdgeSlick hair tool

For those who prefer their edges ‘laid’, a dedicated edge brush is a must (and will do the job a thousand times better than a repurposed toothbrush). Buzzy London-based disruptor Ruka has set out to create the ultimate version in the shape of its innovative EdgeSlick, which has detachable heads designed for smoothing, styling and separating out hairs. (Try teaming with its Hold Me Down Edge Gel, which has been enriched with aloe vera and mongongo oil for baby hairs that are set firmly in place, but not dried out or crunchy.)

Kitsch Satin-Wrapped Microfiber Hair Towel - Aura

Kitsch Satin-Wrapped Microfiber Hair Towel - Aura

With the desire to minimise breakage and retain length front of mind when washing and drying Afro hair, it’s worth investing in a microfibre towel, which proves less aggressive on delicate strands than its terry-cotton counterpart. Better yet, turn to Kitsch for its microfibre-lined hair turban which not only cuts air-drying time significantly but is undeniably glamorous to boot.

Denman DC06 Pin Tail Comb

Denman DC06 Pin Tail Comb

For creating super-sharp partings, you’ll need a trusty pin-tail comb, but not all are created equal. Try this one by heritage brand Denman, which offers pinpoint precision when sectioning and partitioning hair – perfect for expert-level braids, cornrows and more.

Ceremonia Scalp Masajeador

Ceremonia Scalp Masajeador

It’s for good reason that scalp health has been posited as the next frontier in haircare, with countless trichologists deeming it to be the foundation of strong strands – and as deserving of TLC as the rest of your skin. Cutting-edge serums aside, try this scalp massager by Latinx-founded clean haircare brand Ceremonia, which boosts blood flow to the scalp (thought to stimulate growth) and aids in thorough cleansing. It's the sort of tool you’ll wonder how you ever went without.

Re-comb Seashore Pik

Re-comb Seashore Pik

For globally renowned hairstylist and THOM founder Cyndia Harvey, Re-comb is a go-to in her kit. Founded by fellow editorial stylists Sarah Jo Palmer and Christopher Deagle, the eco-conscious comb brand engineers all its wares from recycled plastic waste that would otherwise end up in landfill. This ‘Seashore’ pik comes with a sturdy handle and is designed to detangle 4C hair without snagging – or snapping.

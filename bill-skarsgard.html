Bill Skarsgård is a prominent Swedish actor. Bill Skarsgård gained immense fame for his appearance in several films and series including Hemlock Grove, It, It Chapter Two, Simple Simon, and The Divergent Series: Allegiant among numerous others.

Early Life

Bill Skarsgård was born on August 9, 1990, and he is currently 30 years old. Bill hails from Vällingby, Sweden, and his zodiac sign is Leo. His full name is Bill Istvan Günther Skarsgård. Talking about his family, Bill’s parents are his mother My Skarsgård, and father Stellan Skarsgård. Bill’s father is an actor whereas his mother is a doctor.

Similarly, Bill has seven siblings namely Alexander, Gustaf, Valter, Sam, Eija, Ossian, and Kolbjörn. The latter two are Bill’s half-brothers from his father’s marriage with Megan Everett.

Bill Skarsgård – Net Worth

This veteran actor has earned a decent amount of earnings throughout his career. Moving on, Bill Skarsgård has a net worth estimation of approximately 6 million dollars as of 2021.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Bill is a famous actor. He gained prominence for his roles in It in 2017 and It Chapter Two in 2019. The aforementioned films are based on Stephen King’s novel of the same name. Bill also appeared in the comedy Simple Simon in the year 2010 and thriller The Divergent Series: Allegiant in 2016.

Similarly, Bill appeared in the espionage thriller Atomic Blonde in the year 2017, comedy-horror Villains in 2019, and the drama Nine Days fairly recently in the year 2020. The Swedish actor also acted in the supernatural horror series Hemlock Grove from the year 2013 to 2015. Likewise, Bill also appeared in the anthology horror series Castle Rock from 2018 to 2019.

Bill Skarsgård gained immense fame for playing the role of Roman Godfrey in Hemlock Grove. For this project, the Swedish actor also worked with Famke Janssen. Bill then starred as the main character in the 2010 comedy Simple Simon. Later in 2016, Bill portrayed the role of Matthew in The Divergent Series: Allegiant. Two years later, Bill appeared as the character Pennywise in the 2017 horror film It.

Taylor Swift's 'most meaningful holiday' with Travis Kelce is a special gift

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, April 26, 2024

Taylor Swift got the ‘best gift ever’ this holiday season and it is all happening thanks to her NFL heartthrob boyfriend Travis Kelce.

The love story involving Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce is only getting dreamier. As per sources close to the celebrity couple, the duo are making new memories this holiday season at Travis’s brand new $6 million mansion in Kansas City.

Although the celebrity footballer, who plays for the Kansas City Chiefs, recently experienced a loss against the Las Vegas Raiders, his holiday was made more special with Tay’s company and exchanging gifts with teammates.

Inside Taylor Swift’s ‘most meaningful holiday’ with Travis Kelce

Taylor Swift had the best holiday gift ever as she got to spend the Christmas period with beau Travis Kelce. A source close to the couple revealed that Tay’s family joined Travis and his father at his Kansas City mansion after the Christmas game and they spent the holiday together.

The event was special for the 34-year-old hitmaker, as one source said: “Seeing their families together on such a special holiday was the best gift ever for Taylor. She’s never had this happen before and it made the holiday the most meaningful one for her.”

It was also the first Christmas for Traylor (the couple name of Tay and Trav), so starting new traditions for the holidays will only make their connection stronger.

“Her friends think that this is just more proof that Travis is going to be a Swiftie for life,” the source added.

It is no surprise that lifelong Eagles fan Scott Swift (Taylor’s dad) is now wearing a Kansas City Chiefs sweatshirt to show his support for Tay’s new boyfriend. Scott first met the Catching Kelce star in Argentina, when the tight end flew to watch the music legend perform in her Eras Tour concert. Soon enough, Travis has won him over.

Travis seemed to have gotten close to T. Swizzle’s father as he talked about his football connections in a new Height podcast episode.

‘He’s a huge football guy – he played college ball. I believe a year at Hawaii and then a year or two – I forget how long he was there – at Delaware,’ Kelce said in the podcast about Tay’s dad.

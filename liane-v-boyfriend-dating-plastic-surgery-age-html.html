Liane V's Blissful Relationship:

Liane is currently dating the thirty-year-old model Don Benjamin, who became a finalist on season 20 of 'America's Next Top Model.' V started dating her boyfriend back in 2015.

A couple of months back in December 2017, V took her Instagram, and she shared a picture with her boyfriend while attending the 16th Annual Asian American Awards.

V again shared a cute snap with her love Don back in December 2017; this time her caption was related to their YouTube channel 'forever us' where they post videos every Wednesday. 

The way V and Don look into at each other's eyes tells us that the two are loved up and enjoying each other's company. Likewise, on December 2017, V posted a picture on her Instagram where she was seen admiring the nature with her boyfriend.

V makes a hell of a couple with her man, Don. On the occasion of New Year 2018, V along with her companion wished their fans and followers a happy new year via Instagram.

Likewise, on January 2018, the couple were seen attending an event and didn't miss to share a lovely picture with her fans and followers.

With the passing time, many relationships lose their spark, but V and Don have stood by each other's side all the time. It has been almost three years that they are together. By the looks of it, V and Don share a lovely bond and is enjoying each other's company.

More About Liane V

V was born in San Jose, California on 22 August 1986 and is 31 of age. She was born to parents Wally Valenzuela and Prima Valenzuela. V, who stands at the height of 1.70 meters (5.5 ft), was an athlete in school and competed in gymnastics, cheerleading, and basketball.

From the young age, her one dream was to make a name in music. She then promoted herself online through YouTube and social media. After that, she was signed by the production company, Brand X.

V has amassed over 7 million fans to her LianeV musical.ly account. She is also known by the same name on Vine, where V has earned over 4.6 million followers. V is a confident and articulate personality on camera, and has hosted red carpets of some well-known events including ‘The Voice,’ ‘X-Factor’ and ‘American Idol.’

Likewise, V has changed a lot over the years, which led to speculation that she probably must have done plastic surgery. But she has not spoken about anything about the surgery in any of the interviews or social media.

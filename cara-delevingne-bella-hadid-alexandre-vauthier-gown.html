Cara Delevingne just wore that Alexandre Vauthier gown Bella Hadid wore on the catwalk

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Getty

After a week of impossibly chic movie premiere style, Cara Delevingne wore one of the most-talked-about dresses of couture fashion week on the red carpet last night.

Cast your mind back to the Paris shows earlier this month when Bella Hadid strutted her stuff for one of the sexiest brands on the schedule, Alexandre Vauthier. The model could be seen grappling to hold the dress together in order not to reveal too much. Basically, she was all of us in a wrap dress.

Related Story

Last night, Delevingne attended the Paris premiere of Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets wearing the same plunging silver frock by the French label.

However, while Hadid spent the duration of the catwalk holding the hem as not to reveal too much, Delevingne opted to have hers fitted much looser and did not appear to suffer from the same problem.

Still, both looked sensational in the gown, helping to further cement Vauthier's status as one of the sexiest red carpet dressers of all time.

For the Fashion Archivist Swiftie

Taylor Swift HARPER'S BAZAAR USA August 2018

For the Fashion Archivist Swiftie

Taylor Swift HARPER'S BAZAAR USA August 2018

Past magazines featuring Taylor Swift are a window into her style eras and her old interviews at once. The Swifties who appreciate fashion history and their icon's every move will love a chance to look back at her old photoshoots, especially if they're a cult-favorite like her 2013 Wonderland cover to promote 1989. I'm biased, but I love Swift's mod 2018 cover for Bazaar: It shows her in an aesthetic that was so different from how she dressed in real life at the time.

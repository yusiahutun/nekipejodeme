X-rated model asks fans if they want 'pint and the darts' but everyone is distracted

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Darts fever has swept the nation – and one X-rated model appears to have got many fans even hotter and sweatier.

Morgan Holly Moore has jumped on the darts bandwagon ahead of the Paddy Power PDC World Championship final. She, along with millions around the globe, will be tuning in tonight (Wednesday) to see if 16-year-old Luke Littler can make history.

The teenage sensation comes up against Luke Humphries, 28, in a battle of the two most in-form players in the game right now. And Morgan has shown she'll have her focus fully on the clash at Alexandra Palace.

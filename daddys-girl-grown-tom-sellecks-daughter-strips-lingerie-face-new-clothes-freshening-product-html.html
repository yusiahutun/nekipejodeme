Actor Tom Selleck's daughter, Hannah, is an accomplished equestrian with her sights set on the 2016 Rio Olympics and now, adding another string to her bow, she is trying her hand at modeling.

The 25-year-old from Los Angeles is the face of a Reviver, an on-the-go freshening wipe designed to remove bad odor from clothing.

A behind-the-scenes video from the brand's campaign photo shoot shows Miss Selleck doing a mix of activities so she can put the product to the test, from working out at a gym to lying in a smokey bedroom dressed in lingerie.

Another scene sees her sat at a dinner table tucking into smelly dishes, including oysters, lobster and chunks of char-grilled steak without the use of a knife or fork.

And in the final clips she appears leaning against dumpsters dressed in a red velvet evening gown.

Speaking about the new laundry product she was picked to endorse, Miss Selleck said: 'I spend a lot of my time at the barn with my horses.

Smokin': During a photo shoot for the brand Miss Selleck put the product to the test by puffing on a cigar

Building up a sweat: She later took to the gym for a workout

'When I found out about Reviver – it was amazing. Now I use it every day after leaving the barn to ensure that my clothes smell fresh.

'I also keep a Reviver swipe in my purse to use when I'm out at the bars and clubs and around friends who smoke . . . It's like a breath mint for my clothes.'

For her first modeling assignment Miss Selleck was styled by Marina Monroe and lensed by celebrity photographer Gavin Bond.

He is known for his sexy and sophisticated portraits of stars, ranging from Jennifer Aniston to Rihanna to Sasha Baron Cohen.

Carnivore: One frame from the Reviver shoot sees her eating smelly dishes, including lobster and char-grilled steak without the use of a knife or fork - she wears a poodle top from the yet-to-launch fashion brand Haus

Stinky location: And finally Miss Selleck was made to hold a pose next to dumpsters wearing an evening gown from the Los Angeles-based couture label, Chagoury

Behind-the-scenes footage shows that Miss Selleck clearly feels comfortable in front of the camera.

But for now it is horse riding, not modeling, that remains her primary focus.

The Loyola Marymount University graduate, whose mother Jillie Mack has been married to her Magnum P.I. star dad for 26 years, says that she was introduced to the saddle at the age of four.

Real passion: Natural beauty: While the 25-year-old appears comfortable in front of the camera, horse riding is her primary focus and she has her sights set on the 2016 Rio Olympics

She previously told Sidelines magazine that instead of the Hollywood lifestyle her 'very private' parents encouraged her to find her 'own path'.

Over the years she tried ice skating, ballet, soccer and gymnastics. In college she did take one acting class, but described getting up on stage as 'terrifying'.

After leaving university with a degree in communications she accepted a summer internship at a public relations firm in Beverly Hills but found that she didn’t like 'being inside from 9 to 6'.

Flashback: Miss Selleck pictured with her famous father Tom and mother Jilly Mack at the Emmys in 2000

It was then that she decided to ride full-time.

'My passion was horses and I knew then that horses was what I wanted to pursue.

'[My dad] has always been very supportive of my riding and so has my mom.

'My mom is amazing; she comes to nearly every show and is always at the ring video taping my rounds. I wouldn’t be where I am today without all their love and support.'

Keeping fresh: Reviver odor-eliminating wipes, which Miss Seller's is now helping to promote, were the brainchild of Los Angeles-based entrepreneur, Ben Kusin

Reviver odor-eliminating wipes, which Miss Seller's is now helping to promote, were the brainchild of Los Angeles-based entrepreneur, Ben Kusin.

They are currently on sale at Walgreens sores nationwide, priced at $2.99 per sachet.

There is also an animal-friendly version of the product available at Petco.

An American Werewolf In London's Jenny Agutter says there's too much sex on telly

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, January 1, 0001

Her steamy shower scene in the Oscar-winning film An American Werewolf In London remains one of the most scintillating in cinematic history — but now Jenny Agutter is demanding that broadcasters stop relying on sex to boost their ratings.

'It's wrong to assume the only way now to entice a TV audience is by sexualising a show,' Agutter, 63, tells me.

'I think the viewers will come if the quality of the writing and the acting is good enough in the first place.'

The Somerset-born star first appeared naked on celluloid at the age of 19 in 1971 film Walkabout but, these days, she prefers to wear a wimple as Sister Julienne in the BBC series Call The Midwife.

'People look for good programmes and I think sometimes being commercial means ticking boxes, but not always the right boxes,' adds Agutter.

'Nobody knows beforehand what the attraction will be. I had no idea that Call The Midwife was going to attract the audience it did. I really would not have bet on it.'

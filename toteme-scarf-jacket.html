Toteme's scarf jacket Best scarf jackets and coats

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Totême

Totême

Credit: Courtesy

The OG scarf-jacket, Totême's embroidered design comes in charcoal, black, grey, khaki and beige. Made from 88 per cent wool, it's cosy but its weight stops it from being stifling meaning you can easily wear it from autumn through to the beginning of spring.

Cos

Cos

Credit: Courtesy

A more relaxed take on the style, Cos' padded coat is made from cupro and cotton, and features a detachable scarf that is attached at the back, a curved hem, cut-out vent and double-ended zip closure with a smart leather pull tab. Wear for weekends in the city.

Reiss

Reiss

Credit: Courtesy

Reiss' take on the scarf coat is undeniably smart and would prove invaluable in a working wardrobe. Featuring a flattering waist tie, elegant draping scarf that's detachable and oversized lapels, it's a style you'll be able to wheel out winter after winter.

Tove

Tove

Credit: Courtesy

Tove's wool-blend coat is so beautifully simple. A twist on the peacoat, it features a flared A-line fit and a drape that can be worn as a scarf or left undone as an elongated collar.

Whistles

Whistles

Now 36% Off

Credit: Courtesy

Whistles' design is the perfect item to team with your go-to jeans and favourite knit. The fringing will add a retro edge and its oversized draping will make your look instantly chic in the perfect "oh, I just threw this on" way.

Isabel Marant Étoile

Isabel Marant Étoile

Credit: Courtesy

Another fringed entry but this time from Isabel Marant. Always a brand to rely on for the ultimate laidback 'cool girl' look, this design will go with anything and everything in your off-duty wardrobe.

Vanessa Bruno

Vanessa Bruno

Credit: Courtesy

Vanessa Bruno's design makes changing your look super easy thanks to the detachable belt and scarf. Made from a wool blend, it has a smart yet relaxed shape and features generous front-facing pockets as well as large slits up the sides for easy movement.

Ted Baker

Ted Baker

Now 30% Off

Credit: Courtesy

For those that want to inject pattern into their existing wardrobe, look to Ted Baker. The Jilliya Twill coat hangs beautifully and will steal the show styled with a pared back outfit.

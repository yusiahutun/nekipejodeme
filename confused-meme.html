8 Funny Confused Memes

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 16, 2024

Having the right meme for every moment is an art, especially for reactions. And confused memes are one of the most popular types of reaction memes online. These allow you to express confusion or highlight confusing situations in a funny way.

Let's take a look at the best confused memes on the web today. Keep them in mind and you'll always have the perfect reaction ready to go.

Confusion Memes Explained

If you're not familiar with memes, read up on our introduction to memes to get acquainted first.

Confusion memes compose a broad spectrum of content, but they're all based around laughing at misunderstandings and mistakes. A confused meme is a great way to point out that a statement is inconsistent, paradoxical, or just plain nonsensical.

We'll mainly cover reaction images in this list. This type of confused meme is often used as a response to a statement that makes no sense.

Like a lot of memes, they feature someone (often a famous person, but not always) looking confused about something. They may include text, but don't always do so. You'll often see them in Twitter replies or other social media comments.

However, these aren't the only kind of confusion memes. General image macros, which are pictures with overlaid text, can also qualify as confusion memes. We've included some of these, too.

A lot of these memes are newer, but some are classics. And as time goes on, we'll probably see more confusion memes surface.

1. Confused Nick Young

We start with one of the most popular confusion memes. Confused Nick Young is a reaction image featuring an NBA player of the same name. It came from a show called Thru The Lens, which covered a day in the life of the basketball player.

The meme is an image of Nick with a confused facial expression and question marks around it, which was taken directly from the show. You can use the original image directly, or change it to add text or edit Nick.

It's a punchy way to express confusion, leading to its status as one of the most popular memes of 2015.

2. Confused Travolta

https://imgur.com/0UIwFbR

If you're interested in video memes, here's one for you. This confused man meme features a cutout of John Travolta's character Vincent Vega from the movie Pulp Fiction. In the original clip, Travolta's character looks around the room while hearing someone talk to him over an intercom.

Because he looks lost, this clip from the movie quickly turned into a meme. It places the clip of Travolta in various situations with captions where confusion is appropriate. It's a fun one, and infinitely exploitable with some editing chops.

3. Math Lady

This confused look meme features a Brazilian actress named Renata Sorrah, with a scene taken from the telenovela Senhora do Destino. Her character is popular with viewers, leading to clips from the show turning into memes.

The modern version of this meme features four screenshots from the original GIF of Sorrah looking confused. Each of the shots features math equations overlaid, with a caption that typically mentions having to do some kind of mental calculation due to confusion.

This makes for a funny way to express that you're furiously crunching numbers in your head.

4. Wat

"Wat" is an intentional misspelling of "what." It's most often used to express confusion at a story or image. You can use "wat" on its own, but it's often paired with an image.

One of the most popular image macros featuring "wat" is a surprised old woman. This is a fine way to use it, but you can add "wat" to anything that elicits your confusion.

5. Confused Girl

This confused face meme doesn't feature anyone famous; it's a simple image of a girl with an incredulous look on her face. It pairs well with captions which lead to that look appearing on your face.

Interestingly, there was once a story circulating online that the subject of this image tried to sue Instagram because the photo spread so much. However, this turned out to be a hoax.

The meme isn't as popular as it once was, but it still offers a great face for your most confusing moments.

6. Confused Mr. Krabs

Our next confused meme features the character Mr. Krabs from the cartoon show SpongeBob Squarepants. It's an image of Mr. Krabs surrounded by an angry mob, edited to have a ripple effect around him.

The meme is typically paired with captions of bewilderment, such as waking up from a nap and quickly becoming confused.

Secondary Confusion Memes

The above represent the best confusion memes at this time, as they're all directly related to the theme of confusion. As mentioned in our introduction to memes, there are also plenty of one-off image macros that deal with confusion. However, we're covering only proper memes in this list, as anyone can make an image of confusion.

Thankfully, there are other related memes worth looking at. While the below memes aren't necessarily about confusion, they're similar and can be used as confusion memes in a lot of cases.

7. Are You Serious Face

This meme, often used in old-school rage comics, is a blast from the past. It features a basic drawing of David Silverman, who made a perplexed face during an interview with Bill O'Reilly.

The meme is typically used to react to incredibly stupid or ignorant statements. So while this one isn't strictly about confusion, it can still make for an appropriate confused man meme.

8. Not Sure If

This classic made our list of the best memes ever. It's a shot of the character Fry from the show Futurama, shown with squinted eyes.

The meme starts with the phrase "Not sure if" and evaluates two possible explanations for a scenario. This makes it a good way to express confusion over which answer you think is correct.

The Best Confused Memes You'll Love

We've looked at a collection of great confused memes. Whether you enjoy laughing at different versions of these memes or want something to use for reactions online, they provide plenty of fun.

WWE divas legend hit by X-rated leak as sex act video appears online

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, February 5, 2024

The former WWE Women’s champion was previously targeted by hackers.

But now a video of her engaging in a sex act has appeared online along with other nude images.

It comes after WWE stars Paige and Charlotte had stolen naked photos leaked online.

Victoria, 46, has not worked with WWE since 2009, but had continued to the wrestling scene – performing in TNA and Ring of Honour.

Nude photos of the star were leaked earlier this year in massive dump of celebrity pictures.

But she is now a repeat victim after hackers flooded the internet with a new wave of images.

Real name Lisa Marie Von, Victoria has not yet commented on the alleged leaks.

But it appears to be part of a wave of hacking thefts which has targeted a raft of superstars and ex-wrestlers from the WWE.

Other stars targeted have included former announcer Maria Kanellis, ex-Divas champion Kaitlyn and former WWE star Melina.

Current WWE stars, Charlotte and Paige commented on the issue – demanding the pictures be taken down.

Other wrestlers Alexa Bliss and Summer Rae both denied alleged pictures online were of them.

Police this year convicted a hacker linked to the so-called “Fappening” event which saw dozens of celebs have pics stolen.

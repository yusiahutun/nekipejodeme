Mark Consuelos proved himself an adoring husband on Friday's episode of Live With Kelly And Mark.

The 52-year-old Hollywood hunk — who recently spoke candidly about working with his wife Kelly Ripa, 52— praised her body, calling her petite figure 'perfect.'

It came after Ripa joked that she doesn't have 'actual breasts' during a segment on the daily talk show in which she shared a stat about how often people should wash their bras.

'Now, when you have bras that are essentially just pecks that you’re just trying to attach a microphone to – which is what I’ve got going on here – I wash my bras as I would an undershirt, let’s say, which is every wear,' she quipped.

Left nearly speechless at one point, Consuelos found the words to say, 'I think [your body is] perfect.'

When her husband hadn't said anything for a moment, she asked him: 'You OK? Are you feeling light headed?'

It was at that moment that Consuelos assured his wife, who he wed in 1996, 'I think it’s perfect.'

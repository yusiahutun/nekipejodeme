The movie “This Is 40” is a comedy-drama film directed by Judd Apatow.

This film serves as a spin-off to the 2007 film “Knocked Up,” focusing on the lives of the supporting characters Pete and Debbie, played by Paul Rudd and Leslie Mann.

“This Is 40” was released in 2012.

The film was widely anticipated as a follow-up to the successful comedy “Knocked Up.”

Judd Apatow wrote the screenplay for “This Is 40.”

Apatow is known for his comedic style and ability to portray realistic and relatable characters.

The movie’s title “This Is 40” refers to the age of the main characters.

Pete and Debbie are both turning 40 in the film, and it explores the challenges and changes that come with reaching this milestone.

The film features an ensemble cast.

Alongside Paul Rudd and Leslie Mann, the movie stars Jason Segel, Megan Fox, Albert Brooks, and John Lithgow, among others.

This is Judd Apatow’s fourth directorial feature film.

Prior to “This Is 40,” Apatow directed “The 40-Year-Old Virgin,” “Knocked Up,” and “Funny People.”

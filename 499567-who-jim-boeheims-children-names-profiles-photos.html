Who are Jim Boeheim's children?

How many kids does Jim Boeheim have? The retired college basketball coach has four children, two daughters and two sons. So, in 2023, what are their whereabouts? Here is a breakdown of Jim Boeheim's children below.

Elizabeth Boeheim

Elizabeth is the eldest child of Jim Boeheim. However, she is not a biological child of the ex-coach but rather an adopted one. Elizabeth was adopted, aged one week after her adoptive parents, Jim and Elaine (Jim's ex-wife), had trouble having children.

Elizabeth was born on June 1985 in the USA and is 38 years old in 2023. She attended Jamesville-DeWitt Central School in New York, graduating in 2003. Elizabeth proceeded to join Colby College, where, after graduating, she enrolled at the University of Montana and graduated with a Master's Degree in Literature.

In 2023, Elizabeth is a lecturer at the University of Montana. She teaches English and Literature and is a professor in the English faculty at the university. Furthermore, Elizabeth is the only Boeheim child not involved in basketball.

Jimmy Boeheim Jr

Jimmy is the first biological child of Jim Boeheim. He was born on 4 May 1998 in DeWitt, New York, USA and is 24 years old in 2023. Jimmy's parents are Jim and Juli. His siblings include Elizabeth and twins Buddy and Jamie.

Jimmy attended high school at the Jamesville-DeWitt Central School in New York. He made the basketball team, joining the 2014-15 team that reached the New York State finals. After high school, Jimmy enrolled at Cornell University in 2017 to study finance. He graduated in 2021 with a BA in Applied Economics.

According to Jimmy's LinkedIn, he joined Syracuse University in 2021, pursuing a master's in Instructional Design Development and Evaluation. He graduated in 2022 with his master's degree and is now playing in the Czech basketball league with the CEZ Nymburk.

Jamie Boeheim

Does Jim Boeheim have a daughter? Yes, he does. Jamie Boeheim is the second daughter of Jim. She was born on 1 November 1999 in Syracuse, New York, USA. Her parents are Jim and Juli Boeheim, while her siblings are Buddy (twin brother), Jimmy and Elizabeth.

Jamie attended school at Jamesville-DeWitt Central School in New York, where she joined the basketball team. In season 2017-18, she became the team captain.

After high school, Jamie enrolled at the University of Rochester in August 2018. She joined the school basketball team making 21 appearances in her rookie season. Jamie graduated in May 2022 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology. She is currently a student at Syracuse University pursuing a Master's degree in Social Work (MSW).

Buddy Boeheim

Is Buddy Boeheim adopted? No, he isn't. Jim Boeheim's son is his biological child and has a twin sister, Jamie. Buddy was born on 1 November 1999 in Syracuse, New York, USA and is 24 years old in 2023.

Buddy enrolled at Jamesville-DeWitt Central School in New York, where he was named All-Central New York Large School Player of the Year after a stellar debut season. Buddy transferred to Brewster Academy in Wolfeboro, New Hampshire, in his senior season.

Does Jim Boeheim's son play for him?

No, not currently, because Jim is retired. However, when he was active, his sons, Jimmy and Buddy, did play for him (at different periods) at Syracuse University. His daughter, Jamie, also played under him while studying at Syracuse.

How many sons does Jim Boeheim have?

The retired coach has two sons. They are Jimmy, born in 1998, and Buddy, born in 1999. Both of Jim's sons have pursued a career in basketball, with Buddy playing professionally for the Detroit Pistons and Jimmy playing for the CEZ Nymburk in the Czech basketball league.

Jim Boeheim's children include Elizabeth, Jimmy, Jamie and Buddy. They have each made names for themselves outside their fathers' fame thanks to their hard work and perseverance.

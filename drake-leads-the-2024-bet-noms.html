Drake Leads The 2024 BET Noms

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 8, 2024


(CelebrityAccess) — On Thursday, BET announced the nominees for the 2024 BET Awards, with Drake leading the charge with seven nods. Drake’s nominations include the Album of the Year’ for For All The Dogs (Scary Hours Edition), ‘Best Male R&B/Pop Artist,’ ‘Best Male Hip Hop Artist,’ ‘Best Collaboration’ with Sexyy Red and SZA “Rich Baby Daddy,” ‘Viewer’s Choice’ with Sexyy Red and SZA “Rich Baby Daddy,” and twice for ‘Video of the Year’ for “First Person Shooter” with J. Cole and “Rich Baby Daddy” with Sexyy Red and SZA.

Drake was followed closely by Nicki Minaj, who earned six nominations, including ‘Album of the Year’ for Pink Friday 2, ‘Best Female Hip Hop Artist,’ Video of The Year for “Barbie World” with Ice Spice and Aqua, ‘BET Her’ with Tasha Cobbs Leonard “Blessings” and twice for ‘Best Collaboration’ with Lil Uzi Vert “Everybody” and “Barbie World” with Ice Spice and Aqua.

Other nominees for 2024 include J. Cole, Sexyy Red, SZA, and Victoria Monét, with five each, and 21 Savage, Beyoncé, Doja Cat, Megan Thee Stallion, Tyla, and Usher who are tied with four nominations each.

Nominations were selected by the BET Voting Academy, which is comprised of a group of entertainment professionals and influencers in the fields of music, media, digital marketing, sports journalism, public relations, and creative arts.

The 2024 BET Awards will take place on June 30, 2024 and will air live on BET.

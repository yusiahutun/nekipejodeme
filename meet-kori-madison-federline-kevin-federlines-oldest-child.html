Meet Kori Madison Federline, Kevin Federline's Oldest Child

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 3, 2024

Kori Madison Federline got famous for being the stepdaughter of Britney Spears. Her father is Kevin Federline, and her mother is Shar Jackson. Kevin is known because of his high-profile relationships with famous women. As of 2022, he was reportedly working as a DJ. 

He has a passion for music and has attempted to kick-start his musical career several times but with little success. On the other hand, Shar Jackson, his former wife, and Kori’s mother, is a famous actress and model. 

She did some work for Ubiquitous, John Robert Powers, and Barbizon agencies before focusing solely on acting.

What We Know About Kori Madison Federline

Kori Madison Federline is one of the kids Shar Jackson had with Kevin Federline before they both went their separate ways. She was born on July 31, 2002, in Yorba Linda, California. Jackson and Kevin have done all they can to give her a private life. However, she has been known to join her parents at public functions now and then. 

Kori also rarely gets posted on her mother’s social media pages, but she has an Instagram account. However, she keeps it private. Kori has been described as a “media personality,” “weeb,” and “artist” on her Instagram page. 

Kori Madison Federline’s Father Reportedly Cheated on Her Mother While She Was Pregnant 

The affair between Kevin and Britney Spears began while Jackson was carrying their second child, which spelled the end of what they shared. Kori was only two when the scandal between her father and Britney Spears happened. Spears had been seeing her father even though he was still married to Jackson. 

In a statement directed to Spears, Jackson said: “How do you call yourself a human knowing that you put somebody else through that pain?” The actress believes Spears entered his life before their split. She called it breaking up a family. 

Jackson and Kevin’s relationship ended as a result, but she has claimed they remain friends. She did not ask Kevin to pay child support, saying she was an “independent woman.” However, after their split, Jackson hoped he would spend much time with their kids. 

Kevin also stepped up. Although he did not have to pay child support, he was responsible for their private school fees. 

Kori’s Father’s Marriage to Britney Spears Did Not Last

Kevin and Spears met in 2004 and dated for three months before they announced their engagement in July 2004. They tied the knot in September in Studio City, California; however, the union was not meant to last. 

They stayed married for two years and welcomed two boys before going their separate ways. Spears filed for divorce in November 2006, and by March 2007, the process was finalized. 

Kori Madison Federline Has Many Siblings 

Kori Madison Federline has a brother named Kaleb Michael. He was born on July 20, 2004, to Kevin and Jackson and was their last child before their split. 

Kori also has two older siblings, products of her mom’s previous relationship, and five younger siblings from her dad, including Kaleb, Britney’s sons, and two other kids Kevin shares with his current wife, Victoria Prince. 

As mentioned, Jackson keeps Kori and her brothers away from the spotlight as much as possible. She hardly ever posts about them but is known to turn up at some events with them in tow. The same goes for Kevin. 

Kori’s Parents Excel at Co-Parenting 

Even though Kevin and Jackson’s relationship ended sourly, they were able to work past that to be good parents to the kids they share. A few years after their split, Jackson admitted that Kevin was a great father. Jackson called the former dancer the love of Kori’s life and claimed she got excited when she hears her dad’s voice.

Jackson said: “Everything he does, as far as his career, and everything like that, he does it for his kids.” Kori has a good relationship with her father despite how things ended between him and her mother. She even got along with Britney Spears while they were together.

Bella Hadid Looks Studiously Sexy in Button-Down and Glasses

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 1, 2024

For those looking to revamp their business-casual closets in the new year, look no further than Bella Hadid, whose street style is back and better than ever.

Hadid has been stepping out and working her off-duty model magic all over New York City in back-to-back bombshell outfits. In her latest, she embraced sexy librarian-core—proving even a button-down shirt can make a powerful statement.

Gotham

The model made the NoHo streets her runway on Friday night, as she headed to Zero Bond with a friend. She gave a corporate shirt some sultry flair, wearing a slim-fit white-and-gray striped button-down unbuttoned, creating a plunging neckline. Overtop, she donned a black leather jacket that featured ¾ sleeves, paired with matching loose-fitting slacks and square-toe riding boots. She accented the look with another chic, smart touch in the form of rectangle-framed green tortoise-shell eyeglasses, along with silver hoop earrings, a couple statement rings, and a dainty gold watch. For glam, Hadid opted for a glowy, bronzy look with glossy pink lips and pulled her hair back into a sleek high bun.

Gotham

A day later, Hadid hit the shops for some last-minute Christmas shopping. She played up the preppiness for the outing, trading in her edgy leather jacket for a sophisticated long brown plaid coat. She wore the winter layer over a crisp white button down, which she styled unbuttoned to reveal a matching undershirt. Hadid paired the pieces with straight-leg, dark wash jeans and the same black boots, suitable for store-hopping. She accessorized the outfit with playful accents, including a yellow-and-blue Prada bag, a large gold locket necklace, and red aviator glasses.

Ignat/Bauer-Griffin

Hadid was all smiles as she perused store shelves, and even appeared to make a new friend in the process.

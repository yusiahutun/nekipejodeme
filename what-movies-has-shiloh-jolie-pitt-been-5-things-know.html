Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, March 30, 2024

An appearance in The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

Back in 2008, when Shiloh was a mere two years old, she made her first on screen appearance in The Curious Case of Benjamin Button alongside dad Brad.

Shiloh played the role of the protagonist, Caroline, as a baby in an uncredited role.

And Brad wasn’t her only A-list co-star. The movie also featured Julia Ormond, Cate Blanchett and Tilda Swinton.

Kung Fu Panda 3’s voice role

Shiloh eventually bagged her first credited role in 2016’s Kung Fu Panda 3 as the voice of Shuai Shuai, according to IMDB.

Of course, mum Angelina had a big role in the film, playing the voice of Tigress. Also playing voice roles were the likes of Jack Black, Kate Hudson, Seth Rogen, Lucy Liu and Jackie Chan.

Angelina opened up about Shiloh and her siblings’ involvement in the movie to Entertainment Tonight. “They were kind of shy,” she revealed. “They don’t really want to be actors, but I didn’t want them to miss the opportunity.” She added, “They came in, and they had a lot of fun with it.”

She was offered a role in Maleficent

According to Hola magazine, Shiloh was offered a role alongside Angelina in 2014’s smash hit Maleficent, but turned it down.

The then-six year old apparently decided against playing Angelina’s on-screen daughter. Instead, youngest daughter Vivienne appeared in the film.

Dancing her way to the top

Shiloh has shot up in the height department during her teenage years. And it seems she might be putting those new long legs to use.

Throughout the pandemic, Shiloh has been attending dance classes at a studio in Los Angeles, and really seems to be taking it seriously.

In rehearsal videos online, Shiloh can be seen practising modern choreography to the song Good Ones by British singer Charli XCX. Later, she can be seen breaking it down to Get Ur Freak On by Missy Elliot. And she’s clearly got some impressive moves!

Red carpet ready

In October, Shiloh proved she was a red carpet superstar when she made headlines during Angelina’s Eternals press tour.

In the past, Shiloh generated quite the fashion buzz with her androgynous, fearless chic style at film premieres. She was always encouraged by her parents to dress the way she wanted. As a child, she was most often seen donning casual separates – polo shirts, jackets, blazers, shorts, dark trousers, jeans and oversized tracksuits. Those days seem to be over as she turned 15 this year.

Will 2022 see Shiloh getting even more involved in the entertainment industry? After her transformation in 2021, nothing would surprise us.

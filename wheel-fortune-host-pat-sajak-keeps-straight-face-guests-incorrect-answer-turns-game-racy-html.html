Wheel of Fortune host Pat Sajak keeps straight face as guest's incorrect answer turns game show racy

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, May 25, 2024

Wheel of Fortune host Pat Sajak struggled to keep a straight face when a contestant's guess took things down a racy road during a recent episode.

The 71-year-old had to break the news that Melanie had an incorrect answer as professionally as he could while they filmed the family show.

When she asked to complete the phrase on the board, the woman went for the letter 'G' to spell out 'Bridal & gold shower' in the Thursday evening episode.

A tickled contestant named Jenn swooped in and added $1,000 to her prize money after Melanie guessed the incorrect answer with a sexual connotation on Wheel of Fortune

Melanie (right) was playing the Same Name game which pairs phrases that have the same word in common

The latter part of the answer prompted fellow contestants to make the connection to a golden shower.

While a bridal shower is usually and innocent gathering to present a bride-to-be with gifts and the correct answer - a cold shower - simply refers to the temperature of water in a literal shower.

A tickled contestant named Jenn swooped in and added $1,000 to her prize money as she emphasized Melanie's mistake saying: 'I'd like to solve, 'Bridal and COLD shower'.'

And she couldn't help but cackle more as the show closed out to a commercial break.

Sajak and presenter Vanna White however couldn't be as obvious about their humor as Melanie – who has $2,000 in the bank at the time - seemed oblivious to her faux pas.

Another contestant named David kept a cheeky grin on his face but refrained from chuckling.

Vanna White (pictured left) also had to hold in any laughter as she presented at the board

Melanie was playing the Same Name game which pairs phrases that have the same word in common.

It's the type of thing that would make more conservative audience members blush and Sajak held in his thoughts as he reassured all three panellists they did well.

Thankfully the camera did not show his face at first but it was obvious he was holding in laughter in between pauses and a closed smile as they panned back to him.

He could be heard choking on his words as he said: 'We'll be back with more Wheel right after this.'

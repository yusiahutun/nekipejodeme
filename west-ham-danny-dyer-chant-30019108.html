West Ham stars sing crude song about Danny Dyer's daughter but he finds it 'quite sweet'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 23, 2024

West Ham's stars were singing Jarrod Bowen's X-rated chant after they progressed to the Europa Conference League final.

The Hammers beat AZ Alkmaar 1-0 on the night and 3-1 on aggregate to set up a showdown with Fiorentina in Prague next month. After distressing scenes at full-time which saw their fans and players fighting off the Dutch ultras, they were able to celebrate properly in the changing room.

Speaking about the post-match scenes, captain Declan Rice revealed to BT Sport he and his team-mate were signing multiple chants - one of which being "Bowen's on fire, and he's "s****ing Dani Dyer".

"We was actually singing the er, we was singing 'Bowen's on fire' in there to be fair," Rice sheepishly admitted as he burst out laughing with pundits and ex-West Ham stars Joe Cole and Carlton Cole.

"Jarrod was loving it. We was singing 'West Ham are massive', you know just enjoying the moment. Like I said, this is my first time I've been able to enjoy this, like this moment in a West Ham shirt."

Footage then emerged of Rice filming the squad and staff belting out the chant, which goes to the tune of hit song "Freed From Desire".

Hammers fans quickly came up with the crude lyrics after it emerged in later 2021 that Bowen was dating former Love Island winner Dani, who was reportedly embarrassed after first hearing it.

It was feared it wouldn't go down well with her legendary actor dad and vehement West Ham fan, Danny Dyer - but he's since come out and described it as "quite sweet" having joined in with it himself.

Appearing on The Jonathan Ross Show back in February, he said: "There is a chant, which I think is quite sweet.

"When he scores — and he does score a lot — [they chant]: ‘Bowen’s on fire and he’s s****ing Dani Dyer!’. I’ve been over there when they’re singing it, jumping around. Is that wrong?”

Neither of the Dyer's were in attendance in Alkmaar, with Dani currently expecting twins with Bowen.

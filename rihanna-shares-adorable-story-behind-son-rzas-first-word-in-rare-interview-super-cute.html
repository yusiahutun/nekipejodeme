Rihanna shares adorable story behind son RZA's first word in rare interview - 'Super cute'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 11, 2024

Rhi Rhi is opening up like never before in a stripped-back interview, and she has a whole new look to go with her ‘confessions’. From RZA’s first words to how she really feels next to ASAP, Rihanna spills all.

Unfortunately, the music star doesn’t mention anything about the new album that we’re all so desperately waiting for, but her new look is so mesmerizing, that we’ve pretty much forgotten all about that…

Rihanna reveals adorable story behind RZA’s first word

Although the Pon de Replay singer can’t believe she’s a mom of two, she is, and she’s revealed her eldest son RZA’s first word.

The one-year-old’s first word was “Hey,” the star told Interview Magazine. “I used to try to get his attention all the time, and I would say, ‘Hey, hey, hey,'” she explained.

“And one day he said it back to me in the same melody and I kept singing it and he kept following it over and over again. Super cute.”

The singer has two sons with her partner, A$AP Rocky. Their youngest, Riot Rose, was born in August 2023, and the couple announced his arrival with the most adorable family pic.

Rihanna and A$AP are probably one of, if not THE most fashionable couple around, however, the mom revealed she ‘feels like a bum’ around him. ” Isn’t he the best? I be feeling bummy as s**t next to this man. I feel like, goddamn, I look like his assistant,” she told the publication.

“I’m getting on a plane. We should be in sweats. He wants to be in a full Bottega suit. I’m like, ‘Why you got to do that to me?'”

The star takes on a ‘new look’ for the shoot

To go along with her confessions, the 36-year-old took part in a number of photoshoots, snapped by Nadia Lee Cohen.

In one of the looks, the star could be seen with her iconic short black pixie gelled back, with silver grills similar to the ones Kanye West debuted a few months ago.

Rihanna was a vision in the next look, in a white shirt and black trousers, with white headgear resembling a nun.

The looks are currently going viral on X, formerly known as Twitter, and fans are obsessed. “Oh this is phenomenal,” penned one.

“Rihanna the woman that you are ??!! NO ONE can do that. No one!!” exclaimed another.

A third screamed: “Mother got her superpowers back!!!!”

She sure did!

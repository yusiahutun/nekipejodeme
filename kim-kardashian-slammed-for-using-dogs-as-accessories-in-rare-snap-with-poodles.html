Kim Kardashian slammed for using dogs as accessories in rare snap with poodles Sat 2 Marc

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, April 3, 2024

Kim Kardashian posing with dogs as ‘accessories’ is such a rare occurrence that fans are convinced she “doesn’t like dogs”. Even her sister Khloe Kardashian isn’t convinced by the random poodles photo.

Describing the poodles as “the best in show,” Kim Kardashian smiled while holding the leashes of two white dogs. Although she owns three Pomeranians, The Kardashians star isn’t convincing anybody.

Kim Kardashian poses with dogs

Kim Kardashian held the leash of two dogs, who are both poodles, in her most recent snap on Instagram. She smiled while walking down an elegant hallway, while she donned a tight black jumpsuit.

Her sister, Khloe Kardashian, simply commented “ha” in the photo comments, which Kim captioned, “Best in show.” One fan wrote, “Kim don’t even like dogs,” while another said, “Kim doesn’t give “dog friendly””.

“😂😂😂 She hates dogs!!!!! Anything for a picture,” penned an convinced fan. Kourtney Kardashian once stated Kim doesn’t like the pet animals after taking Kourt’s dog Honey home once.

Poodles ‘used as accessories’

Fans are slamming Kim for using dogs as “fashion accessories” and believe she simply held the poodles for the picture, with one writing, “I bet she didn’t even walk them for a few mins.”

Ever since Kourtney claimed Kim hates dogs, The Kardashians fans have been convinced she only poses with dogs as accessories for social media, after Kim said her dog Sushi barked too much.

She’s got three dogs of her own

Kim has three Pomeranians, Sushi, Soba and Sake, of her own. At one point, the Kardashian pets went missing but were later found — in the garage, who they have had for years.

The busy mom adopted the first four-legged animal of their three pets for daughter North West in 2017 when she was still wed to rapper Kanye West, who she picked up from a litter with her sister, Kourtney.

Later, in September 2019, the SKIMS founder announced that she had added two more mini Pomeranian pups to their home: one black and the other white. And of course, North loves them!

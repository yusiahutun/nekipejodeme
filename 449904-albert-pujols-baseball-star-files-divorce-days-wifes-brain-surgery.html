Albert Pujols: Baseball Star Files for Divorce Days after Wife's Brain Surgery

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, April 21, 2024
  • Celebrated baseball player Albert Pujols has announced he is divorcing his wife after 22 years of marriage, having tied the knot in 2000
  • The separation news comes barely days after his wife Deidre underwent successful brain surgery to remove a tumour
  • The two had five children together and have vowed to remain committed to raising the children in a loving and safe environment

Baseball star Albert Pujols has announced he and his wife Deidre are going separate ways after 22 years of marriage.

Pujols made the announcement on Monday, April 4, days after his wife underwent successful surgery to remove a brain tumour.

Making the announcement in a statement that was released by his agent, Dan Lozano, Pujols said the decision to divorce his wife was not made overnight.

“I’ve been asked a lot of questions over the past few days regarding what’s been going on at home and sadly...I have made the decision to file for divorce from my wife, Deidre,” he said.

He further noted that he realised this was not the most suitable time to file for divorce with Opening Day approaching and other family events that have recently taken place.

"These situations are never easy and isn’t something that just happened overnight," he said.

Raising five children together

He and Deidre had been married since 2000, and according to him, as a devout Christian, this was an outcome that he never wanted to see happen.

“For many long days and nights, I prayed, asking the Lord for His guidance.”

Pujols concluded his statement by noting that he and Deidre will remain committed to raising their five children in a loving and safe environment.

“I am thankful for the five beautiful children that we brought into this world and remain committed to raising them in a loving and safe environment," he said.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce joined by Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper on 'romantic getaway'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 18, 2024

It’s happened! Two of our favorite couples have gone on a double date. According to Travis Kelce’s mom Donna, he and Taylor Swift went on a couple trip with Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper. We can’t cope!

Last year, we gained many celebrity couples. From Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift to Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper and Selena Gomez and Benny Blanco, we had too much cuteness to handle. Fans were waiting and wondering when the members of the girl gang would be stepping out for double dates, and it looks like Taylor and Gigi have done it, although they kept it on the down low.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce took ‘couple’s trip’ with Gigi and Bradley

Trust Donna Kelce to spill the tea we all want (and need) to know. Following the release of Swift’s new album, The Tortured Poets Department, it’s said the couple celebrated with a trip away with Gigi and her new boyfriend, Bradley Cooper.

At the QVC’s Age of Possibility summit in Las Vegas, on April 24, Donna revealed her son had sent her a picture from his from the vacation town of Carmel-by-the-Sea, California, where he and Swift were with Hadid and Cooper, according to PEOPLE.

During their time at the beach, the Kansas City Chiefs star realized the Hangover actor would be at the same event as his mom. The Hollywood star turned up with his food truck at the event, telling Donna, “Travis told me you were going to be here.”

Unfortunately, Trav couldn’t join them at the event, as his mom revealed he was busy finishing production on Are You Smarter Than A Celebrity? Which he’s set to be hosting.

Bradley and Gigi have been linked since October, and although they’ve been spotted kissing in public, neither have publically confirmed the relationship.

Insider Taylor and Gigi’s friendship

The music star and model’s relationship goes as far back as 2014, spotted hanging at the Vanity Fair Oscar Party, and their friendship has been going strong ever since. In fact, the two are so close that they even share an ex, Joe Jonas.

In 2015, Gigi joined a bunch of celebrities for Tay’s Bad Blood music video, which is still iconic to this day.

It was reported during the start of Brad and Gigi’s relationship, Swift let them use her Rhode Island home, so they could enjoy some time away from the spotlight.

“Even though Gigi and Bradley have their own properties in New York, they wanted somewhere more private to spend time getting to know each other,” a source told The Mirror at the time.

“Taylor was only too happy to help. She said the door to her home is always open for them to use.”

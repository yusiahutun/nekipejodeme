iCarly Anniversary Trivia Sweepstakes Official Rules

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, July 14, 2024

1. Acceptance of Rules. By entering the iCarly Anniversary Trivia Sweepstakes (the "Sweepstakes"), via the @ParamountPlus page/account and the @iCarly page on the Twitter Service (as defined below) accessible at the domain "twitter.com/ParamountPlus" and “twitter.com/icarly” (the "Twitter Account(s)"), you hereby accept and agree to these official rules (the "Official Rules") of this Sweepstakes, and the decisions of the Administrator in connection with this Sweepstakes, whose decisions are final. 

No purchase, entry fee or use of wireless device is necessary to enter or win. If you are a wireless service subscriber and have a smartphone, tablet and/or other wireless device that allows you to access the Internet, you may enter the Sweepstakes via any of those devices during the Promotion Period, as defined below. If you decide to participate via any such wireless device, you may be charged message and/or data rates in accordance with the Terms and Conditions of your Service Agreement with your wireless carrier. 

2. Eligibility. This Sweepstakes is offered and open to persons who are: (a) Registered Users (as defined below); (b) followers of the Twitter Account(s); (c) legal residents of the fifty (50) United States and D.C.; and (d) at least 18 years old or age of majority, whichever is older in their state of residence, at the date of entry in this Sweepstakes. For purposes of these Official Rules, "Registered User(s)" means a person that has successfully completed the registration form and has obtained a User Identification ("ID") and associated password ("Password") on the social website accessible at the domain "Twitter.com" (the "Twitter Service"). There is no fee associated with becoming a Twitter Service registrant and/or a follower of the Twitter Account. Employees, directors, and the immediate family members of the Sweepstakes Entities, and their parents, affiliated and subsidiary companies are not eligible to participate in this Sweepstakes. For purpose of the foregoing "immediate family" means parents, spouses, children, siblings or any members of the same households of such employees and directors. This Sweepstakes is void in Puerto Rico, all U.S. territories and possessions, and where prohibited by law. Eligibility will be determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion. All entries must comply with Twitter’s Terms of Service and rules available at the Twitter Service.  

3. Sweepstakes Period. The Sweepstakes begins on September 8, 2021 at 9:00 a.m. Pacific Time ("PT") and ends at 11:59:59 p.m. PT on September 8, 2021 (the "Sweepstakes Period"). Sponsor’s computer is the official time-keeping device for this Sweepstakes. All entries must be received by 11:59:59 p.m. PT on September 8, 2021.

4. Entering the Sweepstakes. In order to enter this Sweepstakes, participants need to register to the Twitter Service and become followers of these Twitter Accounts (@ParamountPlus and @iCarly), if not already. On or about, every half hour from 9:00 a.m. PT to 2:00 PM PT, Administrator will post one (1) iCarly trivia question identified as part of the Sweepstakes on one or both of the Twitter Accounts. Users who post an answer to such trivia question(s) with the hashtag #iCarlySweepsduring the Sweepstakes Period, via the Twitter Account(s) and/or the users' personal Twitter Service account, will receive an entry into the Sweepstakes. There is a limit of one entry per person per post during the Sweepstakes Period. Entries must not include personal information or profane or obscene language as solely determined by Sponsor. Any attempt to obtain entries in excess of the limit on entries as set forth in these Official Rules, including by using or creating multiple Twitter accounts; by using multiple and/or different identities, forms, or registrations; or by any other methods, will void all of the relevant entrant’s entries, and that entrant may be disqualified at Sponsor’s discretion. Multiple entrants are not permitted to share the same Twitter account.

If a Winner chooses to renew his/her subscription, such Winner will be automatically charged $9.99/monthly plus applicable taxes until Winner elects to cancel. To avoid auto-renew charges once a Winner chooses to renew, such Winner must cancel at least twenty-four (24) hours before the billing period ends at Paramount+. Monthly subscription rate cited above reflects current rate and is subject to change. Devices not included. One-time use coupon code can only be redeemed at the Paramount+ website located at . Coupon code cannot be redeemed via the Paramount+ mobile or TV app, but Winners will be able to access their accounts on the app once their subscription has been activated online. When setting up a new subscription account, each Winner must provide all required information including, but not limited to, full name, e-mail address, zip code, date of birth and gender. If a Winner is already a paying subscriber to Paramount+ (formerly CBS All Access), they must first cancel their existing subscription in order to set up a new account for the 12-month free subscription. If Winner is already a paying subscriber to Paramount+ (or CBS All Access) via a third party partner (e.g., Roku), they may have to wait until the end of the billing cycle before cancelling their account via the third party partner provider and setting up a new free account at .

Karolina Kurkova says her son Tobin on the 'road to recovery' following surgery for a broken ankle

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 30, 2024

Karolína Kurková is happy to report that her 12-year-old son Tobin is on the 'road to recovery' after undergoing surgery for a broken ankle.

Earlier this week, the model and actress, 38, shared on Instagram that Tobin broke his ankle joint in three places during football practice, resulting in him wearing a cast and using crutches.

'There's nothing like the emotions a mother feels when her child gets hurt... especially when you see them afraid and in pain and can't do nothing to take it away even though you would in a second,' Kurková confessed in an Instagram post. 'Thankfully, we are already on the road to recovery.'

Health update: Karolína Kurková, 38, took to Instagram to report that her 12-year-old son Tobin is on the mend after undergoing surgery for a broken ankle

In the cover photo, Tobin can be seen sleeping in his hospital bed in Miami, Florida soon after the operation that ended up requiring screws to help keep the ankle breaks in place.

The concerned mom went on to thank 'Dr. Steinlauf and everyone at UM hospital @umiamihealth' for their expertise care, and then asked fans and followers to share any words of support

'We are welcoming all the healing energy as he gets better with the love and support of his favorite people." 

The Instagram post also showed Tobin snuggling up with his one-year-old sister LunaGrace, with his cast in clear view, and another image of him standing up with the help of crutches alongside younger brother Noah, six.

The Děčín, Czechoslovakia, native and her husband Archie Drury had just celebrated their 13th wedding anniversary in early September, about a month before their son's mishap on the football field.

Drury, 52, is an actor and producer, who's best known to many fans for his roles on NCIS (2003), Swing (2003) and Voices Of Iraq (2004), among many others.

Kurková got her big break into modeling when she appeared on the cover of the American edition of Vogue in September 1999 when she was just 15. 

She would make the cover again in February 2001 after moving to New York City when she was  17, and then go on to become among the top earning models in the 2000s.

Going strong: Kurková and husband Archie Drury, 52, celebrated their 13th wedding anniversary in early September, just a few weeks before their son's mishap on the football field

Many fans know her as a longtime Victoria's Secret Angel and for other fashion campaigns that include some of the world's biggest names in fashion like Chanel, Tommy Hilfiger, Hugo Boss, Versace, Louis Vuitton, Jean Paul Gaultier and Christian Dior, among many others. 

After living in New York City for about 12 years, the couple found the 'family-friendly and outdoorsy place' they were looking for and moved to Miami's Fisher Island, according to Stars Offline.

Just last year, the catwalk queen revealed how their family dynamic has changed since the arrival of their daughter in April 2021.

'It's definitely a different energy,' the proud momma confessed to People, adding, 'For me it's very beautiful and special because she's like me. ... We're alike. There is that common ground that we have, not something that you can even describe.'

Sofia Coppola and Barrie release cosy cashmere collection

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 17, 2024

Sofia Coppola has long been admired for her impeccable taste, and the director's latest project will let you have a piece of that for yourself. Coppola has collaborated with cashmere label Barrie on a collection which is inspired by her own personal style, and makes the ideal wardrobe building blocks for work, travel and leisure.

"The Barrie pieces I’ve bought over the years, I wear over and over again and they never look worn or pilly, so they really last and they are timeless," Coppola said of why she wanted to collaborate with the Scottish label. "I love the classic cardigans, the weight, the feeling, and the colours. I wear those a lot and the men’s crew neck sweaters."

"I love the idea that they’re meant to last a lifetime and can be brought back to be repaired if ever needed. Also, wearing Barrie supports artisans and this mill in Scotland that was started in 1903 and keeps this tradition alive."

The director has worked alongside Barrie's creative director Augustin Dol-Maillot on the 17-piece collection which features block-coloured knitwear, a chic travel set, a military-style jacket and a wool coat.

Coppola describes this as "a small travel wardrobe" – it is what she would want to bring on a trip. These are pieces that are versatile, work with one another, but are also super comfortable and casual. "But you still look put together," she says.

Barrie

"You can wear the knit pants on the plane and then dress them up a little with the velvet knit cardigan for dinner in the hotel bar. That’s how I like to wear Barrie. I think the jumpsuit could be worn in almost any situation."

With the rise of quiet luxury, this collection will no doubt be a sell-out success – and these pieces make great versatile additions to any wardrobe, so look no further if you're stuck for Christmas presents this year.

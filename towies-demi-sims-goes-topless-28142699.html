TOWIE's Demi Sims goes topless as she shows off new ink in huge back tattoo

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, April 15, 2024

Demi Sims sent temperatures soaring as she proudly displayed her new back tattoo on Instagram over the weekend.

The influencer, 26, is currently filming her new reality show House of Sims with her sisters Chloe and Frankie.

But on Sunday (October 2), The Only Way Is Essex star took a break from filming to finish off her large back tattoo with her artist.

Her artwork focuses around the theme of love with one quote reading ‘Your heart is safe here’ alongside a beautiful portrait of two people kissing.

To show off the full extent of her artwork, the blonde bombshell went topless and wore a pair of high waisted white pants which were visible underneath a pair of black velvet tracksuit pants.

She complimented the look with a casual black beanie which she pulled over her luscious long locks, with the remainder of her hair pulled to one side to show off the full extent of her new tattoo.

The starlet showcased her gorgeous golden tan as she posed up a storm for her adoring fans online, as she looked off to one side.

In the centre of her back was a portrait framed with a large square box with scripture on the outside which read: “Show me the most damaged parts of your soul and I will show you it how it still shines like gold’.

She also had the Spanish word for love – Amor – inked on the top of her back, alongside another quote which read: “I want the parts of you that you refuse to give’.

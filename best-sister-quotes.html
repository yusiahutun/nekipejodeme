100 Best Sister Quotes that Celebrate Your Special Relationship

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 28, 2024

"Is solace anywhere more comforting than that in the arms of a sister?" —Alice Walker

“You may be as different as the sun and the moon, but the same blood flows through both your hearts. You need her, as she needs you...” ― George R.R. Martin

“You know full well as I do the value of sisters’ affections; there is nothing like it in this world.” —Charlotte Bronte

“Sisters, as you know, also have a unique relationship. This is the person who has known you your entire life, who should love you and stand by you no matter what, and yet it's your sister who knows exactly where to drive the knife to hurt you the most.” ―Lisa See

“But the love of sisters needs no words. It does not depend on memories, or mementos, or proof. It runs as deep as a heartbeat. It is as ever present as a pulse.” ―Lisa Wingate

“Nobody fights you like your own sister; nobody else knows the most vulnerable parts of you and will aim for them without mercy.” —Jojo Moyes

“I do not see as well without her. I do not hear as well without her. I do not feel as well without her. I would be better off without a hand or a leg than without my sister.” —Erin Morgenstern

“You can kid the world, but not your sister.” ― Charlotte Gray

“Unconditional love: the definition of sister.”—Alicia Cook

“Sisters share a bond unlike any other—thornier, but also tender, full of possibility.”—Joy McCullough

“…Among the merits and the happiness of Elinor and Marianne, let it not be ranked as the least considerable, that though sisters, and living almost within sight of each other, they could live without disagreement between themselves...”—Jane Austen

“May and I are sisters. We'll always fight, but we'll always make up as well. That's what sisters do: we argue, we point out each other's frailties, mistakes, and bad judgment, we flash the insecurities we've had since childhood, and then we come back together. Until the next time..”
―Lisa See

“I have always thought that sisters have a way of communicating, no matter what keeps them apart.” ―Kelly S. Thompson

“There is no place for secrets in sisterhood.” —Erin Forbes

“Acquaintances were always on their best behavior, but sisters loved each other enough to say anything.” —Lauren Weisberger

“She is the person I ran to when I got my period; the one who helped me knit back together my first broken heart; the hand I would reach for in the middle of the night when I could no longer remember which side our father parted his hair on, or what it sounded like when our mother laughed. No matter what she is now, before all that, she was my built-in best friend.” ―Jodi Picoult

“Sisters may walk on different paths, but they're never farther than a heartstring.” ―Julia Ash

“We will always be sisters. Our differences may never go away, but neither, for me, will our song.” ―Nancy Kelton

“Sister. She is your mirror, shining back at you with a world of possibilities. She is your witness, who sees you at your worst and best, and loves you anyway. She is your partner in crime, your midnight companion, someone who knows when you are smiling, even in the dark.” ―Barbara Alpert

“My sisters. My blood. My skin. What a gruesome bond we shared.” ―Krystal Sutherland

“A sister is a dearest friend, a closest enemy, and an angel at the time of need.” ―Debasish Mridha

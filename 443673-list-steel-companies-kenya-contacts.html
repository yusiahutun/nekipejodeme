List of all steel companies in Kenya and their contacts

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 25, 2024

There are several Steel companies in Kenya. There are spread across major towns such as Eldoret, Mombasa, Nairobi, and Kisumu. They employ several people in their various departments and significantly contribute to the country's growth.

Kenya is a fast-growing country, thanks to its diversified industrial sector. Over the years, the government has welcomed investors in the country, a move that has seen many companies crop up.

Steel companies in Kenya

Does Kenya manufacture steel? The iron and steel industry forms about 13 percent of Kenya's manufacturing sector. However, the local steel industry is heavily dependent on imported raw materials, as there are no local sources. Here are the steel companies in Kenya.

Tononoka Steel

Tononoka Steels Limited is located in Nairobi and works in steel products business activities. It began in 1980 with the formation of Tononoka Hardware Limited. The hardware became a significant supplier of steel products in the Kenyan market.

Tarmal Steel started as a hardware shop in Mombasa. Over the years, it has now grown into a company with an annual capacity of more than a hundred thousand metric tons. Today, it is one of the leading manufacturers of steel and allied products in East Africa.

Bachu Industries Limited

Bhachu Industries Limited was established as a family business in 1976. In 1997, the company diversified into the manufacturing of steel products. It was marked by the incorporation of its sister company Accurate Steel Mills focusing on the manufacturing of various metallic products.

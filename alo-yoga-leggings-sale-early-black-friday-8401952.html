I Own at Least 20 Pairs of Leggings From Alo Yoga and Its on Sale

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 23, 2024

One thing Jennifer Garner, Kylie Jenner, and I have in common? We all wear Alo Yoga leggings on repeat. I probably own 20 pairs from the celebrity-loved brand, including best-selling styles like the Airlift, Airbrush, and Moto leggings. The stylish and stretchy styles are so comfy, I wear them almost daily everywhere from running errands to the gym — and even out to dinner. 

The best part? Right now, the cool-girl yoga brand is having an under-the-radar early Black Friday sale, including some of its most popular legging styles donned by the stars. In fact, Alo has already marked down some of my own favorite pieces ahead of the savings event. From the buttery soft styles that supermodels always wear to the cool moto design that is a must for any closet, below are six Alo Yoga leggings you can snag on sale before Black Friday:

7/8 High-Waist Airlift legging

There’s a lot of hype surrounding Alo Yoga’s Airlift fabric. Once I slipped on a pair of the silky and sleek leggings, I became an instant convert and now own them in multiple styles (as do Kendall Jenner and Hailey Bieber, FYI). These are seriously the most flattering leggings I own, as the double-layer waistband and high-compression material holds you in (without squeezing your guts out) while smoothing and sculpting your body. They are designed for vigorous sweat sessions, but like Rosie Huntington-Whiteley demonstrated, they are hot enough to pair with an oversized YSL bag and designer kicks and call it a day. I suggest ordering these in every color and wearing them daily, especially while a few shades are 20 percent off. 

7/8 High Waist Airbrush Legging

Alo’s other uber-popular fabric? Airbrush. Loads of celebrities sweat in these ultra-soft leggings, including Olivia Wilde and most recently, Sydney Sweeney. The four-way stretch, medium compression fabric is moisture-wicking and odor resistant for sweaty workouts. Like the Airlift, they also smooth and sculpt your curves but with a little less intensity. If you love Lululemon’s Align leggings, you should definitely order a pair in one of these bright colors right now while they are $20 off. 

Moto Legging

Alo’s uber-popular moto legging was all the rage a few years ago, with stars like Taylor Swift and Bella Hadid donning it. Designed to lift, sculpt, contour, and smooth your booty and curves, the ultra-flattering style is constructed out of the brand’s trademark Airbrush fabric and a glossy, sweat-wicking performance material so you can comfortably bust your butt during workouts. Stylish design details like mesh panels and moto, quilted stitching make them chic enough for the streets. Currently the versatile gravel hue is $48 off, a total steal.

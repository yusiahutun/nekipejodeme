How to keep your dog cool: 18 best heatwave products to keep your dog from overheating this summer,

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

There's no sugar coating it - it's HOT. And you're likely thinking about BBQ essentials, garden glow-up essentials and how you're going to get your outdoor space ready for summer socialising - but don't forget to add cooling dog products to your to-buy list too.

Our beloved pooches need to stay nice and chilled throughout the warmer months, and there are plenty of products to keep your dog cool. After all, they can suffer from heatstroke too. Here are some tips to keep your dogs safe during a heatwave.

How to keep your dog cool in the summer

1. Be a cool rider this summer

Everyone loves a summer road trip, pooches included, so prepare well for your journey to make sure everyone is cool and comfortable on the road.

Pre-cool your car ahead of a journey by setting the aircon to a consistent temperate and also make sure you have plenty of water to hydrate yourself and your dog when you take a pit stop.

Another hack to keep your furry travel companion cool in the car is having sunshades fitted on your windows or having a cooling mat for your dog to lie on. Of course, never leave your dog alone in the car when the temperate starts rising - even in the shade with windows open, the interior of your car can reach extreme temperatures.

It’s best to leave your pup at home if you’re travelling somewhere that isn’t dog-friendly

2. Chill out time

There are a few handy towel hacks too that will be sure to keep pups happy in the heat. Pop a couple of towels in the fridge or freezer to lie down on, or why not soak a towel in cold water for your dog to lie on, just make sure you don’t cover them with the towel as this could trap heat in!

Hanging a towel in front of a protected fan also helps blow cooler air towards your pooch and saves warm air from circulating in the room.

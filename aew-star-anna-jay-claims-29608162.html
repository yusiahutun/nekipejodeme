AEW star Anna Jay claims to 'like pain' because she has 'fat ass' and 'bad attitude'

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, June 6, 2024

The 24-year-old American, who is part of the Jericho Appreciation Society stable in All Elite Wrestling, was giving an impassioned promo about rival Julia Hart.

She returned to AEW television after a long injury lay-off, as a result of a vicious street fight earlier this year, last week when she attacked the House of Black's Hart at ringside.

UDSM online application for undergraduate and postgraduate 2020/2021

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, April 6, 2024

The University of Dar es Salaam (UDSM) is a leading tertiary education institution in Tanzania. It started in 1961 and has grown over the year to now, offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programmes. The institution periodically enrols via the UDSM online application portal.

UDSM courses are carefully crafted to enable you to rise to the top of your chosen profession. Below is everything you need to know about UDSM's second selection, courses, application procedure, and charges.

UDSM admission requirements

Undergraduate students must fulfil the following minimum requirements:

  • Have a Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE), which has passes in five subjects. Three of them should be at Credit Level before taking Advanced Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (ACSEE)
  • Two Principal Passes in the ACSEE examination. You should have a total of not less than five points for Arts and two for Science programmes from the cluster subjects.
  • If you have a diploma, then it should be related to the course you want to take. You need to have at least a Second Class, a Credit Level, or grade B from a collage registered by NACTE and approved by the UDSM Senate.
  • If your country has an 8-4-4 education system, you need to complete a year at the university level to be considered fit for admission.
  • You must have scored over 100 from the Mature Age Entry Examination (MAEE). The 100 should consist of at least a score of 50 marks in Paper 1 and 50 in Paper 2.
  • If you have completed A-Levels, you should apply through the Central Admission System.

    UDSM online application procedure

    Follow the steps below:

  • Make sure you have attained the minimum requirements: Check your course-specific requirements from UDSM advertisements.
  • Go to the UDSM application portal: Connect to the internet, open your browser, key in UDSM address, and press the Enter button.
  • Specify your application type: If you are applying for a degree, diploma, or certificate, choose Undergraduate. For masters, postgraduate diploma, and Ph.D., click on Postgraduate and tap on the Registration button.
  • Enter your personal information: At this stage, you will only need your first name, middle name, surname, and sex.
  • Provide your email address and phone number: Make sure they are valid.
  • Choose a password: It should include some character, numbers, and a mix of lowercase and uppercase letters.
  • Proof that you are not a robot: You can do so by typing in the blue character in the provided text box.

    8. Log in to the system: A link will be sent to the email you provided. Login to your inbox and click on it to sign in.

    9. Complete your profile: You need to specify your application category, name as it appears on your certificates, citizenship, disability if any, and gender.

    10. Pay application fee: Use the procedure for the same covered below.

    11. Enter your academic qualifications: Add your index number and year, then choose the level and click on "Add sitting.”

    12. Upload your certificates: They should be in a PDF format. Click on the Add button then choose the file from your local storage to upload it to the system.

    13. Select your course: For Undergraduate, enter the courses you wish to undertake in the provided text boxes.

    14. Enter your employment records: This step is only made for postgraduates who have work experience. Click on the Add button to complete the step.

    16. Submission and declaration: Answer the question asked and declare that all the information you have submitted is correct. Tap on the Submit button.

    You have now declared your candidature in the courses you chose. If you are among the UDSM selected applicants, the institution will inform you via your contact details. You can also head to their website to check for the list of enrolled students.

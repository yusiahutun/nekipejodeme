Lupita Nyong'ogets close to Joseph Quinn at the Oscars days after romantic trip with 'boyfriend'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, October 9, 2024

Lupita Nyong’o has Oscars fans confused after as she attended the ceremony with co-star Joseph Quinn amid rumors she’s dating Joshua Jackson.

Just days ago, the Black Panther star and Joshua packed on the PDA on holiday, and many fans thought we’d be getting the first official Red Carpet pics of them at this year’s Oscars. Unfortunately, it wasn’t meant to be, but Lupita Nyong’o and The Quiet Place movie co-star Joseph Quinn definitely weren’t staying quiet. One thing we know though, is that whoever she is with (or isn’t) Lupita is always going to serve a look, which is exactly what she did.

Lupita Nyong’o and Joseph Quinn put on a cozy display on the Oscars Red Carpet

Lupita looked stunning as she walked the awards show Red Carpet as she sparkled in a light blue jeweled-up gown with feather detail on the waist, hugging her toned figure.

It was no surprise she slayed, as she does with every look, however, what did come as a surprise to fans was the arm candy next to her.

The 41-year-old was seen just days ago packing on the PDA with rumored boyfriend, Dawson’s Creek star Joshua Jackson. The two didn’t seem to be hiding their romance from anyone, as they locked lips in Mexico.

Although Lupita Nyong’o and Stranger Things star Joseph Quinn weren’t locking lips on the Red Carpet, the chemistry was flowing as they held hands and laughed the night away, intensely looking into each other’s eyes.

This had fans on X, formerly known as Twitter scratching their heads, as one questioned: “Isn’t Lupita Nyong’o dating Joshua Jackson?”

But those who ship Lupita and Joshua, fear not. The ‘date night’ with Quinn was purely platonic, as their appearance comes ahead of their new film, The Quiet Place: Day One. 

The ‘couple’ also attended the after party together

The party didn’t stop there for the two, as they also attended the famous Vanity Fair Oscars party together, once again, posing for pics on the carpet.

Nyong’o had a change of outfit for the A-List celebrity bash, opting for the sequins once again, but this time went for more of a party vibe with a multi coloured low neck gown.

The party was attended by heaps of famous faces including Kim Kardashian and Kendall Jenner. Unfortunately, we didn’t get the hard-launch we were promised from Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper, but there’s always next year!

(Although we’re hoping we don’t have to wait that long!)

Meet The Voice Brayden Lape Parents, Kari Lape And Bryan Lape!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 18, 2024

Meet Kari and Bryan Lape, Brayden Lape’s parents. Learn about his parents, and his siblings in this article below.

Find out more as this article proceeds.

Who Are The Voice Star Brayden Lape Parents?

The Voice star Brayden Lape is born to his parents Kari and Bryan Lape. They have been very supportive of Kari competing on The Voice season 22.

Brayden performed “This Town” by Nial Horgan at The Voice auditions, but only one chair turned. But judge Blake Shelton’s chair turn was the one he needed the most. After that, he performed “Pretty Heart” by Parker McCollum for the battle round, “Mercey” by Brett Young for the knockout rounds, “Buy Dirt” by Jordan Davis and Luke Bryan for the top-16, “Come Over” by Kenny Chesney for the top-10, and “Homesick” by Kane Brown for the top-8.

According to the singer, “Homesick” was the perfect song choice for him that week. “This is a song I can kind of relate to, being out here all this time and missing home. This is a perfect song because I’m definitely missing my friends. Missing my family. I’m 16 and this is like, the longest I’ve been away from home,” he said at the time.

And the judges wholeheartedly concur. Coach Gwen Stefani remarked, “That chorus fit you like a glove.” “It is actually watching you blossom vocally and aesthetically in front of our eyes,” Blake continued. Brayden would likely grow to be way too huge, according to the judge, for them to ever be invited back to the show.

On The Voice, Brayden’s fellow competitors Parijita Bastola (Team Legend), Rowan Grace, Justin Aaron, Kim Cruse (Team Legend), Bryce Leatherwood, Kique (Team Gwen), Morgan Myles (Team Camila), Bodie, and Omar Jose Cardona all made it to the top 10. (Team Legend).

Meet Kari Lape, Brayden Lape Mother

Brayden Lape calls Kari Lape his mother. On Son’s Day, Kari posted on her Facebook, “Happy Sons day to my favorite guys! My heart bursts with JOY watching you do all the things!.”

  • Kari Lape Age

Born in September 1980, Kari Ann Lape is 42 years old. On 24 September, her husband wished her birthday via an IG post writing, “Happy Birthday to my awesome wife Kari Lape. The most supportive, loving, funny, crazy, biggest celebrator on TV wife I could ask for. Love you, Kari.”

  • Kari Lape Job

Kari Lape attended Columbia Central High School. She graduated a class of 1982. By profession, Kari is a teacher. She worked at Columbia Elementary School where she taught 2nd grade.

In addition, Kari is also a business owner of THREADS AND THEORIES LLC. Threads and Theories sell products such as sports apparel, earrings, hats, and more. According to the website, “Threads and Theories has a passion for creating comfy tees, leather patch hats, personalized gifts, and Inspired by Reese, unique wood earrings. We are a family-owned business that works hard to provide unique items shipped quickly to your door. All of our products are put together in Grass Lake, Michigan.”

You can also find her on Instagram (@threadsandtheories).

  • Is Kari Lape On Instagram?

Kari Lape only has a Facebook account (@kari.lape). But, no, she doesn’t have an IG account.

Meet Bryan Lape, Brayden Lape Father

Bryan Lape is very supportive of his son Brayden Lape pursuing a career in music. In fact, he is very proud that he is one of the Top 8 contestants in season 22 of The Voice. After Brayden entered the Top 8, Bryan posted on his Facebook, “Today is the day to vote again for Brayden. Going from 10 to 8 this week. This week will be tough to get through so all your votes are appreciated.”

In an interview with Z93, Brayden shared that he had thought he would follow in his father’s footsteps and play collegiate, and hopefully someday professional, football.

  • Bryan Lape Age

Bryan Lape was born in June 1976. He is 46 years old in 2022 and celebrates his birthday on 17 June.

  • Bryan Lape Job

Bryan Lape is working at Consumers Energy as Senior Supply Chain Lead. Before that, he worked at Ford Motor Company. He joined the company as Dock Supervisor in 2006. After four years in that role, he landed the role of Industrial Material Analyst, Synchronous Material Flow Coordinator.

Before joining Consumers Energy, he was working in the role of Material Flow Engineer/SMF Coordinator. Bryan also worked at Visteon Corporation as Production Scheduler from 2003 to 2005.

Bryan received an undergraduate degree in Chain Management from Western Michigan University in 2002. He also volunteered at Grass Lake Youth Basketball, Football, and Soccer as Youth Coach.

  • Is Bryan Lape On Instagram?

No, Bryan Lape is not on Instagram but you can find him on Facebook (@bryan.lape.1).

Related FAQs

  • Are Brayden Lape Parents Still Married?

Yes, Brayden Lape’s parents are still married. In May 2022, Bryan and Kari stayed married for 18 years. Kari wished her husband, “Celebrating 18 years of marriage to this guy. He is loyal and a complete gem to our family! I’ll think I’ll just spend forever with you.”

  • Where Do Brayden Lape Parents Reside?

Brayden Lape’s parents are currently residing in Grass Lake, Michigan.

  • How Many Kids Do Brayden Lape Parents Have?

Besides Brayden, the singer’s parents Bryan and Kari Lape also have other two children; Boyer and Reesie. Boyer is 8 years old in May 2022. Reesie, is in her teen, 14-year-old to be precise.

Kari wished, “Happy 14th Birthday to our fierce and loving daughter! You are the most beautiful daughter I could ever ask for. I loved you as my little Reesie and love you more with every year that passes. I pray that 14 is one of your best. May God bless you and watch over you. Being your mom is an absolute blessing! Proud of you Reesie K!”

On National Daughter’s Day, Kari wished his daughter, “Happy National daughters day to my greatest girl! I live for you Reesie! You are so strong, loving, and full of adventure. I am forever grateful to be your mom and watch you grow.”

Kari is currently in 8th grade and Boyer is in 3rd grade.

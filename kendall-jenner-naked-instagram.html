Kim Kardashian &amp; Kendall Jenner Post Revealing Instagram Photos

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 27, 2024
  • Both Kim Kardashian and Kendall Jenner have continued their family's long-held tradition of posting revealing Instagram snaps.
  • While Kim K posed in a skimpy swimsuit for her photo, supermodel Kendall stripped down completely.
  • Fashion photographer Mert Alas also posted a naked pic of Kendall, taken from another, even more revealing, angle.

Members of the Kardashian–Jenner clan regularly wear risqué outfits, and they often share super-revealing photos on Instagram, too.

Related Story

So it's completely unsurprising that both Kendall Jenner and Kim Kardashian have almost simultaneously taken to Instagram to share nearly naked shots, you know, to help get you in the weekend mood.

Kendall shared a photo of herself completely naked, with only her nipple censored. The pic appears to be part of a series by fashion photographer Mert Alas, who has shared similarly naked photos of One Direction member Liam Payne and iconic supermodel Kate Moss.

Of course, Kendall is renowned for being a fan of freeing the nipple and has often posed topless for magazines covers and fashion shoots.

Sister Kim shared an almost-naked shot of herself wearing a skimpy black bathing suit. She captioned the photo, "#FBF Take me back please #CostaRica," which is bound to make everyone crave a summer vacay.

Meanwhile, Kylie Jenner has been spending her week in Turks and Caicos on a Kylie Skin promotional vacation, which has meant a plethora of bikini snaps.

And she even got totally naked (apart from her floppy straw hat), because nothing screams "vacation mode" like posing without any clothes on.

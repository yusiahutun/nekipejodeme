Kenya Power internship and career opportunities

KPLC is one of the best companies whose internship opportunities can launch your career. But with so many recent graduates flooding the job market, the competition to get an internship or even a job is more cutthroat. Maybe you even know a friend who applied but got rejected. And the best part about KPLC internships and attachments is that they give equal opportunities to certificate, diploma and degree holders to get hands-on experience that will make you competent in the competitive job market.

Here is an easy to follow method on how to apply for either the KPLC internship or the industrial attachment through the KPLC careers portal.

KPLC Internship intake September/November

In partnership with the government Youth Initiative, KPLC has announced the 2018 internship opportunities. The program which is expected to start in September is for a diploma, certificate holders, and undergraduates. Successful candidates will be placed in the various KPLC departments where they will learn the best employability skills that will come in handy in the intensely competitive labor market.

But here is the catch:

You have to submit your application through the online KPLC careers portal, and most importantly ensure that you complete your KPLC internship application way before 31st July 2018.

Requirements

You might be wondering whether you are the right candidate for KPLC careers 2018. To be eligible for the Kenya Power attachment program, you must meet the following requirements:

  • You must be a Kenyan citizen
  • You must have an introduction letter from your learning institution
  • You should be continuing with your certificate, diploma or undergraduate studies from a recognized institution
  • You should be available full time for three months.

How to apply for KPLC 2018 internship

One of the best ways of landing your dream job is to know precisely what the KPLC job vacancies require. All the vacancies including internships requirements and how to apply are always given in the KPLC official website. To apply for the KPLC internship:

Then register with KPLC in their career portal. Apart from job opportunities and internships, KPLC also offers industrial attachments to help develop the young talent in Kenya.

What is the difference between industrial attachment and internship?

In Kenya, both internships and attachments are a crucial part of a studentâ€™s academics. They help a student gain work experience by applying theory to practice and gain both knowledge and skills that will enhance their market value. But in most cases, an industrial attachment is longer than an internship. For KPLC, the attachment program last for three months in which during this period the student will acquire skills and knowledge related to their career or field of study.

What are the requirements and how do you apply for the Kenya Power industrial attachment program?

Requirements

  • You must be a Kenyan citizen.
  • You should be available full time for the 3-month program duration.
  • You need to have with you an introduction letter from the college or your current higher learning institution.
  • You must be a continuing undergraduate, diploma or certificate student from a recognized institution.
  • You must submit your application before the stated deadline: 31st July 2018.

How to apply

Interested candidates, as well as those who meet the above requirements, should use the KPLC online job application portal to submit their applications. You can submit your application by first clicking on the Kenya power website.

Then under the Public Information tab located on the right-hand side of the page, you will get the tab Career opportunities. Click on it and apply for the position.

Here's the bottom line

Every year KPLC unveils any vacant job advert and this year is no different. It is up to the job seeker or interested party to frequently check their website for any available job vacancies in Kenya power to avoid missing the deadlines. To boost your chances of landing an interview, make sure you meet all the requirements stated in the Kenya power jobs advertisement and update your profile just in case you acquired any relevant skills recently.

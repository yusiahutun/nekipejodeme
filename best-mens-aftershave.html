10 best aftershaves for men 2024: The most loved fragrances from Dior, Chanel, Tom Ford &amp; more

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, March 3, 2024

Treat him to a new signature scent...

    A new aftershave is a birthday present almost any man would love to unwrap, so if you've found yourself looking for a gift for him, you're in the right place.

    A good scent can boost confidence, evoke memories, and give his bathroom shelf a serious upgrade, and if you're unsure of which one to choose, we've done the hard work for you.

    From the bestselling Dior Sauvage to Chanel's new icon Bleu de Chanel, luxury options from Tom Ford and spicy, sexy scents by Gucci and Yves Saint Laurent, below you'll find a curated list of what we believe are the best men's fragrances. 

    The best men's aftershave at a glance

    The bestselling scent: DIOR Sauvage Eau de Parfum, from £79 / from $88

    The fresh scent: Chanel Bleu de Chanel Eau De Toilette Spray, from £72 / from $110

    The sexy scent: Gucci Guilty Eau de Parfum For Him, from £64 (save 15%) / from $120

    The spicy scent:  Yves Saint Laurent Y Eau de Parfum, from £72 / from $125

    The musky scent: Zara Vibrant Leather Eau de Parfum, £22 / $35.90

    The citrus scent: Prada Luna Rossa Ocean Eau de Toilette, from £65 / from $120

    The luxury scent: Tom Ford Oud Wood Eau de Parfum, from £145 / from $295

    The addictive scent: BOSS The Scent Absolute For Him Eau de Parfum, £58 (save £36) / $84.48 (save $13.52)

    The modern scent: Valentino Uomo Born In Roma Eau de Toilette, from £65 / from $97

    The customisable scent: Escentric Molecules 'Theirs' Gift Set, £171

    How we chose the best men's aftershave

    • Personal favourites: I was previously a beauty editor at a major online retailer, so I've tested all of these fragrances and can confirm they smell amazing.
    • Bestsellers: Maybe you already know he loves one of these scents, but if you're blind-buying a gift, it's often a good idea not to go too rogue. Everything featured in this edit is a bestseller for the brand, and for good reason.
    • Budget: I've included aftershaves for a mix of budgets, so you can purchase a gift for as little as £22 or up to £145.

    • The bestselling scent

      DIOR Sauvage Eau de Parfum

      Notes

      Top - Bergamot

      Middle - Sichuan Pepper, Lavender, Star Anise, Nutmeg

      Base - Ambroxan, Vanilla

      With crowd-pleasing notes of Italian bergamot, warm nutmeg and soft vanilla, it's no wonder Dior Sauvage is one of the world's bestselling scents for men. Inspired by the desert at sundown, its sensual and a little bit exotic.

      From £79 at Boots

      From £67.15 (save 15%) at John Lewis

      From $88 at DIOR US

    • The fresh scent

      Chanel Bleu de Chanel Eau De Toilette Spray

      Notes

      Top - Citrus Accord, Vetiver, Pink Pepper

      Middle - Grapefruit, Dry Cedar, Labdanum

      Base - Frankincense, Ginger, Sandalwood

      Bleu de Chanel is the most popular men's fragrance by the French fashion house. It begins fresh and clean but develops into something warmer, masculine, woody and aromatic. Subtle with impressive longevity, it's the ultimate everyday scent.

      From £72 at Boots

      From £61.20 (save 15%) at John Lewis

      $110 at Sephora US

    • The sexy scent

      Gucci Guilty Eau de Parfum For Him

      Notes

      Top - Fresh Rose, Hot Chili Pepper

      Middle - Lavender, Orange Blossom

      Base - Cedarwood, Patchouli

      Gucci scents are always a little more unique than your average. This dark and seductive fragrance mixes classic notes of rose and patchouli with red hot chili pepper for an added kick.

      From £64 (save 15%) at Boots

      From £64.69 (save 20%) at Sephora

      $120 at Sephora US

    • The spicy scent

      Yves Saint Laurent Y Eau de Parfum

      Notes

      Top - Apple, Ginger, Bergamot

      Middle - Sage, Juniper Berries, Geranium

      Base - Amberwood, Tonka Bean, Cedar, Vetiver, Olibanum

      If he likes a bit of spice, Y by Yves Saint Laurent will fit the bill. Mysterious, modern and masculine, it's just soft enough to leave you wanting more.

      From £72 at Boots

      From £65.45 (save 15%) at LookFantastic

      $125 at Sephora US

    • The musky scent

      Zara Vibrant Leather Eau de Parfum

      Notes

      Top - Bergamot, Lemon

      Middle - Bamboo, Leather

      Base - Patchouli, Papyrus

      Not just one of our favourite high street fashion stores, Zara has fast become a go-to destination for fragrance. The sell-out scents could be mistaken for designer but have a high street price tag. Many think this smells very similar to a certain Creed Aventus.

      £22.99 at Zara

      $35.90 at Zara US

    • The citrus scent

      Prada Luna Rossa Ocean Eau de Toilette

      Notes

      Top - Bergamot Essence, Pink Pepper Essence

      Middle - Iris Accord, Sage Essence

      Base - Vetiver Essence, Tonka Bean

      A cocktail of citrus and fresh, floral scents, Prada's Luna Rossa Ocean also has a unique leather heart and sensual accord of suede to add a delicious dose of masculinity. 

      From £65 at Boots

      From £56.95 (save 15%) at John Lewis

      From $120 at Sephora US

    • The luxury scent

      Tom Ford Oud Wood Eau de Parfum

      Notes

      Top - Exotic Rosewood, Cardamom

      Middle - ​Oud Wood, Sandalwood, Vetiver​​​

      Base - Tonka Bean, Amber

      Smooth, woody, and a little bit spicy, Tom Ford's Oud Wood is the definition of luxurious and alluring, which could explain its unwavering popularity. If you really want to treat him, it's a fail-safe.

      From £145 at LookFantastic

      From £145 at Sephora

      From $295 at Sephora US

    • The addictive scent

      BOSS The Scent Absolute For Him Eau de Parfum

      Notes

      Top - Ginger

      Middle - Maninka Fruit & Mondia Roots

      Base - Vetiver

      Rich and intense, the notes of aphrodisiac Maninka fruit combined with elegant Vetiver is what has everyone coming back for more with this BOSS scent.

      £58 (save £36) at Boots

      £94 at The Perfume Shop

      $84.48 (save $13.52) at Walmart US

    • The modern scent

      Valentino Uomo Born In Roma Eau de Toilette

      Notes

      Top - Violet Leaves Absolute, Ginger Essence, Citron Essence

      Middle - Clary Sage Essence, Peppermint Accord, Mineral Salt Accord

      Base - Vetiver Essence, Guaiacwood Essence, Leather

      Launched in 2019, Valentino's Born in Roma has very quickly become a popular choice for men, with it's woody, spicy and youthful but masculine scent. Suitable for all seasons, day and night, he'll be reaching for it constantly.

      From £65 at Boots

      £55.25 (save 15%) at LookFantastic

      $97 at Sephora

    • The customisable scent

      Escentric Molecules 'Theirs' Gift Set

      Notes

      Molecule 01 -  Iso E Super

      Molecule 02 - AMBROXAN™

      Molecule 03 - Vetiveryl Acetate

      Molecule 04 - Javanol

      Molecule 05 - Cashmeran

      Escentric Molecules' minimalist scents are centred around a single note aroma-chemical, which is designed to work with your pheromones, creating something that smells different on everyone. If he likes the kind of fragrance that people stop you in the street to ask what you're wearing, this is it. In this limited-edition customisable gift set, you can choose two bottles from the collections to gift one to your partner and keep one for yourself. 

      £171 at Escentric Molecules

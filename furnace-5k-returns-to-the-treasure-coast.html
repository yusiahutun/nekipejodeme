Furnace 5K Returns to the Treasure Coast

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, May 19, 2024

The Treasure Coastâ€™s hottest 5K returns this weekend. Runners and walkers are welcome to sign up for the Furnace 5K, which will wind its way through North River Shores on August 12.

Participants will meet in front of the Fleet Feet running outfitters store in Stuart (2440 NW Federal Highway) for the 5K, which kicks off at 7 a.m. Runners/walkers can pick up their packets from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. on August 10 and 11 at the store.

Registration is $40 for the 5K Run/Walk, plus a medal; $38.50 for the virtual Furnace 5K; $20 without the race medal; and $5 for the Kids Dash geared toward runners aged 9 years and younger. For more information and to register, click here.

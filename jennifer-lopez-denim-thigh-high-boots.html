Jennifer Lopez swaps traditional trousers for thigh-high boot jeans

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, March 30, 2024

It isn't easy to incorporate jeans during a heatwave, but Jennifer Lopez found a way via a pair of Versace denim thigh-high boots.

The singer attracted international fashion headlines after she scrapped her trousers in favour of her boot-jean hybrid from the Italian brand's pre-spring 2019 collection. Lopez was pictured in New York wearing just an oversized shirt, a Birkin bag and her bold denim footwear, which featured a back pocket akin to jeans and a black belt round the top of each leg. The boots were cut slouchy rather than tight.

Courtesy of Versace

She added an urban appeal with gold hoops, oversized mirrored sunglasses and bejewelled bracelets and rings. Her hair was scraped back close to her head and tied in a slick ponytail.

Her latest look comes with news that she will receive the Michael Jackson Video Vanguard Award at the 2018 MTV Video Music Awards, held on 20 August. Previous winners include Madonna and Beyonce.

Who Is Bachelorette 19's James Clarke? Bio, Job, Parents, Height

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 26, 2024

James Clarke is the ladies’ man. With his dimpled smile and smooth-talking skills, he makes everyone a little more cheerful than the moment before. However, he had yet to find the one he’d share his meatballs with (The guy absolutely loves meatballs). So, in his quest for love, James ventured into the world of Bachelorette season 19.

This James Clarke Bio is all about him.

James Clarke On Bachelorette Season 19

For the first time in the history of Bachelorette, there were two bachelorettes in one season. The 19th installment of the popular reality show featured Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia as they pondered for love in a common pool whilst also keeping their friendship above everything else. For those who don’t know these two leads were the runner-ups from Clayton Echard’s season of The Bachelor.

So, two bachelorettes in the same season meant double the chance at love. However, to keep things even, the pursuers’ size was increased to 32 men which included our star, James Clarke.

Keeping things inconspicuous, James didn’t garner much attention for himself. But he indeed was slowing moving up the chart. Sadly, he just couldn’t keep up with the other contestants and was sent home later in the season.

The final contestants were Erich, Jason, Justin, and Johnny (Gabby’s top 4), and Aven, Tyler, Tino, and Zach (Rachel’s final four).

Luckily, not of them intersected.

Also, in case you missed the earlier episodes, Koy and Corbin didn’t receive a rose and left the show on day 1. And Surprisingly, James rejected Rachel’s rose. “I’m sorry. “I’m here for Gabby,” he said.

On the other hand, Tino received Rachel’s first rose, while Mario got the first from Gabby. 

Overall, joining James to claim the bachelorettes’ hearts this season were his co-stars — Alec Garza, Aven Jones, Brandan Hall, Chris Austin, Colin Farrill, Erich Schwer, Ethan Kang, Hayden Markowitz, Jacob Rapini, Jason Alabaster, Joey Young, John Anderson, Johnny DePhillipo, Jordan Helman, Jordan Vandergriff, Justin Budfuloski,  Justin Young, Kirk Bryant, Logan Palmer, Matt Labagh, Mario Vassall, Michael Vaughan, Nate Mitchell, Quincey Williams, Robert “Roby” Sobieski, Ryan Mula, Spencer Swies, Termayne Harper, Tino Franco, Tyler Norris, and Zach Shallcross.

James Clarke Parents

James Clarke was born to parents, Kathleen and Glen Clarke.

We don’t know much about his mother, but James’ dad was born on Jul 22, 1996. He attended Villanova University and went on to work as “Managing Director and Head of the Diversified Food Group” at Bank of Montreal Harris Bank for 16 years. He then became the managing director of KeyBanc Capital Markets for almost 5 years and in July 2016, he jumped ship as the MD of Baird’s Consumer & Retail Group and worked there ever since.

Glen received his M.B.A. from DePaul University and his Bachelor of Arts from Villanova University.

As for James’s siblings, he has two sisters named Grace (born: September 2, 1998) and Elizabeth (born: June 13, 1990), and two brothers named Will (birthday: March 1) and Harry.

The last we checked, Grace (DePauw University 2018 graduate) led single life in Chicago, Illinois while Elizabeth lived with her husband whom she married in November 2020.

Info about Will and Harry wasn’t shared.

James Clarke Job

James Clarke was an MBA candidate when he appeared on Bachelorette in 2022. Focusing on his studies, the star had just quit his job as a client assistant at Baird in January that year.

Reportedly, he started his job at Baird (where his dad was its MD) in November 2020.

Prior to this, James worked as an acquisition analyst and property manager at Winnemac Properties for a year. Also, he was a client service analyst at Summit Trail Advisors from October 2018 to January 2017.

As for where he kickstarted his career, he interned for 3 months at Wintrust Financial Corporation in 2017.

Listing his education details — James attended Loyola Academy from 2010 to 2014, Spring Hill College from 2014 to 2016, and DePauw University from 2016 to 2018 (Bachelor’s degree in communications) and started his MBA in finance at the same university in 2020.

During his high school and college days, James always was on the varsity basketball team. Infact, he was so good that he played some Euro League ball over in Spain.

His favorite player is LeBron James.

Besides basketball, James also likes playing guitar and listening to John Mayer.

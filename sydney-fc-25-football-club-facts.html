Foundation of Sydney FC

Sydney FC was founded in 2004 and became the inaugural team in the newly-formed A-League.

Home Ground

The club’s home ground is the iconic Sydney Football Stadium, located in Moore Park, Sydney.

Three A-League Championships

Sydney FC has won the A-League Championship three times, in 2005-2006, 2009-2010, and 2016-2017 seasons.

Asian Champions League Success

In 2020, Sydney FC became the first Australian team to win the Asian Champions League, the premier club competition in Asia.

Record-Breaking Unbeaten Streak

Sydney FC holds the record for the longest unbeaten streak in the A-League, going 27 games unbeaten from 2016 to 2017.

Domestic Double

In the 2009-2010 season, Sydney FC achieved the remarkable feat of winning both the A-League Championship and the A-League Premiership.

Rivalry with Western Sydney Wanderers

Sydney FC shares a heated rivalry with the Western Sydney Wanderers, known as the Sydney Derby.

Iconic Players

Throughout its history, Sydney FC has been home to several iconic players, including Alessandro Del Piero, Dwight Yorke, and Milos Ninkovic.

Graham Arnold’s Success

Under the guidance of coach Graham Arnold, Sydney FC achieved unprecedented success, winning multiple championships and breaking numerous records.

Supporter Groups

Sydney FC boasts passionate supporter groups, such as The Cove and The Red and Black Bloc, who create an incredible atmosphere during matches.

Sydney FC W-League

Sydney FC also has a highly successful women’s team, known as the Sydney FC W-League, which has won multiple championships.

Alessandro Del Piero’s Arrival

In 2012, Sydney FC made headlines worldwide by signing Italian legend Alessandro Del Piero, adding immense star power to the team.

Sydney FC Academy

Sydney FC has a flourishing youth academy that has produced many talented players, some of whom have gone on to represent the national team.

Record-Breaking Crowds

Sydney FC has attracted record-breaking crowds to their matches, showcasing the immense popularity of the club among football fans.

A-League Fair Play Award

Sydney FC has been recognized for its sporting behavior and fair play, winning the A-League Fair Play Award multiple times.

International Marquee Players

Sydney FC has had notable international marquee players throughout the years, including Bobo, Juninho, and Marc Janko.

Sydney FC Foundation

In 2007, the Sydney FC Foundation was established to support grassroots football and community initiatives.

Sydney FC Logo and Colors

The Sydney FC logo features a stylized opera house silhouette, representing the city’s iconic landmark. The team’s colors are sky blue, navy blue, and white.

Sydney FC Mascot

The club’s mascot, Syd the Sky Blue Kangaroo, brings joy and entertainment to fans of all ages during matches.

Sydney FC Legends

Sydney FC has honored its legends by establishing the Sydney FC Hall of Fame, recognizing the club’s finest players and contributors.

Sydney FC Community Programs

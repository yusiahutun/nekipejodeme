Courteney Cox Looks Chic in Sexy Leather Pants to Grab Dinner With Friends in L.A.

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Look who came out to dinner! Courteney Cox didn’t disappoint when she went out to eat with friends at Madeo restaurant in Beverly Hills on Friday, January 17. The 55-year-old looked chic in a sexy pair of black pants and a comfortable gray sweater, which matched her signature dark glasses perfectly.

Although the paparazzi didn’t catch who Courteney was meeting with this time, she did reunite with her Friends costars Jennifer Aniston, [David] Schwimmer, Matthew Perry, Lisa Kudrow and Matt LeBlanc in October 2019.

“We just had dinner on Saturday. Yeah, the whole gang, everyone was there. David was in town and we all happened to have a window of time so we all got together,” Jen, 50 recalled on The Howard Stern Show. “We laughed so hard.”

When they all got together, Jen said it felt like “lightning in a bottle.” In fact, “not one” person bothered her the whole night. “Nobody got naked. It was very civilized,” she said. “But we just laughed a lot.”

Since then, Courteney and her buddies have been reuniting quite often. On Sunday, January 12, she hung out with the Murder Mystery star and Lisa, 56, again. Jen shared a photo of their reunion to Instagram and captioned it, “Hi from the girls across the hall.” The post has been liked nearly 9.5 million times, as of this article being published.

After all, the trio love to hang out together. Courteney was there when Jen celebrated her 50th birthday in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. When looking back at the trip there, she recalled how their plane had to emergency land after it lost a tire during takeoff in February 19.

“I’m not afraid of flying at all … my dad was a pilot … but I was really scared because when we were taking off, we heard this really loud bang and I was like, ‘Oh, that sounds like we should probably check that tire,’” the dark-haired beauty recalled to Extra. “There was no tire at all, there was no wheel. Luckily, there are two wheels on each side and two in the front. I have to say, we were in the air for four hours, burning off fuel and just thought of what it is going to be like when we land … it was a really smooth landing.”

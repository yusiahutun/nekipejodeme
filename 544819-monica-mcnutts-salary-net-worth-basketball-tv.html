Monica McNutt’s basketball career

Did Monica McNutt play basketball? Monica McNutt, born on October 24, 1989, played high school basketball for the Academy of the Holy Cross in Suitland, Maryland. This is where she honed some confidence on the basketball court before joining Georgetown University.

She was a two-year team captain for Georgetown Hoyas and, as a senior in 2011, led them to a Sweet 16 appearance.

Monica McNutt’s education

Monica earned her bachelor’s degree from Georgetown University and completed her collegiate career as a standout women’s basketball player in 2011. She earned her master’s degree in journalism from the University of Maryland in 2013.

Monica McNutt’s broadcasting career

Does Monica McNutt still work for ESPN? Monica has been a sports reporter since 2013 and joined ESPN in 2019. From there, she expanded her professional scope to include reporting on women's college basketball and as a WNBA announcer.

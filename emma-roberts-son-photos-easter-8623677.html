Emma Roberts Shared a Rare Photo of Her Son Rhodes

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, April 2, 2024

Even though Easter was last weekend, proud moms are still sharing sweet snapshots of the holiday, including Emma Roberts, who posted a sweet photo with her 3-year-old son Rhodes, whom she with her ex, Garrett Hedlund. Yesterday, the Madame Web and American Horror Story actress posted an image from Easter Sunday that showed a sweet mommy-and-me moment that included coordinating beanies and a leopard-print fleece jacket that looks like a hypebeast's dream come true. 

Roberts looked fresh and glowy, with her signature bold eyebrows peeking out from underneath her ribbed hat. She held her son and smiled as he looked away. The two had similar flowing blonde hair showing from beneath their beanies and Roberts tagged clothing brand The Animal Observatory in her post.

She captioned the photo with a hatching-chick emoji.

While many celebrities choose to keep their kids off of their social media feeds, Roberts does share images of her son for major milestones. Last year, she posted a heartwarming image for his third birthday.

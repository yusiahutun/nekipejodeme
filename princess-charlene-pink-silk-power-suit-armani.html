Princess Charlene rocks silk power suit and fans have thoughts

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Prince Albert II's wife sported an Armani suit for the occasion

    Princess Charlene of Monaco has mastered the art of bringing power dressing to the masses. On Wednesday, the Monegasque royal demonstrated her styling skills as she attended a meeting with the President of the Italian branch of the Prince Albert II Foundation.

    The royal looked to Armani for a helping hand, sporting a silk pink suit crafted by the house's design team. In images captured by @royalfashionpolice, Prince Albert's wife wore the brand's 'Froissé Viscose Lapel Jacket with Slits in Rosa Chiaro ' paired with the coordinating 'Froissé Viscose Trousers with Gathered Waist.'

    Layering to perfection, Princess Charlene topped off with Armani's 'Pure Silk Crêpe-de-Chine Sleeveless Top,' which echoed the blossom pink hue of the luxury suit.

    Featuring a feminine single-breasted cut, sleek notched lapels and a classic fit, the former Olympian's ensemble made for an excellent addition to her spring wardrobe. Not to mention the look is undeniably on-trend in wake of the ongoing influence that Barbiecore is currently exercising over the fashion industry.

    The princess wore her recently darkened pixie cut swept to the side and opted for a natural beauty look that boasted a porcelain complexion and a gentle flutter of mascara.

    Upon seeing images of Charlene emerge online, fans all shared a similar sentiment. "I love this sleek and simple style," one wrote, as another commented: "This is a beautiful and classic tailored ensemble. She’s pretty in pink." A third agreed, noting: "Beautiful," and a fourth said: "Amazing Princess."

    Princess Charlene has been doling out the dreamy new looks this past week. On Wednesday, the former Olympian joined her husband Prince Albert to attend a gala celebrating the 160th anniversary of the Monegasque Consulate of Monaco. As for her chosen 'fit, we are totally hooked.

    The 45-year-old made an entrance in a glistening Akris gown coined the 'Tulle & Sequin Long Sleeve A-Line Gown.' Featuring a divine mesh of materials, long sleeves, an iridescent ebony sequined finish and an operatic cut, the luxurious garment proved a hit among fans. Just look at the dress' £6k price tag for an indication of quite how lavish the piece is…

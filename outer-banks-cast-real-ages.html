How Old Is the 'Outer Banks' Cast?

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, January 26, 2024

New Netflix series Outer Banks follows two sets of rivaling teenaged groups, the Kooks and the Pogues, acting as stand-ins for the all-American parable between the haves and the have-nots. As they search for buried treasure in North Carolina, they're plagued by the quintessential issues that crop up in Hollywood's portrayal of teenage angst: drugs, romance, money, and murder mysteries. The kids are all right, but are the kids even kids?

Although the characters are mostly said to be around 16 years old, the cast is (unsurprisingly) much older than that. This fact, of course, isn't exactly breaking new ground, since most actors who play teenagers tend to exceed the age of their characters by a pretty substantial number of years.

Read on to be shocked (or not) by the real ages of the Outer Banks cast.

Madelyn Cline as Sarah Cameron

Age on the show: 16

Age in real life: 23

JACKSON LEE DAVIS//Netflix

Cline is actually several years older than Sarah.

Chase Stokes as John B

Age on the show: 16

Age in real life: 28

Netflix

Stokes is more than a decade older than the character he portrays on the show.

Madison Bailey as Kiara

Age on the show: 16

Age in real life: 22

JACKSON LEE DAVIS//Netflix

Bailey is six years older than "Kie."

Jonathan Daviss as Pope

Age on the show: 16

Age in real life: 22

JACKSON LEE DAVIS//Netflix

Daviss is also six years older than his character.

Rudy Pankow as JJ

Age on the show: 16

Age in real life: 22

JACKSON LEE DAVIS//Netflix

JJ is six years younger than the actor who embodies him on our laptop and TV screens.

Drew Starkey as Rafe

Age on the show: 19

Age in real life: 27

JACKSON LEE DAVIS//Netflix

Starkey is, shall we say, at least a few years older than his character.

Austin North as Topper

Age on the show: 16

Age in real life: 24

JACKSON LEE DAVIS//Netflix

North is eight years older than Topper.

Julia Antonelli as Wheezie Cameron

Age on the show: 13

Age in real life: 18

JACKSON LEE DAVIS//Netflix

Antonelli happens to be the only teenager present in the main/recurring cast, but her character is still several years younger than her.

Carlacia Grant as Cleo

Age on the show: N/A

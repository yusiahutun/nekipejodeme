Oprah's Favorite Things 2022 - Best Fashion Gift Ideas

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

Dearfoams Indoor/Outdoor Warm Up Bootie Slipper

Dearfoams Indoor/Outdoor Warm Up Bootie Slipper

Now 41% Off

Featuring quilted nylon on the outside and memory foam insoles, Oprah describes the feeling of these plush slippers as being like "walking on a cloud." These can be worn indoors and outdoors alike, which makes them a thoughtful gift for moms, dads, and dog owners of all adult shoe sizes.

Minnetonka Lucie Indoor-Outdoor Slipper

Minnetonka Lucie Indoor-Outdoor Slipper

Oprah named these indoor-outdoor slippers to her Favorite Things List as well this year, and also described this style as being cloud-like thanks to the faux-fur lining and cushioned footbed.

JW PEI Canvas Cell Phone Crossbody Bag

JW PEI Canvas Cell Phone Crossbody Bag

JW Pei not only has fans in Hailey Bieber and Emily Ratajkowski (both of whom own the brand's $80 croissant-shaped Gabbi Bag), but Oprah as well. Per Oprah's description: "This crossbody makes life easier. It fits most phones, has slots for credit cards, and comes in cheery colors."

Girlfriend Collective Compressive Pocket Legging

Girlfriend Collective Compressive Pocket Legging

Gayle King introduced Oprah to inclusive activewear brand Girlfriend Collective. If you're after a gift for your best friend or any other fitness lover on your list, this compressive style is the label's #1 best selling pair of leggings. These are available in sizes XS through 6XL and 79 percent of the fabric is made from recycled plastic bottles.

Ray-Ban Wayfarer Stories Smart Glasses

Ray-Ban Wayfarer Stories Smart Glasses

As Oprah herself put it in this year's Favorite Things List, Ray-Ban Stories "...might just be the gift of the year!"

While these frames might look like Ray-Ban's Wayfarers (the unisex style worn by everyone from Kate Middleton to Tom Cruise over the years), they can actually connect to your phone. Use them to listen to music, make calls, and even take pictures and videos. Fulfilling the tech dreams of any millennial who ever watched Spy Kids, these features can all be activated by just a simple tap or voice command.

Paravel Fold-Up Travel Backpack

Paravel Fold-Up Travel Backpack

Made from 15 upcycled plastic water bottles, this water-resistant nylon backpack can fold into a zippaple pouch, which makes it an especially useful accessory for traveling.

KULE The Malibu 100 Percent Cotton Featherweight Tee

KULE The Malibu 100 Percent Cotton Featherweight Tee

Any Francophile needs a reliable Breton stripe top in their wardrobe. This look from Kule gets it just right with lightness and simplicity. Per Oprah: "I put this shirt on when I saw it and that was it—love at first sight."

Lands' End Quilted ThermoPlume Insulated Coat

Lands' End Quilted ThermoPlume Insulated Coat

Now 19% Off

A long-time fan of Lands' End, Oprah named Lands' End in her Favorite Things List for the third year in a row this year. High-end optics aside, the label's water-resistant ThermoPlume Coat was made from recycled insulation that was tested for warmth in temps as low as -13 degrees F.

Benevolence LA Velvet Travel Jewelry Box Organizer

Benevolence LA Velvet Travel Jewelry Box Organizer

For the jewelry lover on-the-go, this strategically organized jewelry case makes for a no-brainer gift idea. It comes in navy, emerald, and periwinkle as well.

Sterling Forever Five Days of Hoops Advent Box

Sterling Forever Five Days of Hoops Advent Box

There's no such thing as being too old for advent calendars, especially when versatile hoops are involved. This thoughtfully packaged set includes five sets of hypoallergenic, nickel-free hoops. A silver version is available as well.

SPANX AirEssentials Wide Leg Pants

SPANX AirEssentials Wide Leg Pants

Oprah has decreed these airy sweats and their corresponding half-zip her "new leisure suit" in this year's Favorite Things List.

According to the mogul, "[t]he perfectly loose-and-lightweight fabric is like buttah!"

EMU Australia Micro Teddy Winter Real Sheepskin Boots

EMU Australia Micro Teddy Winter Real Sheepskin Boots

They might look like slippers, but these EMU beauties are actually real world-friendly boots.

Brouk and Co 2-in-1 Garment and Duffel Bag

Brouk and Co 2-in-1 Garment and Duffel Bag

Whether you're traveling to a destination wedding, corporate business conference, or anywhere else, Oprah says this carryall "makes schlepping your stuff for an overnight getaway goof-proof." To use this, simply just fill up the garment bag first, then add shoes to the side pockets and zip it all up.

Tourance Beanie and Scarf Set

Tourance Beanie and Scarf Set

Oprah described this wintery accessory's unique design as: "If a scarf, a hat, and earmuffs had a baby, you'd get this warming wonder." The neck warming scarf can be worn together or separately.

Alpine Cashmere 100% Cashmere Striped Travel Wrap

Alpine Cashmere 100% Cashmere Striped Travel Wrap

Cashmere scarves are as timeless as cold-weather accessories come. Making this Oprah-loved option worth the splurge is the fact that it can also be worn as a wrap or travel blanket.

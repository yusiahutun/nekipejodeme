Beyonc Wore Sheer Lace Bodysuit and Thigh-High Boots

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Beyoncé is continuing her sartorial country-inspired reign with her latest ‘fit, this time while taking public transit. While vacationing with Jay-Z, the "16 Carriages" singer infused her Cowboy Carter-esque outfit with a little sultry Renaissance glam – and it's clear Queen Bey was feeling flawless. She took to the social media platform on Friday, April 19 to share a carousel of 10 images and videos all highlighting a sexy sheer lace bodysuit that hugged her body from head to toe.

The “Lemonade” star, who is currently in Japan with her husband, had fun with a playful photo shoot, modeling the mahogany lace catsuit with various accessories including a statement gold chain Chloé belt with the designer brand’s name written in cursive, a camel poncho featuring a plaid under trim, and mahogany thigh-high leather boots. In a few of the photos, she switched out her two-toned cap for a black cowboy hat – a fashion staple during Beyoncé’s latest era. She accessorized with chunky gold earrings, tinted rose sunglasses, a mauve lip, and a boxy nude leather purse.

While most of the footage appeared to be taken in a Japanese train station and in the train while passing by Mt. Fuji, Bey also appeared to continue the photo shoot at home. After sharing a photo of her serving looks on public transit ‘fit, the star took a more playful approach by posting a photo compilation video of her posing and dancing in the skin tight sheer outfit, this time with a cowboy hat on her head. The third image – which showed three Beyoncés in various sultry poses, including one of her with a flower between her teeth – was set against a tartan background. She also shared detail shots of her ‘fit, including three different takes of her belt.

Sopranos star who sells nude 'Sopornos' pics to flog iconic 'cameltoe' outfit

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 9, 2024

Former star of The Sopranos Drea de Matteo is set to sell her iconic catsuit from the show as she “can’t live with that cameltoe”.

The 51-year-old Emmy Award-winning actress starred as mob wife Adriana La Cerva in the hit series, which many regard as the greatest TV show of all time, between 1999 and 2006. But after a long, lucrative spell in Hollywood, Drea switched to a racier line of work to sell nude snaps online which she dubbed the “Sopornos”.

On Tuesday, the New Yorker revealed that she’s selling an iconic piece of Sopranos history. “Might be auctioning this c***y catsuit off soon,” she told her 492,000 Instagram followers on her story on Monday.

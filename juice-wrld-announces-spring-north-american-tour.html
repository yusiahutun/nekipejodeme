Juice WRLD Announces Spring North American Tour

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, September 30, 2024

(CelebrityAccess) – Juice WRLD has announced a spring North American tour. The rapper will be hitting the road across the U.S. and Canada beginning April 25 in Vancouver, BC, and will make stops in Seattle, Los Angeles, New Orleans, New York, and Toronto among other cities before wrapping up in Dallas, TX on June 8.

Tickets go on sale to the general public on February 22, but fans can access a pre-sale by pre-ordering the rapper’s upcoming second LP, A Deathrace for Love, or by purchasing merch from his four collaborations with VLONE, Lyrical Lemonade, Half Evil and Ransom.

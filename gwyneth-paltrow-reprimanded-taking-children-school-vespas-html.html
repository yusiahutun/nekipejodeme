She's famed for her strict parenting choices.

But it seems Gwyneth Paltrow's methods are being questioned since she has reportedly come under fire for doing the school run in Los Angeles with her young children Apple, 10, and Moses, eight, on board Vespas.

School staff reportedly advised the Iron Man 3 actress and her estranged husband Chris Martin to find a new mode of transport after TMZ revealed the blonde beauty had a near-miss with a bus while riding one of the vehicles with one of her kids.

'But after Gwyneth was photographed on the Vespa pulling out in front of a bus, school chiefs reprimanded her and said they'd rather she and Chris didn't bring the kids to school on scooters.'

The 41-year-old star admitted earlier this month that her children had only just begun taking a school bus and it has given the youngsters new independence.

Ticking off: School bosses were reportedly worried after it was claimed Gwyneth almost collided with a bus

 

She revealed on Jimmy Kimmel Live: 'Actually, this is their first year on the bus. They take the bus and then I pick them up at the bus stop. It's pretty cute.

'They feel very independent ... [Their first day back] was great. They seemed really happy. They got off the bus. They were smiling. They had backpacks.'

A spokesperson for the 41-year-old actress insisted the claims are false, despite Gwyneth recently being pictured on the mode of transport.

They said: 'This story is not true. It was last year that they would occasionally drive the kids to school on scooters. This year, the kids take the bus.'

Gwyneth and Chris have remained friends in spite of 'consciously uncoupling' earlier this year.

The Coldplay frontman is said to be dating Jennifer Lawrence and has reportedly already introduced the Hunger Games superstar to his friends.

The Bachelorette's Jillian Harris poses in bikini months after giving birth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 11, 2024

She just gave birth to her baby boy Leo in August.

But it looks like 36-year-old Jillian Harris didn't waste anytime in getting her pre baby shape back.

The Canadian television personality and interior designer posted a photo of her wearing a black bandeau bikini on Instagram on Sunday.

Sunday funday: Jillian Harris posted a photo of her wearing a black bandeau bikini on Instagram 

Lovely: The brunette beauty wore a black scalloped bandeau top 

The brunette beauty wore a black scalloped bandeau top and a pair of high waisted bottoms as she sat on a pool chair.

She added a chic hat to her look, which covered her face as she rested her hand on the hat for the photo. 

The former Bachelorette showed off her highlighted wavy brunette locks as she gave a little smirk for the photo. 

Posing up a storm: The pretty family all stood beside one another for a photo on the beach

New mama: Jillian donned a stripy monochrome bathing suit, while cradling Leo

The host of HGTV Love it Or List it Vancouver shared several beach/pool photos to her Instagram.

In one shot, her partner Justin Pasutto, 26, held their son Leo high in the air, which looked like a scene from The Lion King.

Jillian admired her partners pose as she wore a floaty nude dress and raised her hand to her adorable son.

She captioned the photo: 'One of the best decisions on this trip... was to get a photographer to capture our love... Thank you "Wendy from Maui" & @Flytographer for these incredible snaps.'

Her treasure: The pair got in the pool at one point as she raised him in the air

In another photo the happy family all pose beside one another as Jillian dons a stripy monochrome bathing suit, while cradling Leo. 

She gave birth to Leo on August 5 2016 and was candid with her fans about her difficult labour, which included included 'major ripping and an episiotomy'.

She wrote on her blog jillianharris.com: 'We found out that the umbilical cord was wrapped around our little babe twice, and during the contractions, his heart rate was plummeting. I could hear it slow down, and we started to panic. I tried changing positions, moving around, but nothing.

Cocktail time: She also wore a white two piece while sipping on a refreshing drink

'We were told, that if I didn’t dilate quick, we were going to have to proceed with a C-section. I didn’t want a C-section, but I remember Justin and I knowing at that point, we had to do whatever it took to get our sweet babe out … QUICK.'

Luckily, they were able to evade heading to the OR.

'It came time to push, and after about an hour of pushing, some major ripping, an episiotomy… we welcomed little Leo, at 6:02 am…'

Simba! Justin Pasutto held their son Leo high in the air, which looked like a scene from The Lion King

'Literally, THE BEST DAY of our LIVES and I am so happy I am able to share it with all of you,' she wrote.    

She also posted intimate pictures from hospital on her website. 

The reality star recently opened up about how motherhood has been these past few months.

'The first three months were tough,' the former Bachelorette told People last week. 

However the new mother did reveal that she is now 'figuring it all out.' She said: 'I literally felt like a new person and was learning everything all over again!'

Slide time! It looked like the family were having a blast 

Her and Justin have been together for two years and the media personality shed som light on how the pair met to People: 'I was 32 when we met, and Justin was a fresh ripe 24 (BTW I SWEAR he told me he was 27, which I rounded up to 28… so in my mind we were basically the same age HAHA),' she said.

'Our age difference was never really an issue for us, our love was always strong and very real and that’s all that mattered. But truth be told, I could have gotten knocked up with his baby on that second date and I probably would have been over the moon… haha… ok MAYBE too soon, but I WAS smitten.'

Jillian originally found love on The Bachelorette in season 5 when she chose Ed Swiderski.

However due to Ed's wandering eye and infidelities, their relationship ended in 2010.

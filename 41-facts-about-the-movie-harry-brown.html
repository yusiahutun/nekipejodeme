FAQs

Q: What is the plot of the movie “Harry Brown”?

A: “Harry Brown” follows the story of Harry, a retired Marine and widower living in a crime-infested neighborhood. When his best friend is brutally murdered by a gang, Harry takes matters into his own hands to seek justice.

Q: Who stars in “Harry Brown”?

A: The lead role of Harry Brown is portrayed by the legendary actor Michael Caine. The movie also features performances from Emily Mortimer, Charlie Creed-Miles, and Ben Drew.

Q: Is “Harry Brown” based on a true story?

A: No, “Harry Brown” is a work of fiction. However, the film addresses real-life issues such as urban decay, gang violence, and the challenges faced by communities affected by crime.

Q: What genre does “Harry Brown” belong to?

A: “Harry Brown” is categorized as a crime thriller and drama. It combines elements of action, suspense, and social commentary to create a powerful and engaging narrative.

Q: Is the movie suitable for all audiences?

A: “Harry Brown” is rated R for strong violence, language, and drug use. Due to its intense and graphic nature, it is recommended for mature audiences.

Q: What is the overall reception of “Harry Brown”?

A: The movie received positive reviews from both critics and audiences. It was praised for its performances, gritty atmosphere, and thought-provoking story. Many consider it to be one of Michael Caine’s standout roles.

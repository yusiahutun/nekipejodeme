Seth Rogen's house and cars

The actor has invested in real estate. In 2006, he parted with $1.65 million to buy a house in West Hollywood, California. He listed it and sold it for $2.124 million in October 2020.

In 2012, he purchased another West Hollywood home for $1.1 million. In early 2020, it was put on the market for $1.7 million. Around the same time, he paid $1.9 million for another home in the same neighbourhood.

Seth and his wife own a sprawling property near Hollywood Hills worth $11 million. In December 2014, they paid $8 million for a 7-acre house with a tennis court and a pool. In July 2020, he added $3 million for a contiguous 1-acre property. He also owns a Toyota Highlander worth $35,000.

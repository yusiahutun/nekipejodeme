Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

Business Chicks founder Emma Issacs has shocked friends and family after marrying girlfriend Azure Antoinette in a surprise wedding in Las Vegas.

The 43-year-old Australian entrepreneur tied the knot in a laid back ceremony with her US sweetheart on Tuesday and later shared the news to followers on her social media.

They were later captured in a gallery after the wedding strolling the neon streets of Las Vegas.

In one photo Emma, who is a mother of six, can be seen smiling widely as she walks arm-in-arm with Azure, who holds a large bouquet of pink flowers.

Another picture shows the pair hand-in-hand against the romantic backdrop of Las Vegas at night.

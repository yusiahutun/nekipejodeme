Maureen Lipman reveals she marched out of the room in disgust when made to watch Naked Attraction

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 25, 2024

Maureen Lipman has revealed that she couldn't sit through Channel 4 show Naked Attraction when taking part in Celebrity Gogglebox, but that the clip didn't make the final broadcast.

The 74-year-old actress sits with her old pal Gyles Brandreth on the series and watches TV - and were made to sit through an episode of the nude dating show which sees singletons decide whether or not they want to romance one another based on their naked bodies.

It got a bit much for Maureen she said - slipping in a racy confession as she explained herself in a recent interview about the show.

She said: 'Without question, the worst thing we watch is Naked Attraction. It’s a long time since I’ve seen five uncircumcised penises all in a row. And I hope it’s a long time before I see them again!

'With the willies, I walked out of the room! I said, "I have one life and I do not intend to spend another moment here watching this!"

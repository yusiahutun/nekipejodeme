Megan Fox Is Sizzling in Lace-Up Pants on Date Night with Machine Gun Kelly

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, May 12, 2024

Megan Fox is back with yet another sizzling street style moment.

The Jennifer's Body actress and her fiancé, Machine Gun Kelly, were spotted enjoying a night out in Milan this week. And as per usual, the couple coordinated in bold and experimental outfits.

Fox looked like a bombshell in a pair of olive green croc-embossed trousers, which featured two columns of crisscrossing lace-up straps on each leg. She wore the statement pants with a taupe tank top and a red, white, and blue flannel button-down. To accessorize, Fox opted for a tortoiseshell box bag and fluffy red hat.

Meanwhile, Kelly coordinated in a vampy, nearly all-red ensemble. He looked edgy in a shiny maroon trench coat, which he layered over black leather trousers and a black sweater with a bright red design. He, too, accessorized with a fluffy hat—his appearing in black—as well as gray-framed sunglasses, a pearl earring, a necklace, and chunky rings.

Splash News

In an interview earlier this year, Fox opened up about how her relationship with Kelly has encouraged her to embrace her personal style.

"Prior to being famous everyone who knew me always knew that I would always wear one really insane piece, and everybody would be like, 'You dress weird.' And it wasn't until I was molded and had to dress one way that I gave up on that," she said. "And being with him, obviously, he's slightly eccentric in the way that he dresses—and that has freed me up to express myself more. And he helped hook me up with Maeve Reilly, my stylist."

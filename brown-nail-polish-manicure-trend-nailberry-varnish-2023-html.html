Nailberry's Scented Collection offers three suits-all-skin-tones shades, named after their fragrance infusions. Coffee, a rich, warm brown, Strawberry, a Barbie pink, and Mint, a soft sage green.

'This year, I wanted to do something very different and fun. Scented polishes are ideal,' says Sonia Hully, Nailberry’s Founder. 'I love how they encourage us to get playful and try something new - all while caring for nails and making manicures even more joyful!'.

While their scents are certainly a unique selling point, the Nailberry L’Oxygéné formula is what really makes the polishes so popular.

Richly pigmented, glossy and long-lasting, the patented oxygenated formula also better respects nail care, allowing airflow to help prevent the yellowing and damage often associated with bog-standard nail polish.

According to the brand, a single sweep of the Nailberry patented formula delivers a smooth and streak-free professional finish that will never clump or crack

Keeping your nails healthy and perfectly polished, the new Nailberry polishes have gained impressive reviews.

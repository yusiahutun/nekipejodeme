And just in time for the Passover Seders next week, I’m overhauling my dress collection with fresh picks from Amazon; the retailer has more than 30,000 floral options to choose from, but I narrowed it down to the 10 best dresses under $50. 

My picks include mini, midi, and maxis in a range of sleeve lengths and silhouettes. Just be sure to grab your favorites before word gets out about Amazon’s epic dress selection.

Naggoo Short-Sleeve Mini Wrap Dress

When nearly 10,000 Amazon shoppers give the same dress a five-star rating, you know it must be good. This short-sleeve wrap mini is the “best and most comfortable dress,” per a reviewer thanks to its flattering fit and high-quality material. It comes in 30 colors and patterns, including solid shades, polka dots, and florals, of course. Plus, it’s “versatile to wear for many occasions,” as one shopper put it; style the dress casually with sneakers and a denim jacket, or zhuzh it up with strappy heels and a clutch. 

Dokotoo Three-Quarter-Sleeve Mini Babydoll Dress

If you prefer a fit-and-flare silhouette, check out this Dokotoo babydoll number. Available in six floral prints, the square-neck dress has billowing three-quarter-length sleeves, a shirred bodice, and ruffled hemline. Not to mention, it’s completely lined, so you don’t have to worry about your undergarments showing through the fabric. The dress strikes the perfect balance between comfort and style, making it ideal for sitting through an hours-long Seder next week. 

Prettygarden Halter-Neck Ruffle-Hem Belted Maxi Dress

Wedding season is upon us, and this Prettygarden floral maxi is a foolproof guest outfit for a semi-formal event. It has a flattering halter neckline with a keyhole button closure on the back, as well as a fabric tie belt and a ruffled skirt to create movement. One reviewer wore the dress to a wedding and “received compliments as soon as [they] walked in the door.” They said it was “so comfortable and [they] felt so sexy” in it. You can’t ask for much more from a $43 dress.

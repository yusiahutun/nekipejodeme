Homebase slashes price of 3-seater swing seat by 50% in huge summer-saving offers

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

Brits are still making the most of their gardens even though Covid restrictions have been lifted.

Family and friends are still getting together to have garden parties and gatherings at home whilst enjoying the glorious UK sunshine.

If you want to get even more enjoyment out of your garden, then you will be pleased to know that home improvement retailer Homebase has reduced the price of its 3-seater swing seat to just £125., originally priced at £250, saving you a massive 50%.

The grey swing seat will make the perfect garden accessory as it's ideal for anyone who wants to just sit back, relax and listen to the bird song... or the neighbours kids screaming, whilst having fun from their own gardens.

It's also the perfect chill out zone for tired kids, especially now that the school holidays have begun, there will be lots of activity going on, so the 3-seater swing will provide the ideal moments of chill.

The swing has a steel frame that is made from easy drying and easy-to-clean textilene fabric.

Customers can order the item and qualify for a free standard delivery. Next day delivery and ordering into the store will cost you £6, and click and collect is also free.

To find your nearest store, we have included this handy store locator tool here to make things easier.

Customers hoping to get their hands on the 3-seater swing seat at the discounted price will need to be quick, as once the deal is gone, it's gone.

If you do miss this fantastic deal, worry not, as other retailers are also selling similar versions of the swing seat.

We spotted this Angel Living 3 Seated Canopy Swing, priced at £109.95 at Amazon and it comes in several bright colours that would cheer up any garden. It also boasts an adjustable ceiling angle for suitable shade come rain or shine.

We also spotted this bargain Outsunny 3 Seater Canopy Swing Chair in black, priced at £89.99 also from Amazon. The 3-seater also comes in brown, for a money-saving £79.99.

We also came across this Blue And White Striped 3-seater Swing Chair for £74.99 at The Range. The Weather-resistant material is designed to sustain bad weather while the canopy will help to shield you from the sun's UV rays.

Argos is selling the Home 3 Seater Metal Swing Chair in Grey for £75. The neutral colour and simple design works in any garden, and the frame can be left outside no matter the weather, so you'll have no worries about packing it away when you're done.

Families preferring something a little more sturdy are in luck, as Aldi launches new Wicker Garden Coffee Set that's perfect for summer chilling.

Vivica A. Fox stuns in two vibrant summer dresses

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 29, 2024

Vivica A. Fox looked positively radiant in an elegant, yellow sundress in New York City earlier Tuesday morning. 

The actress donned the eye-catching outfit on her way to an interview in the big apple. 

Vivica, 57, has been promoting her series, Keeping Up With The Joneses, which was renewed for another season on LMN. 

Stunning: Vivica Fox, 57, looked radiant in a yellow dress as she headed to Good Morning America in New York City Tuesday morning 

The Independence Day actress opted for a summery look, and wore a yellow, sleeveless dress to stay cool in the warm weather.

She added a thick, white leather Valentino belt to add a fashionable edge to her look. 

The price for the luxury brand's belt can range around $990. The star added white pumps to coincide with the color scheme of her outfit. 

Beautiful: The actress sent smiles to the cameras as she stepped outside in New York City 

The Kill Bill actress accessorized her look with look with a pair of gold hooped, dangly earrings. 

She also added a silver necklace with a heart-shaped pendant with a larger necklace underneath with what looks like the word Vivica on the chain. 

The beauty carried a large, brown purse in her hand and wore a watch on her wrist to keep up with her busy schedule. 

The star had her hair pulled back into a sleek ponytail, with her long locks pulled into a rubber band and curled in the back. 

Classy outfit: The star looked very elegant and chic in her yellow dress as she enjoyed the summer day 

So stylish: Vivica had a small outfit change for another interview, wearing a tighter and shorter dress later in the day 

Later as the day went on, the actress had a quick outfit change, and opted for a tighter and shorter dress - still with a yellow theme to it. 

The outfit contained a pattern with hues of yellow and purple. She added a pair of stylish open-toed purple heels. 

She carried a vibrant yellow purse in her hand, and switched her jewelry to a necklace with a fox pendant and large, silver hooped earrings. 

Glamorous: Vivica donned another eye-catching outfit during her busy day of interview in NYC

Great conversations: The Independence Day actress also stopped to talk on Live With Kelly And Ryan 

Vivica made a few stops on Tuesday for a couple interviews, and stopped by to talk with GMA and also Live With Kelly And Ryan. 

During her time on Good Morning America, the actress had a chance to talk about her drama series, Keeping Up With The Joneses, which airs on the Lifetime Movie Network. 

She plays the character of Robin who takes over the family business after her husband passes away. 

On the topic of having the opportunity to have a leading role on the movie mini series, the actress stated, 'For me it was rewarding, you know, because everyday it's like wardrobe is absolutely fabulous.'  

Big projects: The actress discussed her role as Robin on the LMN drama series, Keeping Up With The Joneses 

Busy bee: Vivica has also dabbled into the filmmaking industry, and will have four films for Keeping Up With Joneses premiere on LMN in July 

The Soul Food star revealed that four films part of Keeping Up With The Joneses movie series will premiere on LMN in July. 

'We're back with four new movies that are going to be dropping this Friday and it's just been exciting for me to become a filmmaker and I'm grateful to Lifetime Movie Network,' she stated. 'The whole month of July is the Vivica Fox month.' 

The talented star has also jumped behind the scenes and is an executive producer for the project. 

'I have to answer a lot of questions,' she explained about the additional job role. 'But I love it. I love the magic of making movies. How we just taking something from a piece of paper and turn it into a wonderful drama.' 

On the go: Vivica looked like a classy Hollywood star opting for an elegant summer dress for one of her interviews 

Amazing career: The actress has been working for many years in the industry, and continues to work hard, starring in the hit series, Black-ish

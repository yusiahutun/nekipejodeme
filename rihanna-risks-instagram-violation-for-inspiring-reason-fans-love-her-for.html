Rihanna risks Instagram violation for 'inspiring' reason fans love her for

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, May 16, 2024

Rihanna has released her new maternity line with her brand Savage x Fenty, and it seems like her son RZA approves as she can be seen breastfeeding him in one of her promo pics for the bras.

The collection features an array of garments, including bras and bralettes for “moms and moms-to-be” as the music star is expecting her second child with A$AP Rocky. Fans have taken to the comments to praise Rihanna on her line, and for the breastfeeding pic on the ‘gram.

Rihanna’s son RZA ‘approves’ of her maternity line

Taking to the Savage x Fenty Instagram, the Umbrella singer posted a range of photos of herself breastfeeding baby RZA in her new Savage x Fenty maternity bra.

In the caption, the star wrote: “Not ur mama’s maternity bras…designed by @badgalriri, approved by baby RZA #SavageXMaternity.”

Robyn can be seen smiling for the camera as she sports a chic long hairstyle, with bangs covering her eyes. Her lips are glossed up, no doubt with her Fenty lipgloss.

Although Instagram says it allows breastfeeding pics, many have been removed from the platform, as they say, “we occasionally make a mistake”. Nudity on the platform is banned.

Fans praise the star

Fans rushed to the comments of the post to praise the pregnant star for showcasing her breastfeeding and for the maternity inclusion.

“Pregnant with one while breastfeeding another. Women are amazing,” penned one fan.

“I remember in 2020 when I was breastfeeding I was like damn Riri needs to sell us fashionable maternity bras. Now I need another baby,” joked another.

“It’s like she’s even prettier pregnant,” one praised.

Another wrote: “It’s crazy what a woman’s body can do; breast feeding while creating another human at the same time is WILD!!!!”

Rihanna and A$AP Rocky’s relationship explored

One of the most adored celebrity couples are about to have their second baby, so we thought we’d take a look back to where it all began.

The couple made their relationship public back in 2021, after being friends for over a decade. Just before this, Rocky featured in Rihanna’s Fenty Skin campaign, and eagle-eyed fans, spotted their flirty chemistry in videos for Vogue and GQ from a mile off.

It was with an interview with GQ in 2021 where the Fashion Killa singer called Rihanna the “love of his life” and it’s all been uphill from there.

In January 2021, it was revealed that the Unfaithful singer was pregnant, with son RZA, who was born in May. Just nine months later, she announces she’s pregnant again with an epic Superbowl Half-Time performance reveal.

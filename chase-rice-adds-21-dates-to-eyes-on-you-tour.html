Chase Rice Adds 21 Dates to Eyes on You Tour

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, February 9, 2024

Chase Rice has extended his Eyes on You Tour, adding 21 new dates. The trek will have Rice criss-crossing the U.S. and Canada from February until May.

The second leg of Rice's tour starts Feb. 22 in St. Louis, Mo., borrowing its name from Rice's latest single, "Eyes on You."

"Having a song like 'Eyes on You' connect at this level has taken our live show to another level," Rice says. "Every night I have to take a minute and film them singing the words back to us on stage."

Rice released "Eyes on You," the second single off his Lambs & Lions album, last year, and it shows his more sensitive side. On the track, Rice sings of how the best part about being in any room is having his girl right there with him. He wrote the song with Nashville hitmakers Ashley Gorley and Chris DeStefano.

Beyonc Wears a Fabulous Yellow Suit to Louis Vuittons Show

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Beyoncé was front-row royalty at today’s Louis Vuitton runway show.

The singer traveled to Paris to get the first look at the fashion house’s menswear Spring/Summer 2024 collection. For the occasion, she wore a fabulous, silky golden-yellow suit (which looked like a luxurious pajama set) and huge, reflective golden shield sunglasses. She further accessorized in the most Beyoncé way—with a massive diamond choker necklace—and carried a black handbag with the brand’s signature logo print.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

The “Break My Soul” singer was joined in the front row by husband Jay-Z, Zendaya, and Rihanna. The rapper looked cool in a brown pixelated suit, white dress shirt, black dress shoes, and black round sunglasses.

The Euphoria star looked incredible in a shimmering, plunging top and tailored pants, both in a black-and-silver print. She finished the look with brown pointy pumps and silver jewelry. And Rihanna showed off her growing baby bump in a blue workwear-inspired denim jumpsuit with a pixelated pattern, plus a matching beanie. Underneath she wore only a bra—which she left exposed—and she accessorized with a black choker and big hoop earrings. Next to her, boyfriend A$AP Rocky wore a version of her look, which included a denim jacket, Bermuda shorts, and a patterned beanie. He also wore long white socks and fuzzy brown slippers.

Louis Vuitton’s show today marks Pharrell Williams’s first as creative director of menswear.

JULIEN DE ROSA//Getty Images

Rihanna and Rocky’s looks seemed to pay homage to friend Williams’s streetwear roots. The “Lift Me Up” singer also appeared earlier this month in Williams’s first campaign for the brand. In the photo, Rihanna is seen on a stylish coffee run, holding a rainbow stack of bags covered in the house’s signature logo motifs. She wears a multitoned gray and pixelated leather button-up, which is mostly left unbuttoned, exposing her baby bump underneath. She also wears black trousers, layers of necklaces, and a glittering earring cuff.

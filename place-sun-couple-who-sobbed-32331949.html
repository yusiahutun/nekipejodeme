A Place in the Sun couple who sobbed after buying dream home share sad update

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, October 3, 2024

A Place in the Sun buyers were left in tears — and then made a shock admission after cameras stopped rolling.

Caroline and Gary were searching for a three-bedroom property in Valencia, Spain on the Channel 4 show last night (March 11). The couple were armed with a budget of £150,000 and were looking for a spacious property for their family of nine children.

The property had four bedrooms, a bar and a pool and the couple were visibly emotional as they viewed the incredible home. They immediately made their decision to make an offer of £148,000 at the end of the show.

Much to their surprise, the offer was immediately accepted in what Jasmine Harman called the "quickest response they've ever had". The couple burst into tears of joy. Even the host was welling up from the news.

Before the credits started rolling, Jasmine offered the pair a "huge congratulations", adding: "This feels so right. It's just like fate. I'm so proud of Caroline and Gary for taking this opportunity to get their dream home in Spain."

In an interview with the show's official website after filming finished, Caroline was thankful for the help she received on the show. She said: "The crew was lovely and Jasmine too. We definitely wouldn't have ended up going to buy the place if we hadn't gone on the show."

The two had never visited the region before and shared their plans to renovate the outside of the house and paint around the pool. Caroline revealed how the home needed to be refurbished when she arrived at the house.

"We've had some family health matters to deal with. But I really hope that we can all gather together there as a family before too long there are no more trips planned as yet. ".

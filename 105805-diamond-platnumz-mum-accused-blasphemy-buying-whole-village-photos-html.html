Diamond Platnumz mum allegedly plans to buy a whole village

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

- Neighbours of top Tanzanian singer Diamond Platnumz's mother are allegedly appalled at the 'ridiculous' amount she has used to renovate an old home

- She is also believed to be intending to buy the surrounding houses, so as to end up owning the entire village

Sanura Kasimu ‘Sandra’, mother of top ranking Bongo Flava artiste Diamond Platnumz, has been accused of some kind of blasphemy, following claims of the ‘exorbitant’ amount spent to renovate her home, and that she intends to buy a whole village, local media report.

According to Tanzanian media outlet Global Publishers, residents have been left agape with rumours in the area indicating that the star’s mother has already spent TSh 100 million (KSh 4.7 million) in face-lifting of the family’s old home in Dar-es-Salaam’s Tandale area, which is in its final stages.

She is said to have been showing up for some hours of the day to oversee the construction, before heading back to her other plush house.

The family apparently also intends to buy other houses in the neighbourhood and end up owning the whole village, so as to allow expansion of the home, including adding a swimming pool.

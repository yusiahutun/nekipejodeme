Jennifer Lawrence Is Chic in a Gray Three-Piece Suit at Dior's Paris Show

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, April 3, 2024

Jennifer Lawrence perfected a classic look to support Dior at the French fashion house’s Fall/Winter 2024–2025 show in Paris today.

The actor looked Parisian-chic in a smoky gray three-piece tweed suit, which consisted of a plunging fitted vest, high-waisted trousers she folded at the bottom, and a sleek blazer. Over the suit, the No Hard Feelings star layered a cozy long black coat.

As the cherry on top of a delicious sundae of an outfit, Lawrence wore the suit pants over sheer black tights, which she paired with pointy black matte pumps.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

Her accessories for the head-to-toe Dior look celebrated the house’s recent releases; these included the brand’s new edition of the classic Lady Dior in black leather and gold hardware, as well as black pointy oval Butterfly sunglasses. For jewelry, she pulled out a pair of small white gold and diamond hoops.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

Lawrence, a Dior ambassador, is a regular presence at the brand’s runway shows and events. Last December, she also wore a timeless Dior look to celebrate the holidays with the house in New York City. To unveil Dior’s “Carousel of Dreams” at Saks Fifth Avenue in November, the actor wore a crisp white dress shirt layered under a long black collared coat that was buttoned and cinched at the waist with a thin black belt. Underneath, she wore a silky black pleated maxi skirt and black velvet heeled boots. Lawrence gave the look a preppy, feminine spin with a black headband, a quilted black Lady Dior bag, diamond stud earrings, and a gold necklace and bangle. She also wore pretty, rosy Dior Beauty makeup.

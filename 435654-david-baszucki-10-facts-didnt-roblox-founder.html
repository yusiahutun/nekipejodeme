Who is the real founder of Roblox?

David Baszucki and Erik Cassel are the Roblox owners and inventors. Baszucki and Cassel had worked together in the 1980s when he was his vice president of Engineering for Interactive Physics. The two worked on the Roblox prototype, which they first named DynaBlocks. In 2005, they settled on the word Roblox, a blend of robots and blocks, and launched it in 2006.

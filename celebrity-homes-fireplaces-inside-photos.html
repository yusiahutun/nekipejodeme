11 celebrity fireplaces you'll want to cosy up by: Goldie Hawn, Victoria Beckham, J Lo and more

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, May 27, 2024

From log burners to impressive marble features

    One small highlight of long winter months has to be the evenings spent by the fire – and it appears these celebrities also treasure those moments. From open log fires to chic glass features, the A-list know how to up the cosy factor in their jaw-dropping living rooms. Take a look at the amazing fireplaces of Ruth Langsford, Jennifer Lopez, Stacey Solomon and more…

    REVEALED: 30 of the most enviable celebrity bedrooms

    Geri Horner

    While having a Spice Girls reunion at one of her countryside homes, Geri Horner (née Halliwell) gave fans a look inside her living space. In-keeping with her rustic aesthetic, the open fire features wood and coals and above it, the singer has added ornaments and photographs to the mantle.

    Jennifer Lopez

    J Lo's incredible home in the Hamptons, which she shares with Alex Rodriguez and her twins, Emme and Max, is full of incredible features – however, her fireplace has to be one of our favourites. The stone surrounded open fire has a statement black front and wooden mantle. Adding to the ambience, the singer has placed a bouquet of flowers in front of her jaw-dropping fireplace.

    SEE: Jennifer Lopez gives full tour inside incredible $24 million New York penthouse

    Goldie Hawn

    Goldie Hawn and Kurt Russell's incredible Los Angeles mansion has beautiful features around every corner – but during an online video, Goldie showed off the couple's stunning fireplace. The star sat on a yellow armchair positioned in front of the chic white mantelpiece with marble interior. There is a television mounted on the wall above it, along with some houseplants to liven up the space.

    Catherine Zeta-Jones

    Catherine Zeta-Jones and Michael Douglas' New York mansion is every inch as incredible as you would expect. The actress took a selfie in what could easily be mistaken for a luxury spa, but is in fact her bathroom! The space includes a jaw-dropping marble fireplace, large arched mirror and a fur-covered chair.

    LOOK: Inside 31 of the most luxurious celebrity bathrooms

    Gok Wan

    Gok's followers and many celebrity friends adore the interiors of his London home – and his living room is often in the limelight on his Instagram. The bold blue walls create a warming atmosphere and the star's striking black fireplace really sets the scene. It looks like his dog, Dolly, is a fan too!

    SEE: Gok Wan's quirky home feature has fans obsessed

    Gregg Wallace

    At Christmas time, Masterchef star Gregg let fans see into his cosy family home. Their brickwork fireplace has a wood stove in the centre and logs piled either side. Ready for the festivities, the family had stockings and lights hanging from the mantlepiece.

    Jamie Oliver

    Celebrity chef Jamie lives in Essex with his wife Jools Oliver and their five children. Here, three of his children are posing by the family's open fire. The brickwork and stone step give the fire a rustic feel and they have accessoried the space with a large patterned rug.

    RELATED: Jamie Oliver reveals terrifying home feature

    Lisa Faulkner

    As beautifully ornate fireplaces go, Lisa Faulkner and John Torode's is something special. Their black open fire sits within a white marble surround which features stunning carved detailing. The couple have dressed the space with plenty of photographs, ornaments and a mantlepiece clock. The white mirror, which matches the fireplace, ensures their living space looks even larger than it is.

    GALLERY: John Torode and Lisa Faulkner's cosy home is everything we hoped for

    Ruth Langsford

    This Morning's Ruth Langsford and Eamonn Holmes have a stylish mansion in leafy Surrey. Shunning any traditional features, the presenting duo have gone for a very modern in-built glass fireplace that sits below their flatscreen television. We think the Kardashians would be proud!

    Stacey Solomon

    star Stacey is known for her DIY projects and she took to Instagram Stories to show off the wooden beam she and her fiancé Joe Swash have added above her fireplace to create a stunning shelf. The addition complements her faux log burner and fluffy rug, creating a country-cottage feel inside her Essex home.

    Victoria and David Beckham

    The Beckhams have an unbelievable home in the Cotswolds, complete with country-chic features and their rustic fireplace does not disappoint. As Victoria and David's son Brooklyn was pictured sipping wine, fans could see the stone fireplace with chevron-detail tiles and a simple black grate. There are also candlesticks beside the fireplace, ideal for when David and Victoria want a candle-lit date night.

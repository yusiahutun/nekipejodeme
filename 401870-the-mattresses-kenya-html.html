Top 10 best mattresses in Kenya in 2022: Prices and other details

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, March 28, 2024

Good sleep is essential for every human being. Without it, your brain and body cannot function properly because it affects memory, learning, decision-making, and even creativity. Therefore, it is important to invest in a quality mattress that supports your body as you sleep. Finding the best mattresses in Kenya can be challenging because there are numerous brands in the market.

Looking for the best mattresses in Kenya can be a daunting task because every manufacturer claims theirs is the best. Although marketing gimmicks can influence you to buy a particular brand, that alone is not enough. You need to pick one with the correct firmness, texture, longevity, and padding.

Best mattresses in Kenya in 2022 and their prices

Different people have varying needs when it comes to mattress selection. Some need the memory foam type, while others would rather have innerspring ones. Another option is orthopaedic mattresses in Kenya for people with underlying medical conditions. Read on to know the best mattresses in Kenya and their prices.

10. Simba Foam

  • Mattress sizes: All
  • Type of mattress: Foam

Simba Foam sleeping solutions come in great colours and in high, medium, and super heavy density. The company also manufactures car seats, lounge seats, and upholstery. The sleeping solutions provided are high-quality and comfortable.

9. Mekan EA

  • Mattress sizes: All
  • Type of mattress: Foam, spring coil, orthopaedic and Posturepedic

Mekan East Africa Limited uses high-quality materials imported from Turkey to manufacture its products. The company utilises the latest technological innovations to manufacture the products.

The products are ideal for people with back and shoulder pain. Health professionals usually recommend orthopaedic and posturepedic mattresses, and this company meets this need. It also produces spring coil products.
